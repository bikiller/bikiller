Gold (XAU/USD) remained steady on Wednesday as traders awaited the Federal Reserve's upcoming interest rate decision. The price action has been contained within a recent consolidation zone, with XAU/USD currently trading around $4,204. This marks a slight decline from the intraday high of $4,218, reflecting cautious sentiment in the market.

As the Fed's verdict looms, market participants are closely monitoring potential implications for the USD and broader currency pair dynamics. Any shifts in the Fed's stance could lead to increased volatility in gold prices, influencing trading strategies across commodities and affecting the exchange rate between gold and other currencies like the EUR.
