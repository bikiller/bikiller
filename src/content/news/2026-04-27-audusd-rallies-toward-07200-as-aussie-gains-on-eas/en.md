The Australian Dollar (AUD) gained 0.53% against the US Dollar (USD) on Monday, with the AUD/USD exchange rate rallying toward 0.7200. This surge came as market sentiment improved on easing concerns over the US-Iran conflict. Traders are closely watching the ongoing US review of Tehran's latest peace proposal, contributing to a shift in risk appetite. 

At the time of writing, AUD/USD was trading at 0.7185, after bouncing off a daily low of 0.7125. Despite the Aussie’s advance, the Greenback remained resilient, reflecting broader uncertainties in global markets. The currency pair’s movement indicates that geopolitical risks and macroeconomic events are shaping the forex landscape, with traders keeping a close eye on further developments.
