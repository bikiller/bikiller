Silver (XAG/USD) is experiencing a sharp decline, dropping to $87.90 during Monday's trading session. The precious metal has lost 6.33% on the day, reversing the early gains and highlighting significant volatility in the market. This downward movement comes as the US Dollar surges, exerting pressure on silver prices and pushing the currency pair lower.

The surge in USD strength is largely attributed to broader market dynamics, including positive economic data and rising interest rate expectations in the US. As the Dollar continues to strengthen, silver is facing headwinds, making it difficult for the XAG/USD exchange rate to sustain its earlier advances. Traders are now focusing on further developments in the USD's performance to gauge the next potential moves for silver.
