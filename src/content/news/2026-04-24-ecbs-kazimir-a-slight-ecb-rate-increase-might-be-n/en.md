European Central Bank (ECB) Governing Council member Peter Kazimir has indicated that a modest interest rate hike may be necessary. Speaking during European trading hours on Friday, Kazimir pointed to potential geopolitical risks, notably the ongoing conflict in Iran, which could dampen global economic growth and impact market sentiment. 

The EUR/USD exchange rate reacted to Kazimir's comments, reflecting traders' sensitivity to shifts in monetary policy. As the market digests these signals, investors are closely monitoring how the ECB will navigate inflation and growth concerns. With the potential for a rate increase looming, shifts in trading strategies are expected as participants adjust their positions in anticipation of future ECB decisions.
