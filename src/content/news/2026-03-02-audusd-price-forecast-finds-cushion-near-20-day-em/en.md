The AUD/USD currency pair is down 0.85% in the European session, trading around 0.7050, as a risk-off sentiment has gripped global markets. The Australian Dollar (AUD) took a hit following rising tensions from the US-Iran conflict, which has sparked concerns over global instability and dampened risk appetite.

Despite the sharp decline, the pair has found some support near the 20-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA), which is currently acting as a cushion for the exchange rate. Traders are closely monitoring geopolitical developments, with further escalation in the conflict potentially leading to more volatility in the AUD/USD pair.
