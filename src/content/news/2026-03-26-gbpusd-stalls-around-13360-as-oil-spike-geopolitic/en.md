GBP/USD is holding steady around the 1.3360 mark as Thursday's trading sees the currency pair consolidating. Geopolitical tensions in the Middle East have added pressure, with US President Donald Trump pushing Iran to reach a deal, creating uncertainty. This, coupled with a surge in oil prices, has led to increased support for the USD.

Meanwhile, stronger-than-expected US job data provided a solid foundation for the dollar, helping maintain the current trading range. The stability of the exchange rate highlights the ongoing influence of global political developments on the currency markets, with traders remaining cautious amid rising geopolitical risks. The focus now remains on any further US economic data or shifts in global tensions that could affect market sentiment.
