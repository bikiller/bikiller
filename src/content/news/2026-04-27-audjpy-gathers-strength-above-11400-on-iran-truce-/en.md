AUD/JPY strengthens to 114.15 in early European trading, buoyed by positive developments in global geopolitics. The currency pair gains momentum as reports surface of a proposal from Iran to the US, aiming to reopen the strategic Strait of Hormuz and bring an end to ongoing conflicts. This diplomatic move has lifted investor sentiment, encouraging risk-on behavior across the markets.

The Australian Dollar (AUD) benefits from the overall market optimism, with the AUD/JPY exchange rate rising above the key 114.00 level. Riskier assets such as the AUD have seen increased demand as traders price in the potential for reduced geopolitical tensions. This shift supports the currency pair’s upward movement, maintaining its strength in the face of broader market volatility.
