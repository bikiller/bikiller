GBP/USD surged above the 1.34 mark during the North American trading session on Monday, climbing 0.59% following the release of UK GDP data that matched forecasts. The positive economic performance provided support for the currency pair, allowing it to capitalize on buoyant market sentiment amid thin trading conditions as investors prepare for the Christmas Eve holiday.

As liquidity wanes, the dollar trades cautiously, and the exchange rate reflects a broader trend influenced by economic indicators. The recent uptick in GBP/USD underscores the volatility often seen in the forex market during festive periods, while traders remain attentive to potential fluctuations ahead of the year-end.
