The People's Bank of China (PBOC) has set the USD/CNY reference rate for Wednesday’s trading session at 6.8680, slightly lower than the previous day’s fix of 6.8854. The new rate also comes in above the Reuters estimate of 6.8369, signaling some strength in the yuan relative to the US dollar. 

This adjustment in the central parity rate reflects ongoing market fluctuations and the PBOC’s efforts to manage the exchange rate within a controlled range. Traders will be watching for any further shifts as the yuan continues to respond to both domestic economic data and global dollar movements.
