Gold (XAU/USD) continues its downward momentum in Monday’s European session, trading just below $4,550. Bears are pushing towards last week’s lows, with targets now set around the $4,500 mark. This marks a notable acceleration in the recent reversal, as the precious metal struggles to maintain support at higher levels.

The bearish sentiment in the market is driven by a stronger USD, with investors reacting to rising expectations of tighter monetary policies. This, combined with a broad risk-off mood, has placed pressure on gold prices, pushing them lower. Traders are closely monitoring the $4,500 area as a key technical level, with further declines potentially testing lower supports.
