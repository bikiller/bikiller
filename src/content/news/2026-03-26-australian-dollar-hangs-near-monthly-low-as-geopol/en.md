The Australian Dollar (AUD) remains under pressure, trading near its monthly low against the US Dollar (USD) as geopolitical tensions and rising Fed interest rate expectations continue to support the greenback. The AUD/USD currency pair is struggling to gain momentum, lingering just above its weekly low set earlier today. Market participants are cautious, keeping the exchange rate within a narrow range as they await further developments.

Geopolitical risks have added uncertainty to global markets, with traders seeking safer assets like the USD. Additionally, Fed bets are leaning towards more aggressive tightening, which has kept the USD in a dominant position. As the AUD/USD pair hovers around key technical levels, the 0.6630 support remains a critical point for traders to watch, while the pair's ability to break higher depends on shifts in market sentiment and economic data in the coming days.
