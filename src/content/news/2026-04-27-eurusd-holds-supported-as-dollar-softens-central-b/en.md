EUR/USD is holding steady around 1.1740 on Monday, marking a 0.21% gain for the day. The currency pair extended its rebound from Friday’s low near 1.1670, bolstered by a softening USD. Despite challenges in the Eurozone's macroeconomic outlook, the Euro remains supported as investors await key central bank decisions.

The US Dollar’s recent weakness has allowed EUR/USD to climb, even with economic uncertainty lingering in the Eurozone. Market attention is shifting towards upcoming central bank meetings, with traders positioning ahead of potential policy shifts. The exchange rate continues to reflect this delicate balance between USD softness and Eurozone risks.
