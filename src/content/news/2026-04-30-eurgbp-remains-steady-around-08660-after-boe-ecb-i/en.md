EUR/GBP remains steady around the 0.8660 level on Thursday, showing only slight losses against the British Pound. The currency pair has been fluctuating within this narrow range over the past few days, with no strong momentum in either direction. Market participants are largely in a wait-and-see mode ahead of upcoming central bank meetings.

With the Bank of England (BoE) having already made its move, traders are now looking to the European Central Bank (ECB) for further direction. The euro continues to trade within familiar ranges, with the lack of significant changes in sentiment leaving the exchange rate muted. The market's next key focus will be the ECB's policy stance and any potential moves in the wider forex market.
