Silver (XAG/USD) is trading at $76.00 on Friday, showing a notable 3.05% increase for the day. The precious metal continues to attract buyers despite a challenging macroeconomic environment, marked by the Federal Reserve's hawkish stance on interest rates and ongoing inflation concerns. These factors typically weigh on non-yielding assets like silver, making its recent rally even more striking.

The currency pair's rise comes amid persistent inflation risks, which continue to influence market sentiment. Traders are adjusting their positions as silver’s appeal as a hedge against inflation gains momentum. While the Fed's restrictive policy puts pressure on precious metals, silver's resilience underscores its ongoing role in portfolios seeking protection amid uncertain economic conditions.
