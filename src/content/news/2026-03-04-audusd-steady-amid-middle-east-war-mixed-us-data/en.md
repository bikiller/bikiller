The AUD/USD currency pair remained largely unchanged on Wednesday, trading near the 0.7040 mark as investors weighed the ongoing tensions in the Middle East and mixed economic data from the United States. With little directional movement, the exchange rate hovered around this technical level, reflecting market indecision.

Traders are closely monitoring global developments, with the Middle East conflict influencing risk sentiment. Meanwhile, US economic indicators presented a mixed picture, adding further uncertainty to the US dollar's strength. The subdued action in AUD/USD reflects a balancing act between these external factors, keeping the pair within a tight range.
