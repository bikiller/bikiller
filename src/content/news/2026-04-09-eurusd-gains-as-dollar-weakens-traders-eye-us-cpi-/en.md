The EUR/USD currency pair continued its upward momentum on Thursday, reaching 1.1676 as the US Dollar (USD) weakened. The Greenback remains under pressure following the US-Iran ceasefire and ongoing de-escalation talks, which have helped lift sentiment towards the Euro (EUR). This marks the fourth consecutive day of gains for the Euro, signaling positive momentum in the market.

Traders are now turning their focus to upcoming US data, with the Consumer Price Index (CPI) report and US-Iran negotiations likely to influence the next moves in the EUR/USD exchange rate. As investors await these crucial events, the pair is likely to see increased volatility, with any surprises in the data potentially shifting the market dynamics in favor of either currency.
