The Australian Dollar (AUD) remains largely unchanged against the US Dollar (USD) on Friday, despite positive market sentiment that pushed the Greenback to a four-week low near 98.52 on the US Dollar Index (DXY). The AUD/USD exchange rate faced resistance around the 0.7100 mark, where selling pressure caused a pullback. 

Despite the rejection at 0.7100, the outlook for the Aussie remains constructive, with upside risks still intact. The broader market optimism has softened the USD's strength, but the pair may continue to face technical hurdles. Traders will be eyeing any further developments in global risk sentiment and potential shifts in the USD for clues on the next move in AUD/USD.
