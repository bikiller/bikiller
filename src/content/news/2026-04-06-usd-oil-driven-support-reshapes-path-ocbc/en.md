The USD is showing resilience as rising oil prices and safe-haven flows have bolstered the currency, overturning previous expectations of a steady decline. Strategists at OCBC, Sim Moh Siong and Christopher Wong, noted that this shift is reshaping the path for the greenback. The USD has gained traction as oil's upward momentum strengthens investor confidence, leading to increased demand for the dollar.

The EUR/USD exchange rate has seen notable shifts as the USD benefits from these market forces. With oil prices moving higher, the dollar has reversed the downtrend, prompting traders to reassess their bearish outlook. Key technical levels are now under scrutiny as the market anticipates further moves in the currency pair. As the oil-driven rally continues, USD momentum remains a critical factor for traders in the weeks ahead.
