USD/JPY remained steady around 159.50 on Tuesday, edging up by 0.07% after briefly dipping below the 159.00 mark following the Bank of Japan's (BoJ) latest policy shift. The BoJ's decision, which suggested a more hawkish stance, initially pressured the yen, but it quickly regained ground as broader market factors came into play.

The ongoing tensions in the Middle East have added a layer of support to the U.S. Dollar, with global risk sentiment driving demand for safe-haven assets like the USD. As geopolitical uncertainties persist, the USD/JPY exchange rate is likely to see continued volatility, with traders closely monitoring both regional developments and upcoming central bank announcements.
