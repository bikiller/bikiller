The Australian Dollar showed little reaction to the softer-than-expected CPI data, with the AUD/USD currency pair consolidating around the 0.7000 mark during the Asian session on Wednesday. Despite the data indicating weaker inflationary pressures, the Australian Dollar struggled to make significant moves, leaving the exchange rate trapped in a narrow range.

With the AUD/USD pair unable to gain momentum, the market remains focused on the broader trends influencing the currency. Traders continue to monitor global economic data, particularly from the U.S., as the USD’s strength persists. The exchange rate's stability around the 0.7000 level suggests a cautious market awaiting further catalysts to spark volatility.
