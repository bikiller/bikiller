GBP/USD opened the week on a weaker note, briefly dipping to a low of 1.3380 during early trading. However, the currency pair quickly reversed course, gaining strength throughout the session. By the close, GBP/USD had rallied to 1.3510, marking a 0.35% daily increase. This marked the highest level for the pair in seven weeks.

The resurgence in GBP/USD came as the US Dollar softened, with the greenback losing ground against major currencies. As traders adjusted positions, the pound gained momentum, pushing the exchange rate above key resistance levels. The rally highlights ongoing volatility in USD-driven markets and signals potential for further movement in the coming sessions.
