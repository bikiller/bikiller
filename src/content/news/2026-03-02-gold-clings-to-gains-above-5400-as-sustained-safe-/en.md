Gold (XAU/USD) continues to hold steady above the $5,400 mark, maintaining strong intraday gains through the early European session. This price level marks the highest for the precious metal since late January, as safe-haven demand remains robust despite the dollar's strength. The sustained buying interest comes amid ongoing global uncertainties, keeping gold's appeal intact.

The U.S. dollar's strength has had limited impact on gold's upward momentum, as investors continue to flock to the metal as a hedge against market volatility. As the EUR/USD exchange rate shows moderate movement, the gold market remains supported by geopolitical concerns and inflationary fears, with traders focusing on potential economic risks. Gold’s resilience against the stronger dollar signals ongoing investor confidence in its safe-haven status.
