AUD/USD is trading around 0.7090 on Tuesday, up 0.25% for the day. The currency pair recovered after an initial dip following the Reserve Bank of Australia's (RBA) split decision on its monetary policy. The RBA’s hawkish tone, signaling a cautious stance on future rate hikes, helped the Australian dollar rebound against the US dollar.

The exchange rate’s movement reflects market sentiment as traders digest the RBA's cautious approach, which contrasts with recent expectations of a more aggressive tightening cycle. While the pullback in AUD/USD was short-lived, the overall outlook remains sensitive to global risk sentiment and the USD’s strength, with traders looking ahead to upcoming economic data for further direction.
