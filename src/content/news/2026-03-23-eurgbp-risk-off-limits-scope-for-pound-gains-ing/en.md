EUR/GBP has been trading slightly above its short-term fair value estimate, as highlighted by ING's Francesco Pesole. The current exchange rate reflects a subdued outlook for the Pound, with risk-off sentiment in the broader market limiting its potential for gains. This cautious market mood, driven by global uncertainties, keeps the currency pair constrained within a modest range.

While the EUR/GBP exchange rate remains above fair value, the broader market environment, particularly the USD strength, plays a significant role in curbing any upward momentum for the Pound. With limited catalysts for a breakout, the pair’s movement is likely to remain muted in the near term, as traders monitor risk sentiment and broader economic data.
