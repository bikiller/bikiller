ICE Brent crude oil prices rose nearly 1.2% last week, buoyed by a surge on Friday following a Ukrainian attack on the Russian port of Novorossiysk. As traders monitor the ongoing developments, fluctuations in oil prices could impact currency pairs such as USD/EUR and USD/RUB, as changes in energy prices often correlate with shifts in exchange rates. Investors are keenly observing these movements, as they can signal broader market trends and affect economic forecasts for oil-dependent economies.
