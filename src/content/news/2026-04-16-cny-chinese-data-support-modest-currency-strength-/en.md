Chinese economic data has provided some support for the yuan, with GDP and industrial production outperforming expectations. Both indicators showed better-than-expected growth, bolstering the outlook for the economy. However, retail sales remained weak, and the unemployment rate edged higher, highlighting ongoing challenges. Despite these mixed signals, the CNY found some stability in the forex market.

In the currency markets, the USD/CNY exchange rate saw modest fluctuations, reflecting the balance of positive growth and persistent domestic issues. Meanwhile, EUR/CNY showed limited movement, as traders weighed the economic landscape in China against broader global risks. With the mixed data, market participants remain cautious but continue to monitor China’s economic recovery.
