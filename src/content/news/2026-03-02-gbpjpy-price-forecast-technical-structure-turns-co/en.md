GBP/JPY surged on Monday after opening with a bearish gap-down, driven by heightened volatility across global markets. The currency pair quickly rebounded, trading above the critical 210.00 mark, a level that has now turned technically constructive. The market sentiment was heavily influenced by the joint US-Israeli strikes on Iran over the weekend, which added uncertainty to the broader FX landscape.

The rebound in GBP/JPY comes amid broader risk aversion in FX trading, with the US Dollar and Euro also seeing fluctuations. The strikes have contributed to shifts in investor sentiment, pushing volatility across multiple pairs. With GBP/JPY above 210.00, traders are watching for further momentum, while key levels remain in focus for potential resistance and support as geopolitical tensions continue to weigh on the markets.
