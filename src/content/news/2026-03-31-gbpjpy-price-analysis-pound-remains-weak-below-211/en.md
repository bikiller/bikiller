The GBP/JPY currency pair remains under pressure, struggling to sustain a recovery after Monday's sharp reversal. Despite an attempt to rebound, the Pound is trading weakly below the 211.00 level, facing resistance from a previous support zone. The exchange rate found a floor around 210.00, but upward momentum continues to be capped, reflecting broader market hesitancy.

This stagnant price action reflects the broader market sentiment, with the Pound grappling with ongoing economic uncertainties. The Japanese Yen, on the other hand, remains relatively stable, bolstered by safe-haven demand amid global market fluctuations. As the pair stays below 211.00, traders are closely monitoring potential breakouts, with 210.00 serving as a key support level. Continued weakness in the Pound could signal further downside in the near term.
