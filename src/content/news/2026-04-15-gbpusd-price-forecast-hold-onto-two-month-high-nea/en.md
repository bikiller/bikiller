The GBP/USD currency pair remained steady at 1.3565 during the European trading session on Wednesday, maintaining its position near the two-month high of 1.3590 reached the previous day. Traders are monitoring global risk appetite, with positive market sentiment favoring higher-yielding assets like the GBP. The pair's ability to hold near recent highs suggests potential for further gains if risk sentiment continues to drive market activity.
