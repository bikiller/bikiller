EUR/USD is trading higher on Monday, with the Euro (EUR) making gains against the US Dollar (USD) amid a slight recovery in market sentiment. The currency pair is benefiting from the news of a potential ceasefire in the Iran war, which has supported risk appetite. However, despite this positive news, EUR/USD has struggled to break the key resistance level at 1.1570, unable to sustain a move above it.

The US Dollar's weakness continues to play a significant role in the currency pair's movement. Despite the broader downturn in the USD, the Euro faces technical challenges, with 1.1570 capping upside momentum. Traders remain cautious, awaiting further developments on the geopolitical front, which could drive the next major shift in the EUR/USD exchange rate.
