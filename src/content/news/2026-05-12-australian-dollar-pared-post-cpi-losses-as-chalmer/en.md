The Australian Dollar (AUD) showed resilience on Tuesday, managing to recover its post-CPI losses as the budget announcement by Treasurer Jim Chalmers addressed concerns stemming from the recent oil shock. The AUD/USD currency pair fluctuated within a choppy intraday range but ultimately closed the session nearly unchanged, reflecting the market's cautious sentiment.

The exchange rate for AUD/USD demonstrated volatility but found stability in the face of economic developments. Traders are closely monitoring the implications of Chalmers' budget on future monetary policy, as it may influence the Reserve Bank of Australia's approach to interest rates amid fluctuating global oil prices. As the market digests these factors, the AUD's performance will be pivotal in determining the broader landscape for currency trading against the USD and other major pairs.
