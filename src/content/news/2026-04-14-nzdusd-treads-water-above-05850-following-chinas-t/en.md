NZD/USD remains steady above 0.5850 as it trades around 0.5860 during the Asian session on Tuesday. After gaining 0.5% the previous day, the currency pair shows limited movement. The release of China’s March Trade Balance data has contributed to the subdued market sentiment, leaving the pair without significant volatility.

Despite the slight pullback, NZD/USD holds above the 0.5850 technical support level. Traders are closely monitoring broader market trends and the USD's performance, as the data from China adds uncertainty to the forex market. The pair's ability to stay above key levels will likely determine its next direction in the coming sessions.
