The USD/JPY exchange rate has seen significant downward pressure, falling sharply from near the 160.00 level to around 158.70. This decline suggests a continued bearish momentum, with further downside potential towards the key support level of 158.00. The move marks a notable shift in market sentiment as traders adjust their positions amid a fluctuating global risk environment.

Despite this downward trend, analysts at UOB, Quek Ser Leang and Lee Sue Ann, do not foresee a sustained break below the 158.00 threshold at this stage. While the currency pair is facing pressure, the technical outlook indicates that a solid move past 158.00 would require further market shifts. For now, the focus remains on whether USD/JPY can stabilize or if more declines are in the cards.
