Gold (XAU/USD) kicked off the week on a bullish note, surging to new heights as safe-haven demand intensified amid escalating geopolitical tensions. The price of gold is currently hovering near record highs, driven by investor concerns over President Trump's tariff threats, which have sparked a risk-off sentiment in the markets.

As traders flock to gold for protection, the precious metal's strong performance is reflected in its exchange rate movements. The ongoing uncertainty in global trade dynamics is likely to keep pushing gold higher, influencing trading strategies across the forex markets. This heightened interest in gold could also have implications for the USD and EUR currency pairs, as traders reassess their portfolios in light of increasing volatility.
