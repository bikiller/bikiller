USD/JPY is trading at 158.70, up 0.16% on the day, supported by the continued strength of the US Dollar. Geopolitical tensions and economic uncertainty have contributed to a risk-averse market environment, driving demand for safe-haven assets like the USD. The currency pair has been gaining momentum as global markets navigate ongoing volatility.

The US Dollar’s bullish bias remains intact, with investors flocking to it amid uncertain economic conditions. However, the release of weak PMI data has weighed on sentiment, limiting any major gains for USD/JPY. Despite this, the exchange rate continues to hold steady, suggesting that the USD's demand remains robust in the face of broader market challenges.
