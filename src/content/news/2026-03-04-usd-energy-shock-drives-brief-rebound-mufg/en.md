The USD saw a strong rebound as fresh energy price shocks, triggered by Operation “Epic Fury,” pushed the Dollar index back toward the 96.000–100.00 range. The surge in energy costs led to a brief recovery in the greenback, which had been under pressure in recent weeks. This sudden market movement sparked heightened trading volatility across major currency pairs, including EUR/USD and GBP/USD.

The energy shock has reignited concerns over inflationary pressures and its potential impact on central bank policies. As a result, the USD strengthened against a basket of currencies, with EUR/USD dropping below key support levels. Traders are closely monitoring further developments in the energy market, as any sustained rally could keep the Dollar elevated in the near term, especially if global inflation concerns continue to escalate.
