Silver (XAG/USD) is facing downward pressure on Tuesday, dropping more than 2.5% as the higher-for-longer interest rate outlook continues to weigh heavily on the precious metal. The currency pair remains below key simple moving averages (SMAs), signaling sustained bearish momentum in the market. Traders are cautious as the ongoing strength of the USD continues to dampen demand for silver.

The overall trend suggests a continued struggle for silver, with the XAG/USD exchange rate unable to break free from its bearish trajectory. Market participants remain focused on the Federal Reserve's stance on interest rates, with expectations for prolonged tightening adding to downward pressure. As a result, silver’s appeal as a hedge against inflation diminishes, further contributing to its weakened performance against the stronger USD.
