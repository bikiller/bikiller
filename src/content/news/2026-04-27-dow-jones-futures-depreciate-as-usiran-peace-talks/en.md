Dow Jones futures declined by 0.16%, trading around 49,300 during European hours on Monday, as market sentiment soured following a halt in US-Iran peace talks. This pause in diplomatic progress has left investors on edge, impacting risk sentiment globally. S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 futures also edged lower, dropping 0.10% and 0.06%, respectively, to around 7,190 and 27,420.

The stall in negotiations between the US and Iran raised concerns over geopolitical stability, which dampened investor confidence. In forex markets, the USD struggled to find direction, with major pairs like EUR/USD holding steady. Meanwhile, the broader risk-off sentiment pressured commodities, and markets continued to watch for fresh catalysts. Investors remain cautious as they await further developments in both international relations and domestic economic indicators.
