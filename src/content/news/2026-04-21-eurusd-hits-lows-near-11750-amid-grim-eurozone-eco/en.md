The Euro (EUR) continues its downward trajectory against the US Dollar (USD), dipping to session lows near 1.1750 on Tuesday. This follows the failure of the EUR/USD pair to maintain Monday’s gains, which had peaked just above 1.1790. The weakness comes amid disappointing economic sentiment data from the Eurozone, fueling concerns over the region’s economic outlook.

The currency pair’s inability to push past 1.1790 highlights a lack of bullish momentum, as traders react to the grim sentiment figures. With the EUR under pressure, the focus now shifts to key technical levels, with 1.1750 acting as a crucial support zone. A break below this level could signal further losses for the Euro, while resistance remains firmly in place near the 1.1790 mark.
