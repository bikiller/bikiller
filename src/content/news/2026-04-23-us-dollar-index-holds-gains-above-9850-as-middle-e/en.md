The US Dollar Index (DXY) is maintaining upward momentum, holding gains above 98.50 for the third consecutive day. As of Thursday's Asian session, the index is trading around 98.70, driven by ongoing uncertainty in the Middle East. The dollar's strength continues to be supported by a risk-off market sentiment, as investors seek safe-haven assets.

This surge in the USD is impacting major currency pairs, including EUR/USD, which has been pressured lower. The DXY’s move past the 98.50 level reinforces its resilience amid geopolitical tensions. Traders are closely monitoring developments, as further volatility in the Middle East could provide continued support for the US Dollar, influencing exchange rates across the board.
