The GBP/USD currency pair is trading around 1.3310, recovering nearly all of Friday’s losses and breaking a four-day losing streak. This rebound comes as market participants digest the ongoing escalation in the US-Israel conflict with Iran, which had been a major focal point for global markets over the weekend. The exchange rate’s recovery indicates a shift in sentiment, with investors moving past the geopolitical tensions.

As the market stabilizes, traders are closely monitoring any further developments in the Middle East, which could influence the broader forex market. The current technical level of 1.3310 is crucial for the pair’s short-term outlook. A sustained break above this level could signal further bullish momentum for GBP/USD, while a pullback may reignite concerns of a continued downtrend for the pair in the coming days.
