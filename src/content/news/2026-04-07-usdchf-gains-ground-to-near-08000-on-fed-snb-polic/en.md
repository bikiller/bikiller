The USD/CHF currency pair strengthened during early European trading on Tuesday, nearing the 0.7990 mark. The Greenback gained momentum against the Swiss Franc as market participants reacted to the growing divergence in monetary policy between the US Federal Reserve (Fed) and the Swiss National Bank (SNB). While the Fed remains on a tightening path, the SNB continues to maintain a more dovish stance, weighing on the Franc.

This policy divergence is driving the recent movement in the USD/CHF exchange rate, as traders anticipate further Fed rate hikes. As the Fed stays committed to combating inflation, the USD is benefiting from expectations of higher returns on US assets, prompting increased demand for the dollar. Meanwhile, the Swiss Franc faces downward pressure, with the SNB's cautious approach contributing to the widening gap between the two central banks' policies.
