Silver (XAG/USD) has seen a notable decline, dipping below the $75.00 level amid increasing risk aversion in the financial markets. As investors seek safe-haven assets in light of market volatility, the demand for silver has waned. The continued pressure on XAG/USD suggests that traders are adjusting their positions, potentially anticipating further declines.
