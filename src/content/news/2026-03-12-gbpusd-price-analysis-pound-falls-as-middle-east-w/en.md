The GBP/USD currency pair is under pressure, trading near the 1.3350 mark, marking its third consecutive day of losses. The pound has struggled amid escalating tensions in the Middle East, which have contributed to broader market instability. The ongoing conflict has spurred risk aversion, driving investors toward safe-haven assets like the USD, further weighing on the British currency.

As geopolitical concerns mount, the exchange rate between the British pound and the US dollar has faced downward momentum. This continued decline highlights investor uncertainty and market caution, as the potential for further volatility in global markets remains high. Traders are closely monitoring developments, with the 1.3300 level acting as a key technical support. A break below could signal further losses for GBP/USD.
