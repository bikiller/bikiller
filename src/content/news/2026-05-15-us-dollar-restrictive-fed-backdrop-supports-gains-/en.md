The US Dollar has extended its gains as a restrictive Federal Reserve backdrop continues to influence trading dynamics. Strong oil prices have propelled bond yields higher, putting pressure on equities and supporting the USD across key currency pairs. Traders are keeping an eye on the USD/EUR exchange rate, which has been edging upward as market participants weigh the implications of tighter monetary policy.

Market sentiment also reflects cautious optimism following the recent US-China summit, which yielded only marginal diplomatic progress. This limited outcome has not alleviated risk aversion, prompting investors to favor the USD over riskier currencies. The combination of firm commodity prices and resilient Treasury yields continues to underpin dollar strength in trading sessions, with technical levels showing upward momentum.

For forex traders, the current environment suggests the USD could maintain its advantage against major counterparts, including the EUR and JPY. Volatility is likely to persist as market participants digest geopolitical developments and economic data. Monitoring key support and resistance levels in currency pairs will be crucial for navigating potential swings, with the interplay between equity markets, oil prices, and central bank guidance driving the direction of exchange rates.
