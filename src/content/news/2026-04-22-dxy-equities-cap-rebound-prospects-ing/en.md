The US Dollar (USD) remained largely unchanged following Kevin Warsh’s testimony at a Senate hearing, where he defended the Federal Reserve's independence but refrained from offering specific policy guidance. Despite some anticipation, the event had minimal impact on market sentiment, leaving the DXY index flat. The exchange rate for USD/EUR hovered near 1.09, with no clear directional move observed.

Market participants continue to watch the broader economic data for guidance, as the USD remains pressured by equity market dynamics. ING strategists highlight that the rebound potential for the USD is limited, with equities capping the upside. The DXY’s lack of significant movement underscores this ongoing balance between risk sentiment and monetary policy uncertainty. Traders are likely to focus on upcoming data to determine the next major moves in the currency markets.
