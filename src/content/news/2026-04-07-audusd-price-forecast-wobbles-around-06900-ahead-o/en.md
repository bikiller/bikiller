The AUD/USD currency pair is trading within a narrow range around 0.6900 during early European trading on Tuesday. Market participants are awaiting developments following President Trump's ultimatum to Iran, which has created uncertainty in global markets. This geopolitical tension is keeping the Australian Dollar subdued against the US Dollar, as traders assess potential risks in the broader financial landscape.

The exchange rate for AUD/USD remains volatile with technical support near 0.6880 and resistance at 0.6920. As the situation in Iran continues to evolve, the Australian Dollar faces pressure, particularly with concerns over any immediate retaliation. A sharp movement in the currency pair could materialize depending on Iran’s response, while traders closely monitor any updates from global leaders for clearer guidance on the outlook for the USD and other major currencies.
