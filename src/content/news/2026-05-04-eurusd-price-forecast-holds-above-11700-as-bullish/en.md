The EUR/USD currency pair sees some intraday selling pressure after a brief uptick during the Asian session, which saw the exchange rate climb to the mid-1.1700s. The pair has since filled a significant portion of the bullish gap formed at the start of the week. Despite this pullback, the broader bullish trend remains intact as the euro holds above the critical 1.1700 level.

Market sentiment remains optimistic for the euro, with potential for further gains if the pair can maintain support above 1.1700. The USD has shown signs of weakness amid ongoing concerns over U.S. economic growth and monetary policy expectations. As traders continue to monitor global economic data and central bank signals, EUR/USD's technical levels will be key in determining future market direction.
