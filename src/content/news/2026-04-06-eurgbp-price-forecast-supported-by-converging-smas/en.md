EUR/GBP remains under pressure on Monday, with a negative bias prevailing as diplomatic developments between the US and Iran fuel positive market sentiment. This has benefited risk-sensitive currencies, while the British Pound (GBP) outperforms the Euro (EUR). The exchange rate for the currency pair hovers below 0.8750, reflecting the shift in market dynamics.

The pair finds support near converging short-term moving averages (SMAs), yet the upside is capped as GBP gains traction amid broader risk appetite. With EUR/GBP unable to breach 0.8750, traders are monitoring for any changes in risk sentiment, which could shift the balance in favor of EUR. Until then, the pair faces a range-bound scenario.
