Gold prices fell sharply on Thursday, dropping nearly 2.7% amid a lack of clear catalysts. The precious metal, which has been sensitive to US economic data, saw a decline despite solid jobs numbers in the US. Although the latest figures showed steady job growth, the number of Americans filing for unemployment benefits exceeded expectations, adding uncertainty to the market.

The unexpected rise in claims, coupled with falling US yields, failed to provide the support gold bulls had hoped for. This market reaction highlights the cautious trading environment in which gold is struggling to gain momentum. The gold price movement is impacting currency pairs like EUR/USD and USD/JPY, with the USD remaining a central factor as it continues to dictate the flow of capital in global markets.
