The US ISM Manufacturing PMI for February will be released today at 15:00 GMT, a key indicator for the health of the manufacturing sector. Traders are closely monitoring this data as a strong reading could push the USD higher, while a weaker result may benefit the EUR. The current EUR/USD exchange rate is hovering around 1.0600, with support seen near 1.0500 and resistance at 1.0700.

The PMI report can influence market sentiment and volatility, particularly for the EUR/USD currency pair. A stronger-than-expected PMI reading would reinforce the USD’s bullish momentum, potentially driving the exchange rate lower. Conversely, a disappointing PMI result could trigger a reversal, causing EUR/USD to test the key resistance levels and shift market dynamics toward euro strength.
