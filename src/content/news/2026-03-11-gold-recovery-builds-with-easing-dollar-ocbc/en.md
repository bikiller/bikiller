Gold prices have continued their upward momentum as the Dollar weakens and oil prices pull back. The easing of risk sentiment, driven by signs that the Iran conflict may be nearing a resolution, has provided support to the precious metal. As a result, the gold price has gained steadily, benefiting from a retreat in the USD, which has seen its strength diminish against other major currencies like the EUR.

This recovery is also reflected in the broader market, with trading in USD/EUR showing signs of stabilization. The decline in oil prices has alleviated some inflationary pressures, further supporting gold’s appeal as a safe haven. As the market digests these geopolitical shifts, gold’s technical outlook remains positive, with the precious metal showing resilience and potential for further gains if the US Dollar remains under pressure.
