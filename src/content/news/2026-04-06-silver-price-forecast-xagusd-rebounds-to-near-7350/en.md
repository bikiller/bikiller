Silver (XAG/USD) has staged a strong rebound, climbing to approximately $73.30 per troy ounce in European trading on Monday, recovering from earlier losses. The rally comes as positive market sentiment surrounds the potential for a ceasefire in the Middle East, easing concerns about global uncertainty. As a result, silver has gained a foothold, edging closer to the key $73.50 resistance level.

The price action reflects broader market optimism, which is impacting precious metals. Investors are watching for further developments in geopolitical tensions that could shift trading dynamics. With the USD remaining in focus, any movements in the dollar could provide additional volatility for silver prices, making the $73.50 level an important technical threshold for traders to monitor in the coming sessions.
