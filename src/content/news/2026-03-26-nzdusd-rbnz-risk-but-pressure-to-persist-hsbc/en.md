HSBC Global Research predicts continued pressure on the New Zealand Dollar (NZD) against the US Dollar (USD) in the coming weeks. Despite expectations of further tightening by the Reserve Bank of New Zealand (RBNZ) over the next 12 months, the NZD/USD currency pair is likely to face headwinds due to ongoing global market uncertainty and stronger USD dynamics.

With the USD maintaining its dominant position, traders are cautious of the NZD’s ability to gain significant traction. The exchange rate is expected to remain volatile, and while RBNZ's tightening stance may provide some support, the broader USD strength could overshadow any potential upside for the Kiwi. As a result, the NZD/USD pair remains vulnerable to further downside movement in the near term.
