Gold (XAU/USD) continues to exhibit a cautious tone as traders adopt a wait-and-see approach ahead of the crucial US Nonfarm Payrolls (NFP) report. Currently, the precious metal is trading with a mild negative bias, reflecting uncertainty in the market as participants assess potential clues regarding future Federal Reserve rate cuts.

The NFP report is expected to have significant implications for the USD and may influence trading strategies across various currency pairs. Traders are closely monitoring this data, as a stronger-than-expected payroll figure could bolster the dollar, while weaker results might reignite speculation about a dovish shift from the Fed. As the session unfolds, gold's performance will be closely tied to the outcomes of this key economic indicator.
