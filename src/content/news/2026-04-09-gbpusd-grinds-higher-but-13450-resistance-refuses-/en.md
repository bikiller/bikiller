GBP/USD saw modest gains on Thursday, rising by 0.31% to reach the mid-1.3400s. The US Dollar remained under pressure, largely due to the ongoing US-Iran ceasefire, which continued to impact broader market sentiment. Despite the upward movement, the currency pair struggled to break through the key resistance level at 1.3450, signaling that momentum is waning.

The US Dollar's weakness has been a key factor in supporting the British Pound in recent sessions, though the 1.3450 resistance remains a significant hurdle. Traders are now watching closely for any developments that might push GBP/USD above this level, as failure to do so could signal a potential reversal. The current outlook suggests that the pair may continue to face a choppy trading environment as both technical and geopolitical factors weigh on the market.
