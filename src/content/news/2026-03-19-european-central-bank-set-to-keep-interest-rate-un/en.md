The European Central Bank is widely expected to leave interest rates unchanged following its two-day policy meeting, as policymakers weigh persistent inflation against rising geopolitical risks tied to the Middle East conflict. The cautious stance has kept the EUR under pressure, with the EUR/USD currency pair hovering in a narrow trading range as investors avoid aggressive positioning ahead of the announcement.

Market participants remain focused on how uncertainty is shaping the eurozone outlook, particularly through energy prices and trade flows that directly impact the EUR exchange rate. Safe-haven demand for the USD has strengthened slightly, limiting upside in the euro and reinforcing defensive trading strategies across major currency pairs.

Traders will closely parse the ECB’s forward guidance for clues on future rate adjustments, as any shift in tone could trigger volatility in EUR crosses. A dovish signal may push EUR/USD lower, while a more hawkish stance could support the euro, influencing short-term trading sentiment and broader forex market direction.
