The AUD/JPY currency pair is trading higher around 111.70 in early European trading on Monday, extending gains above the 111.50 technical level. The pair’s upside momentum is supported by bullish chart patterns and positive economic data from China, which have strengthened risk appetite among traders. Investors are closely watching how these factors influence broader AUD and JPY movements against major currencies like the USD and EUR.  

Geopolitical tensions, particularly the ongoing US-Israel conflict with Iran, are adding a layer of complexity to forex trading. Typically, such developments increase demand for safe-haven currencies like the Japanese yen, potentially limiting AUD/JPY gains. Traders are weighing these risk-on and risk-off signals, while keeping an eye on USD liquidity and European market sentiment, which could further impact intraday exchange rate fluctuations.  

Market participants are positioning themselves for possible volatility as technical support at 111.50 holds. A sustained break above this level could pave the way for further upside toward 112.00, while any retracement may see the pair testing nearby support zones. Overall, the interplay between global risk sentiment, economic releases, and geopolitical events continues to shape AUD/JPY trading dynamics.
