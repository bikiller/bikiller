AUD/USD experienced a minor decline of approximately 0.2% on Monday, trading around the 0.7180 level after opening the week with a notable downside gap near 0.7115. This movement suggests that traders are currently downplaying the risks associated with escalating tensions in Iran, which had a potential impact on market sentiment.

Throughout the session, the currency pair managed to recover much of its initial weakness, yet it remained below the recent high of around 0.7220 reached on Friday. The exchange rate's fluctuations indicate a cautious approach among investors, as they weigh geopolitical developments against overall market conditions.
