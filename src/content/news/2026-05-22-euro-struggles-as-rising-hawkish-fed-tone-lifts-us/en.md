EUR/USD continued to trade in a narrow range during the Asian session on Friday, hovering around 1.1610. The euro remained under pressure as the US Dollar held firm, reflecting heightened expectations for a hawkish stance from the Federal Reserve. Market participants are pricing in the likelihood of further rate hikes, keeping the exchange rate subdued for the second consecutive day.  

Traders noted that the USD’s resilience is underpinned by stronger Treasury yields and hawkish Fed commentary, which weighed on the currency pair. Technical analysts highlighted that a break below recent support could open the path to lower levels near 1.1580, while resistance is seen around 1.1650. Market flows indicate cautious trading as investors monitor central bank signals closely.  

The subdued euro reflects broader market sentiment favoring the dollar, with investors seeking safe-haven assets amid tightening monetary policy expectations. As the week progresses, the exchange rate may continue to mirror shifts in Fed communication, economic data releases, and risk appetite in global markets. Traders are watching for any signs of easing hawkish sentiment that could lift the EUR/USD back toward earlier ranges.
