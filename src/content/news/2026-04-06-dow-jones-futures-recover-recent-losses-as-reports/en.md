Dow Jones futures have regained some ground following recent losses, currently trading at 46,660, up 0.06% during European hours on Monday. This bounce comes as reports suggest potential progress in US-Iran ceasefire talks, sparking optimism in global markets. The recovery signals a stabilizing sentiment ahead of the US market open, with traders eyeing key geopolitical developments.

The futures movement has provided a brief reprieve to the USD, with currency pairs such as EUR/USD seeing moderate volatility. Investors remain cautious as the situation develops, weighing the potential impact on the exchange rate and broader market sentiment. Should ceasefire talks gain traction, the dollar could strengthen further, affecting major currency pairs and potentially shifting investor focus toward risk-on assets.
