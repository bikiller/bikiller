The AUD/USD currency pair surged on Wednesday, trading at 0.6940, marking a 0.56% increase for the day. The Australian Dollar gained traction as investor sentiment improved, buoyed by easing geopolitical tensions surrounding the US-Iran situation. The risk-on mood prompted a selloff in the US Dollar, with the greenback losing ground against several major currencies.

The rebound in AUD/USD reflects broader market optimism, as traders reacted positively to hopes of de-escalation between the US and Iran. With the USD under pressure, the pair continues to hold above the key 0.6900 level, indicating potential for further gains if the positive sentiment persists. Traders will keep an eye on any developments in global risk sentiment for further direction.
