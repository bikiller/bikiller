Gold (XAU/USD) remains steady below the $5,200 level, trading with modest intraday gains as the market enters the European session on Tuesday. Despite the ongoing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East, the precious metal has failed to gather enough momentum for a strong bullish push, holding its ground but without clear follow-through buying.

The outlook for a potential Federal Reserve rate cut has also added a layer of uncertainty, influencing market sentiment. With the USD under pressure, traders are watching for any signs of movement in gold prices, especially as the global economic landscape and currency fluctuations play a critical role in shaping the trading outlook. Currently, gold continues to hover below $5,200, keeping traders cautious as they await further developments.
