Currency markets are entering a phase of cautious consolidation as the April 28 deadline for the War Powers Resolution looms. According to DBS Group Research's Philip Wee, this uncertainty has led to a flattening of the USD/CNY exchange rate following an earlier relief rally. Traders are adjusting their positions in light of the looming political developments.

In the broader Asian forex landscape, the USD is navigating a mixed trading environment, with notable movements across various currency pairs. The EUR/USD remains under pressure as investors weigh potential shifts in monetary policy, while the USD/JPY is also experiencing fluctuations. The current market sentiment reflects a wait-and-see approach ahead of key political events that could impact trading dynamics in the coming days.
