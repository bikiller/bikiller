The EUR/JPY exchange rate surged on Wednesday, climbing to highs above 183.80 after opening at 182.65 on Monday. The euro gained strength against the Japanese yen, bolstered by stronger-than-expected Eurozone manufacturing data. The positive figures lifted market sentiment, contributing to the euro’s recovery as traders reacted to the upbeat economic numbers from the region.

This sharp upward movement pushed EUR/JPY closer to the critical 184.00 level, signaling strong bullish momentum. With the Eurozone data reinforcing expectations of economic resilience, market participants are now eyeing further gains in the currency pair, especially if sentiment continues to improve and the euro maintains its relative strength against the yen.
