USD/CAD is under pressure, extending its decline for the second consecutive session. Trading around 1.3710 during the Asian session on Monday, the currency pair is testing key support near the 1.3700 level. This comes as the exchange rate continues to hover near its moving averages, suggesting a potential shift in momentum.

The ongoing bearish sentiment in USD/CAD reflects broader market trends, with the U.S. dollar struggling against the Canadian dollar. As the pair moves closer to critical support, traders will be watching for any signs of a reversal. A break below 1.3700 could trigger further losses for the USD, while a bounce could signal a resumption of the uptrend.
