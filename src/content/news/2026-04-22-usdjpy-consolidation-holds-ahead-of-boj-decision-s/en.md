The Japanese Yen (JPY) has remained stagnant against the US Dollar (USD), showing limited movement as it underperforms relative to other G10 currencies. The USD/JPY exchange rate is currently consolidating within the 157.50–160.50 range, with little momentum driving price action. This subdued trading reflects a cautious market ahead of the Bank of Japan's upcoming policy decision.

Technical indicators, including a flat RSI, suggest that momentum remains weak, and there is no clear directional bias in the currency pair. Investors are likely staying on the sidelines as they await clarity from the BoJ, with the market anticipating potential policy adjustments or signals that could impact the Yen’s outlook in the coming days.
