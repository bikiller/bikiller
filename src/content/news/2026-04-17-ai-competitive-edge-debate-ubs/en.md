UBS Chief Economist Paul Donovan discussed how Artificial Intelligence (AI) could reshape productivity, particularly between the European Union (EU) and the United States (US). He raised the possibility that AI could provide the EU with a competitive edge, potentially strengthening the EUR/USD exchange rate. A stronger EU economy driven by AI could increase demand for the euro, leading to upward pressure on EUR/USD in trading.

The debate around AI’s economic impact has been influential on forex markets, as traders closely monitor US and EU economic policies. Currently, the EUR/USD is trading near 1.0900, reflecting slight bullish momentum for the euro. However, the USD remains supported by robust economic data, keeping the exchange rate range-bound. As the AI impact becomes clearer, future movements could see greater volatility, especially if the EU outpaces the US in implementing AI innovations.
