Rabobank’s FX Strategy team has revised its short-term EUR/USD forecasts lower, driven by ongoing disruptions in the Strait of Hormuz and rising energy prices. The prolonged instability in this key oil shipping route has escalated both oil and gas prices, placing additional pressure on the euro. As a result, the EUR/USD exchange rate is expected to face downward pressure in the near term.

With oil prices surging, the US dollar has gained strength as traders react to the economic implications. The higher energy costs are exacerbating inflationary concerns in the eurozone, leading to an expected weakening of the euro against the USD. Market participants are now closely monitoring these developments, as the EUR/USD pair approaches key technical support levels, which could trigger further downward movement if breached.
