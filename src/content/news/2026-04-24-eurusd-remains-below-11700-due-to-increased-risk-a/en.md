EUR/USD is currently trading around 1.1690, stabilizing after three consecutive days of losses. The currency pair remains under pressure as it struggles to break above the 1.1700 mark. This steadiness reflects a broader trend of risk aversion in the market, driven by ongoing geopolitical tensions, particularly concerning the US-Iran situation.

The US Dollar (USD) continues to attract safe-haven demand, contributing to its strength against the Euro (EUR). As uncertainty looms, traders are closely monitoring the developments in the conflict, which may influence future trading patterns and exchange rates. The current sentiment suggests that unless there is a significant shift in the geopolitical landscape, EUR/USD may remain capped below the 1.1700 level for the time being.
