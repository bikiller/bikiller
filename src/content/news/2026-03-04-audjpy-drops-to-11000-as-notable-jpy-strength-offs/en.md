AUD/JPY dropped to 110.00 as Japanese yen strength overshadowed a positive Australian Q4 GDP report. The currency pair failed to maintain momentum after a brief rebound from the mid-109.00s, its weekly low, highlighting ongoing pressure from JPY demand. Despite stronger-than-expected GDP data out of Australia, which showed a solid economic performance, the yen’s appeal as a safe haven led the pair lower.

Traders took little comfort from the robust Australian growth figures, with the Australian dollar struggling against the yen’s bullishness. The AUD/JPY exchange rate's failure to gain meaningful traction signals that market sentiment remains skewed towards yen strength, especially amid global uncertainties. With 110.00 acting as a key level, further downward movement could see the pair testing lower support zones.
