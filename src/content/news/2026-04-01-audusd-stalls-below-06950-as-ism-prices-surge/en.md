AUD/USD stalled just below 0.6950 on Wednesday after a brief bounce from the 0.6900 level. The currency pair gained around 0.33% early in the session, moving higher as traders reacted to the latest market data. However, the rally lost steam, and the exchange rate slipped back under the 0.6950 resistance as trading momentum slowed.

The pullback came as ISM prices surged, highlighting inflationary pressures and dampening optimism about the broader economic outlook. The USD strengthened against several major currencies, contributing to the decline in AUD/USD. As the pair faces challenges at the 0.6950 mark, traders will keep a close eye on upcoming data for further direction in the currency pair.
