The USD/CHF currency pair is trading positively, rising to around 0.7960 in early European session on Wednesday, thanks to a rebound in the US Dollar. This movement reflects a strengthening of the Greenback as traders react to recent economic data and market sentiment.

However, potential interest rate cuts by the US Federal Reserve next year cast a shadow over the USD's outlook against the Swiss Franc. As market participants weigh the implications of a softer monetary policy, the exchange rate could face downward pressure, leading to increased volatility in the USD/CHF pair in the coming sessions.
