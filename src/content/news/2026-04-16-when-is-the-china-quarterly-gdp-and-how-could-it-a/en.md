The National Bureau of Statistics of China (NBS) will release its Q1 GDP data on Thursday at 02:00 GMT. Analysts expect a 1.3% quarterly growth, slightly higher than the 1.2% seen in Q4. Year-on-year, China’s economy is forecast to expand by 4.8%, an improvement over the 4.5% growth in the previous quarter. These figures will be closely watched by forex traders, particularly those trading the AUD/USD currency pair.

If China’s growth surpasses expectations, the Australian dollar (AUD) could see upward momentum against the USD, as stronger economic performance typically boosts demand for Australian exports. Conversely, a weaker-than-expected result may pressure AUD/USD lower, with traders adjusting positions based on the outlook for China’s economic recovery. The release is expected to drive significant volatility in the Asia-Pacific trading session.
