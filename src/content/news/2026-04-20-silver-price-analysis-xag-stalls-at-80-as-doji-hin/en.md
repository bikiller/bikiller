Silver (XAG/USD) prices dropped over 1% on Monday, trading below $80 as negative sentiment overshadowed last week's optimism. The metal gapped down over the weekend, reacting to rising geopolitical tensions. The closure of the Strait of Hormuz and the US's seizure of an Iran-flagged vessel fueled concerns, triggering a shift in market sentiment.

This downturn is further highlighted by the formation of a doji candlestick pattern on the daily chart, indicating potential downside. As investors reassess risk amid the escalating conflict, silver's price action faces resistance at $80. A sustained break below this level could signal further declines, impacting the broader precious metals market and influencing the broader USD and EUR exchange rates.
