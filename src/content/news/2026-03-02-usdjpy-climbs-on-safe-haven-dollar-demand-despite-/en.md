The USD/JPY currency pair is trading at 157.50 on Monday, marking a 0.84% increase on the day. This upward movement brings the exchange rate closer to the February high of 157.66, reflecting the sustained strength of the US Dollar. Despite expectations for a Bank of Japan (BoJ) rate hike, demand for the safe-haven USD continues to push the pair higher.

The bullish momentum is primarily driven by broader USD strength in the forex market, overshadowing potential shifts in BoJ policy. As the pair nears critical resistance at 157.66, traders are eyeing whether USD/JPY will maintain its upward trajectory, or if any surprise dovish actions from the BoJ could alter the current dynamic.
