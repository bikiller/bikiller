EUR/USD continues to trade under pressure, hovering around 1.1740 during the Asian session on Wednesday. The currency pair has remained subdued for a second consecutive day as the US Dollar (USD) strengthens. Market caution is palpable, primarily driven by the ongoing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East, which has led investors to seek safer assets.

As uncertainty looms over the region, the exchange rate has struggled to break above the key level of 1.1750. The dollar's advance reflects a broader risk-off sentiment in the markets, affecting trading strategies among investors. Without significant developments to alleviate the geopolitical pressures, the EUR/USD could remain vulnerable in the near term, with traders closely monitoring any shifts in sentiment.
