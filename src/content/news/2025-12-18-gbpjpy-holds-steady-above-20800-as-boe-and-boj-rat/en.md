The GBP/JPY currency pair remains stable above the 208.00 mark as traders await critical rate decisions from the Bank of England (BoE) and Bank of Japan (BoJ). Following a rebound from a one-week low of 206.75 reached on Tuesday, the exchange rate has found itself oscillating within a narrow range during Thursday's Asian trading session.

Market participants are closely monitoring upcoming monetary policy announcements, which are expected to influence the future direction of the GBP/JPY. With ongoing speculation surrounding interest rate adjustments, fluctuations in this currency pair could intensify, reflecting broader trends in the forex market as traders position themselves ahead of these pivotal decisions.
