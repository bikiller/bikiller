The US Dollar extended its gains on Thursday, surging past the 99.00 level on the US Dollar Index (DXY) and approaching the psychologically significant 100.00 mark. Traders noted strong USD momentum against major counterparts, including the euro, as geopolitical tensions and safe-haven flows underpinned demand. Currency pairs like EUR/USD and GBP/USD saw increased volatility as the greenback's advance put pressure on their exchange rates.  

Market participants highlighted that the USD's strength is reinforced by uncertainty in global markets. Investors are actively trading the dollar amid concerns over geopolitical developments, prompting a shift from riskier assets into the world’s reserve currency. Technical analysts point to the DXY nearing key resistance around 100.00, suggesting potential for further upside if momentum holds.  

The euro and other major currencies faced headwinds, with EUR/USD retreating sharply amid the dollar rally. Traders are closely watching USD/JPY and other crosses for reaction to the DXY’s movements. Overall, the robust USD is reshaping currency trading strategies, pushing investors to reconsider positions across FX markets while monitoring macro and geopolitical signals that could drive further exchange rate swings.
