Brent crude oil prices are nearing the $100 mark as escalating geopolitical tensions surrounding Iran and the Strait of Hormuz raise supply concerns. According to Rabobank’s RaboResearch Global Economics & Markets team, these uncertainties are driving traders to react, impacting not only oil prices but also currency pairs sensitive to energy costs, particularly the USD/EUR exchange rate.

As Brent continues to trade higher, market participants are closely monitoring how these developments may influence inflation and monetary policy decisions. A sustained increase in oil prices could lead to stronger demand for safe-haven currencies, altering trading strategies across the forex market. The potential for heightened volatility remains, making it crucial for traders to stay informed about geopolitical developments that could further impact supply dynamics.
