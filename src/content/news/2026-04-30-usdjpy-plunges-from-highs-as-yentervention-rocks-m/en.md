USD/JPY tumbled sharply on Thursday, shedding 2.25% in a dramatic session that erased roughly 500 pips from the currency pair within hours. The sudden plunge came after the pair tested fresh highs earlier in the day, only to reverse violently amid signs of intervention in the Japanese yen. Traders cited the rapid swing as a response to central bank signals and heightened market sensitivity around USD/JPY levels, sending shockwaves through forex markets.  

The intraday reversal pushed the USD/JPY exchange rate back toward key support zones, prompting renewed caution among traders. Volatility spiked as speculative positions were unwound, while EUR/USD and other major currency pairs also felt ripple effects from the sharp yen movement. Market participants noted that liquidity conditions amplified the move, with stop-loss triggers accelerating the decline and fueling aggressive trading flows.  

Analysts suggest the plunge may signal a turning point for USD/JPY, highlighting the persistent influence of yen interventions on global trading. Investors are now closely watching technical levels, with implications for broader currency strategies as USD strength faces challenges. For forex markets, the move underscores how sudden policy signals or central bank actions can sharply reshape trading dynamics across both G10 and emerging currencies.
