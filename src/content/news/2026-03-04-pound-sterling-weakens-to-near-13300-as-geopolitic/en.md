The GBP/USD currency pair saw a dip to around 1.3310 in early European trading on Wednesday, as geopolitical tensions in the Middle East sparked a "flight to safety" that benefited the US Dollar. The escalating conflict has driven investors towards the USD, pushing the exchange rate lower against the Pound Sterling.

The risk-off sentiment strengthened the USD, with market participants flocking to safe-haven assets amid growing uncertainties. As a result, the GBP/USD pair now hovers near the key psychological level of 1.3300, indicating increased pressure on the British currency. Traders will be closely watching how the geopolitical situation evolves, as it could further influence the pair’s movements.
