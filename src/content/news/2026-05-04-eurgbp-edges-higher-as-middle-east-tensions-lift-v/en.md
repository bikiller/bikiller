The EUR/GBP currency pair edged higher on Monday, with tensions in the Middle East keeping market volatility elevated. At the time of writing, the exchange rate was trading around 0.8645, recovering from a low of 0.8629. These geopolitical uncertainties have prompted investors to remain cautious, amplifying the volatility in the forex market.

In addition to global tensions, political instability in the UK is putting further pressure on the British Pound. As uncertainty around the UK’s political landscape grows, traders are weighing the potential impact on the British economy and its currency. This combination of geopolitical and domestic factors has caused fluctuations in the GBP, contributing to the recent movement in the EUR/GBP pair.
