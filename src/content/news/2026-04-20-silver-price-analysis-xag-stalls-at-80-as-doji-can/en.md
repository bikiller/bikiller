Silver (XAG/USD) experienced a decline of over 1% on Monday, reversing Friday’s positive momentum. The precious metal gapped down over the weekend, with prices stalling at $80 amid rising concerns in the global market. The escalation of tensions, including the closure of the Strait of Hormuz and the US seizing an Iran-flagged vessel, added to the pessimism, overshadowing prior optimism.

As market sentiment soured, silver's price action hinted at potential downside risks. A doji candle pattern on the chart suggests indecision in the market, and traders are cautious ahead of further developments. The XAG/USD exchange rate remains under pressure as the US dollar strengthens, adding to the bearish outlook for silver in the short term.
