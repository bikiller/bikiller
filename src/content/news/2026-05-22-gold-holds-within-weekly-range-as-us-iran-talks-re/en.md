Gold trading remained range-bound on Friday, with XAU/USD consolidating near the week's established levels as investors weigh ongoing geopolitical developments. The precious metal hovered within familiar support and resistance zones, reflecting cautious market sentiment amid U.S.-Iran negotiations aiming to resolve tensions in the Middle East. This steady pattern kept broader forex markets, including USD and EUR exchange rates, largely on hold.  

Traders noted that XAU/USD’s resilience stems from its safe-haven appeal, while movements in major currency pairs such as EUR/USD and GBP/USD remain sensitive to any updates from the diplomatic front. The market is also balancing economic data and central bank signals, which influence USD strength and, in turn, gold pricing. Technical analysts point to the current weekly range as a key reference for potential breakout or pullback scenarios.  

Looking ahead, market participants are expected to continue monitoring diplomatic headlines closely, as any breakthrough or stalemate could trigger renewed volatility across commodities and forex markets. A decisive shift in the U.S.-Iran talks may prompt sharp moves in XAU/USD, while correlated currency pairs could experience shifts in liquidity and volatility, reinforcing gold’s role as a hedge in uncertain conditions.
