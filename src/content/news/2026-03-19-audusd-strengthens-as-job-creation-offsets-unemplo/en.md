AUD/USD is trading at 0.7050 on Thursday, gaining 0.34% on the day. The Australian Dollar (AUD) remains resilient following the release of the latest employment data, which showed strong job creation. Despite a slight increase in the unemployment rate, the data has provided optimism for the currency, supporting its upward movement against the US Dollar (USD).

However, the upside for AUD/USD remains capped as the Federal Reserve’s cautious stance continues to limit further gains. With the Fed maintaining its tightening measures, the USD remains relatively strong, preventing any significant surge in the currency pair. Market participants will be watching for any new signals from the Fed that could influence the exchange rate in the coming days.
