Analysts at Societe Generale are eyeing the upcoming Bank of England (BoE) meeting, fueled by stronger-than-expected UK inflation and positive Purchasing Managers' Index (PMI) data. While the consensus predicts a unanimous decision to hold rates, the potential for a hawkish dissent is emerging, which could influence the GBP/USD exchange rate. Traders should monitor this development closely as it may impact trading strategies.

The recent economic indicators suggest a resilient UK economy, prompting speculation on the BoE's future monetary policy direction. If dissenting opinions arise within the committee, the British pound could strengthen against the euro and USD, potentially pushing the GBP/EUR and GBP/USD pairs higher. Investors are advised to stay alert for any shifts in sentiment leading up to the BoE's announcement, as it could create volatility in the currency markets.
