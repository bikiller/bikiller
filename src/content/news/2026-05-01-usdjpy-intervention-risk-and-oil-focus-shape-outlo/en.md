The USD/JPY exchange rate has seen a notable shift, with the Japanese Yen staging a sharp rebound. This movement is largely attributed to suspected foreign exchange intervention, as the Japanese Ministry of Finance signals its readiness to take action. Authorities are reportedly prepared to step in on both the currency market and crude oil futures to stabilize the Yen, which has been facing persistent weakness.

The market now watches closely for key technical levels in USD/JPY, particularly the range of 155–158. If the pair pushes higher, it could signal further intervention, as authorities look to curb Yen depreciation. Traders are keenly aware of the risks of intervention, which could significantly influence the direction of the exchange rate in the near term.
