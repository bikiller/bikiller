The Euro has recently emerged as the best-performing G10 currency, with EUR/USD seeing a steady rise as markets adjust to the European Central Bank's (ECB) tightening stance. Rabobank's Senior FX Strategist Jane Foley highlights that this shift in expectations has supported a cautious upside in the EUR, as the pair trades near key resistance levels.

The ongoing repricing of ECB policy tightening is driving the EUR/USD exchange rate, with the euro benefiting from the market's anticipation of further rate hikes. Despite some volatility, the pair's movement reflects broader market sentiment, with traders closely monitoring developments in both the ECB's actions and the broader global economic outlook. The shift may signal further upward potential for the EUR, depending on how market conditions evolve.
