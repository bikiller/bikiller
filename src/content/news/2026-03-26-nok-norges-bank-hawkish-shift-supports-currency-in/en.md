Norges Bank’s recent hawkish stance has given the Norwegian krone a boost, with the central bank signaling future rate hikes to combat inflation beyond the energy shock. The bank’s updated projections now fully incorporate at least one rate hike. This shift has supported the NOK against major currencies, including the EUR/NOK, which continues to trade within a firm range as the market reacts to the policy change.

While ING anticipates a more cautious approach, forecasting only one rate hike, they base this outlook on a bearish view of the oil and gas sector. The outlook on crude prices will likely play a significant role in determining the krone's path. As traders adjust to this policy shift, the NOK’s strength could face volatility, depending on global oil market dynamics and broader risk sentiment.
