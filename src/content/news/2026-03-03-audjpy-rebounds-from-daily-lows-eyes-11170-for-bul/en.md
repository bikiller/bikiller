The AUD/JPY currency pair has recovered from earlier losses, bouncing off daily lows beneath the 111.00 mark. As the session closes, the pair is down 0.63%, trading at 110.89. This rebound suggests that despite a temporary pullback, the market remains sensitive to technical levels and price action. Traders are watching closely for a potential breakout towards the key resistance level at 111.70.

The movement in AUD/JPY reflects broader market sentiment, with fluctuations driven by shifts in risk appetite and global economic data. As the pair eyes 111.70, the breakout could signal further bullish momentum, especially if the Australian Dollar strengthens against the Japanese Yen. Investors will likely remain focused on the upcoming developments for both currencies, which could influence future trading strategies.
