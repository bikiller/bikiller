The USD/JPY currency pair remained largely unchanged on Friday, with the exchange rate hovering around 159.58. Price movement was limited as holiday market conditions created subdued trading, causing fluctuations between minor gains and losses. Traders adopted a cautious stance, awaiting the release of the US Nonfarm Payrolls (NFP) report, which could significantly impact market sentiment.

Intervention risks also loomed over the pair, with concerns about potential government action to curb yen weakness. While the market remained focused on the upcoming jobs data, the lack of liquidity amplified volatility. As the NFP report approaches, traders are poised for potential shifts in the USD/JPY exchange rate, depending on the strength of the US labor market.
