The USD/JPY pair dropped to around 157.55 during the Asian session on Wednesday, as the Japanese Yen strengthened against the US Dollar. The yen's rise comes amid heightened geopolitical risks, particularly the escalating tensions between the US, Israel, and Iran, which have sparked a surge in safe-haven demand. As investors flock to less risky assets, the yen has gained traction, impacting the USD/JPY exchange rate.

Market participants are closely monitoring developments in the Middle East, as the ongoing conflict continues to weigh on global sentiment. The USD/JPY's move toward the 157.50 level highlights the yen’s resilience in uncertain times. With the geopolitical risks still looming, the Japanese currency is likely to maintain its strength in the near term, further challenging the US Dollar’s dominance in the currency markets.
