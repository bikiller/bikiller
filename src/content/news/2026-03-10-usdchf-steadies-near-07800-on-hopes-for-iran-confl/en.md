The USD/CHF currency pair is holding steady around the 0.7800 level, recovering from two consecutive days of losses. During the Asian trading session on Tuesday, the pair was seen at 0.7780, as the US Dollar (USD) made a modest rebound after a sharp drop in the previous trading session. This stabilization comes amid growing optimism surrounding a potential resolution to the ongoing Iran conflict, which has provided some support to risk sentiment in global markets.

The recent movement in the USD/CHF exchange rate reflects a broader trend of dollar strength as the market digests geopolitical developments. As investors closely monitor the situation in Iran, the pair’s price action will likely remain sensitive to any news or announcements, with the 0.7800 mark serving as a crucial support level. If the dollar continues to recover, the pair could push higher, testing resistance levels near 0.7850.
