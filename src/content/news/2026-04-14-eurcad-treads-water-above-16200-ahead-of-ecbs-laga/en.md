EUR/CAD is holding steady above the 1.6200 mark as traders await remarks from European Central Bank (ECB) President Christine Lagarde. The currency pair is currently hovering around 1.6220 during European trading on Tuesday, showing minimal movement after a slight decline the previous day. Market participants are keeping a close watch on Lagarde’s speech, which could provide insights into the ECB’s future policy stance and impact the exchange rate.

The EUR/CAD pair is experiencing tight consolidation ahead of key developments, with the exchange rate stabilizing around 1.6220. The slight losses from the previous session suggest a cautious market tone as investors await more clarity on the ECB's monetary policy outlook. Any shift in sentiment following Lagarde's comments could drive further volatility, particularly with the ongoing fluctuations in USD-related markets affecting the broader forex landscape.
