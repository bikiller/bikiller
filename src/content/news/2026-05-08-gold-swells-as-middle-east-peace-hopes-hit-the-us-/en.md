Gold (XAU/USD) saw a notable increase of around 0.75% on Friday, driven by growing optimism in financial markets regarding a potential resolution to the ongoing Middle East conflict. The possibility of peace in the region sparked hopes of reduced oil prices, which could alleviate inflationary pressures and support global economic stability.

As a result, the US Dollar (USD) faced downward pressure, with traders adjusting their positions amid expectations of easing inflation. The gold market remains sensitive to shifts in the USD exchange rate, as a weaker dollar typically boosts demand for the safe-haven asset. Traders will closely monitor geopolitical developments for further impacts on gold and broader currency pair dynamics.
