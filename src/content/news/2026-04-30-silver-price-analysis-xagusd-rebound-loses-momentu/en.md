Silver (XAG/USD) has gained 2.81% on Thursday, trading at $73.40 after a significant pullback in recent weeks. The white metal is attempting a technical rebound, following a rejection near the $80 mark in mid-April. This move comes amid a broader market response to persistent concerns over restrictive policy risks, which have pressured metals and other commodities.

Despite the upward momentum, the rebound in silver faces headwinds as investors remain cautious due to potential tightening measures from central banks. The risks tied to restrictive policies, especially in the context of the USD's strength, are likely to limit further upside for the currency pair. Traders will be eyeing any changes in policy or economic signals that could impact XAG/USD in the near term.
