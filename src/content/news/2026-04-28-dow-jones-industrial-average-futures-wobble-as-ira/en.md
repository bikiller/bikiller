Futures for the Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA) showed minimal movement on Tuesday, hovering near the 49,200 mark. The cash index saw a slight gain of 0.1%, supported by a 5% rise in Coca-Cola's (KO) stock following a stronger-than-expected earnings report. Despite this, broader market sentiment remained cautious as talks surrounding Iran’s nuclear deal stalled, keeping traders on edge.

In the forex market, the USD continued to face mixed reactions, with the EUR/USD pair holding steady at 1.0850 as market participants assessed potential geopolitical risks. Crude oil prices surged, contributing to volatility, as concerns over supply disruptions escalated. This spike in oil prices added pressure to the broader markets, with traders monitoring the potential impact on global inflation and economic recovery.
