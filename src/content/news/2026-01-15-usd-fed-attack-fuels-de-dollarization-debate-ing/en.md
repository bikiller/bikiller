The recent comments from the Federal Reserve have intensified discussions surrounding the de-dollarization trend, particularly impacting the USD. This decline reflects increasing investor caution amidst ongoing geopolitical uncertainties, which could influence broader trading strategies across various commodities and currency pairs. As the market reacts, participants are urged to stay alert to evolving conditions that may affect both oil prices and the overall strength of the USD.
