Gold (XAU/USD) is experiencing downward pressure on Thursday, continuing its pullback for a second consecutive day. The precious metal has struggled to maintain bullish momentum after facing rejection near the significant psychological level of $4,500. Currently, the exchange rate is around $4,425, reflecting a decline of nearly 0.60%.

The strengthening US Dollar has contributed to gold's recent weakness, impacting investor sentiment and trading strategies. As the market reacts to various economic indicators and central bank policies, traders are closely monitoring the interplay between the USD and gold prices, which may influence future movements in this currency pair.
