The outlook for the USD remains shaped by multiple factors, with TD Securities strategists Oscar Munoz and Eli Nir highlighting the role of geopolitical risks, notably developments in Iran. These external risks, alongside recent inflation data and upcoming economic indicators, are set to influence the Federal Reserve's policy stance. The USD's performance against major currencies like the EUR will likely be sensitive to these dynamics.

With the Federal Reserve adopting a patient approach, the USD's near-term trajectory will depend on how inflation evolves and how economic activity unfolds. Market participants will closely watch economic data for hints of potential Fed action, especially in relation to inflation expectations. The EUR/USD pair, currently trading around technical levels, could experience heightened volatility as these factors develop.
