The EUR/USD exchange rate has faced downward pressure, dropping to 1.1554 as the currency pair struggles to hold onto recent gains. The downside momentum has intensified, reflecting the prevailing bearish sentiment in the market. As the trading day unfolds, analysts expect the pair to continue its decline, with the next potential target at 1.1530, a key short-term support level.

Despite this downward trend, the major support level at 1.1480 remains intact for now, offering some buffer against further declines. The EUR/USD's movement reflects a broader market environment where the USD maintains its strength, and traders are eyeing critical levels for potential reversals. A sustained break below 1.1480 could pave the way for more significant losses in the near term.
