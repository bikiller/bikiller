Oil prices have surged recently, causing concerns over energy shortages and posing risks to the growth outlook in Asia. Higher oil prices are pressuring regional currencies, with many Asian FX pairs under strain. The USD/JPY is showing resilience amid these challenges, holding steady around 134.50, while the EUR/USD remains range-bound, fluctuating between 1.0700 and 1.0750. The growing energy uncertainties are increasing volatility in Asian currencies, particularly the Chinese yuan, which has seen a notable decline.

As energy costs climb, Asian economies are facing mounting pressures, with inflation risks rising. The USD continues to benefit from these global headwinds, with its strength exacerbating the situation for Asian exporters. Market participants are closely monitoring technical levels for signs of further deterioration, especially as oil prices hover above $85 per barrel. The outlook for regional currencies remains cautious, with key support levels in focus for USD/INR and USD/KRW amid ongoing market volatility.
