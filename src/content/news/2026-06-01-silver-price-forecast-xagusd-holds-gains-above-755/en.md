Silver price (XAG/USD) remained supported above the $75.50 level on Monday, with the metal trading near $75.60 per troy ounce during the Asian session after a period of heightened volatility. Market participants continued to favor safe-haven assets as uncertainty surrounding a potential Iran deal weighed on risk sentiment, helping silver retain recent gains despite fluctuating trading conditions across financial markets.

The precious metal’s resilience came as investors monitored geopolitical developments and their potential impact on global supply chains, energy markets, and broader risk appetite. Demand for defensive assets increased as traders assessed the implications of ongoing negotiations, while movements in the USD and major currency pairs also influenced short-term price action. A softer risk environment often supports silver alongside other safe-haven instruments.

From a market perspective, holding above the $75.50 technical level reinforces the constructive outlook for XAG/USD in the near term. Traders will continue to watch geopolitical headlines, shifts in the US Dollar exchange rate, and broader sentiment across forex and commodity markets for fresh direction. Sustained trading above current levels could encourage additional buying interest, while any easing in geopolitical tensions may limit further upside momentum.
