The European Central Bank (ECB) held interest rates steady, signaling caution amid rising geopolitical tensions. Updated projections indicated an uptick in inflation across the Eurozone, with growth forecasts downgraded. As the ECB refrains from immediate tightening, the EUR/USD exchange rate remains pressured, trading near key support levels. Traders are wary of potential volatility driven by external risks, which could further destabilize market sentiment.

With inflation expected to rise and economic growth slowing, the euro faces significant headwinds. Despite the ECB's hawkish stance, the currency pair has struggled to break through resistance levels. The ongoing uncertainty in global markets, particularly in light of geopolitical concerns, leaves the EUR vulnerable to further weakness. Investors are closely watching how the Eurozone navigates these challenges as the USD continues to dominate, maintaining a strong position in global trading.
