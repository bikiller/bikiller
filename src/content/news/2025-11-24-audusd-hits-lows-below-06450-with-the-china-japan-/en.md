The Australian Dollar (AUD) is facing downward pressure, with the AUD/USD currency pair falling to lows below 0.6450 as tensions between China and Japan dampen market sentiment. Despite a generally positive market outlook on Monday, the Aussie has struggled to gain traction against the US Dollar (USD), reflecting broader regional uncertainties.

Traders are closely monitoring these geopolitical developments, which have overshadowed any potential support from favorable trading conditions. As the exchange rate continues to fluctuate in response to these external factors, market participants remain cautious, assessing the implications for future trading strategies involving the AUD and other major currencies like the Euro (EUR).
