EUR/USD is experiencing a pullback from its recent highs, trading at approximately 1.1585 on Thursday after briefly surpassing the 1.1600 mark earlier in the week. The currency pair has maintained most of its weekly gains despite this retreat, indicating robust bullish momentum in the market.

The recent uptick in the US Dollar has contributed to the drift from these elevated levels. As traders assess economic indicators and anticipate potential shifts in monetary policy, the fluctuations in the exchange rate reflect ongoing market dynamics. With the USD gaining traction, the EUR/USD pair remains a focal point for investors navigating these developments.
