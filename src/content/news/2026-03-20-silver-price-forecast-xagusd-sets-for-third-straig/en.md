Silver (XAG/USD) is trading within a narrow range around $72.80 during the European session on Friday, maintaining the gains made in Thursday's recovery. The rally was supported by a weakening US Dollar (USD), which allowed silver to hold steady. The currency pair's price movement indicates that the white metal is attempting to stabilize after recent declines.

However, despite the recovery, XAG/USD is on track for a third consecutive negative weekly close. The ongoing pressure from the broader market sentiment and the USD's strength over the past week could limit any significant upward movement. Traders are eyeing key technical levels and will likely wait for further clues from global economic data to gauge silver's next major price move.
