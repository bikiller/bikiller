Gold (XAU/USD) is struggling to maintain its footing in the European session, hovering just below the critical level of $4,700. The precious metal has displayed some resilience, but ongoing pressure from a strengthening US Dollar (USD) has kept prices subdued. The USD has gained traction for the third consecutive day, significantly impacting gold's appeal as a safe haven.

As the exchange rate fluctuates, the firm performance of the USD continues to be a pivotal factor weighing on gold's performance. Traders are eyeing market movements closely, as any further strengthening of the USD could exacerbate gold's struggles. With the EUR also facing pressure, the dynamics within the currency pairs are crucial for investors assessing gold's short-term outlook.
