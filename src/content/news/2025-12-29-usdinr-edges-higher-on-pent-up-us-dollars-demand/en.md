The USD/INR currency pair has started the final week of 2025 on a slightly bullish note, with the Indian Rupee (INR) opening lower against the US Dollar (USD). Traders are closely watching the exchange rate dynamics as the pair fluctuates.

As demand for the US Dollar persists, analysts predict that the USD/INR pair will remain sensitive to economic indicators and geopolitical developments in the coming days. The current trading environment suggests that the exchange rate could experience further volatility, influenced by shifts in market sentiment and investor positioning ahead of year-end financial adjustments. Market watchers will be keen to see how this trend unfolds amid global economic uncertainties.
