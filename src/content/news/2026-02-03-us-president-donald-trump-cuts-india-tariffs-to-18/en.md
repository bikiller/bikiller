The decision is expected to have a broader impact on market sentiment, with implications for global trade. The USD/INR pair could see increased volatility, particularly with oil prices potentially rising as a result of India shifting away from Russian oil. Market participants will be eyeing further policy actions from both governments as they react to the changing trade relationships.
