EUR/USD remains under pressure as market sentiment leans toward downside risks, with ING's FX team highlighting the 1.150 level as a key focus. While Europe's gas supply situation has improved compared to 2022, the Euro continues to struggle against a stronger USD. Traders are eyeing the currency pair's movement closely as the European economy faces ongoing challenges.

The exchange rate between EUR and USD has been consistently volatile, driven by the divergence in economic outlooks. While the Eurozone benefits from more stable energy supplies, the Dollar is bolstered by resilient U.S. economic data and expectations of further tightening from the Federal Reserve. This dynamic leaves the EUR/USD vulnerable, with a potential drop toward 1.150 increasingly in focus for forex traders.
