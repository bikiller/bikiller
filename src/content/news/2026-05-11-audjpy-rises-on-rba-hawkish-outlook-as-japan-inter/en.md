AUD/JPY edged higher on Monday, trading around 113.70, reflecting a 0.13% gain as investors reacted to a hawkish outlook from the Reserve Bank of Australia. The Australian dollar found support on expectations of sustained interest rate pressure, while traders remained cautious amid ongoing uncertainties in global risk sentiment. The currency pair’s modest advance underscores a steady appetite for AUD amid mixed market conditions.  

Market participants noted that potential intervention from Japanese authorities could limit further gains for the pair. While the exchange rate has lifted on improving sentiment for risk currencies like the AUD, yen strength remains a consideration, keeping traders vigilant. Technical levels around 113.70 now serve as a focal point, with momentum indicators hinting at restrained upward movement in the near term.  

The broader forex landscape saw USD and EUR trading with relative stability, adding context to AUD/JPY flows. Analysts suggest that any decisive move above key resistance could hinge on further RBA commentary or shifts in Japan’s monetary stance. For now, trading remains cautious, with investors balancing hawkish Australian signals against intervention risks, maintaining a measured approach to positioning in major and cross currency pairs.
