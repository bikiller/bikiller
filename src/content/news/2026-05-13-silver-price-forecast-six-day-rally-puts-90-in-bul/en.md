Silver (XAG/USD) extended its rally on Wednesday, marking a sixth consecutive day of gains with prices climbing more than 1.30%. The bullish momentum has traders eyeing the $90 level, as the currency pair strengthens amid mixed signals from the U.S. economy. Despite elevated inflation figures, which could dissuade the Federal Reserve from cutting interest rates, market participants are increasingly bullish on silver as a safe-haven play.

The ongoing advance reflects a combination of technical buying and a weaker USD, as investors adjust their positions ahead of key economic data. Traders are noting that the exchange rate dynamics against the EUR and other major currencies also support precious metals, providing an added layer of trading optimism. Momentum indicators suggest that short-term traders continue to favor long positions, fueling the rally further.

Looking ahead, silver faces resistance near $90, while support remains anchored around previous consolidation levels. Any shifts in U.S. interest rate expectations or currency pair fluctuations could impact the trajectory of XAG/USD. Analysts caution that while the current trend is strong, volatility in the forex market could bring sudden retracements, making strategic risk management essential for those trading silver against the USD and other major currencies.
