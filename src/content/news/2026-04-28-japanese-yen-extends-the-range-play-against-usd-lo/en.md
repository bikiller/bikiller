Japanese Yen extended its range-bound trading against the US dollar on Tuesday, with the USD/JPY currency pair consolidating around the mid-159.00s during the Asian session. Exchange rate action remained subdued as traders refrained from placing fresh directional positions, awaiting the upcoming Bank of Japan (BoJ) policy decision for clearer guidance on monetary policy direction.

Market participants continue to observe tight trading conditions in USD/JPY, with volatility compressed as the currency pair remains stuck within a narrow band. The Japanese yen’s weakness against the US dollar has persisted, but momentum has stalled ahead of potential signals from the BoJ that could reshape expectations around interest rates and yield differentials in the forex market.

Outlook for the USD/JPY exchange rate now hinges on the Bank of Japan’s stance, with traders watching for any shift that could trigger a breakout from the current consolidation zone. A decisive move could influence broader USD trading sentiment and spill over into other currency pairs such as EUR/USD, shaping near-term forex market direction.
