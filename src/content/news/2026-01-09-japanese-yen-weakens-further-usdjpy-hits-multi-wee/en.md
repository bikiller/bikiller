The Japanese Yen (JPY) continued its downward trend, with the USD/JPY currency pair reaching a multi-week high as the US dollar strengthens ahead of the upcoming Non-Farm Payrolls (NFP) report. The exchange rate climbed amid growing uncertainty surrounding the timing of the next interest rate cut by the Bank of Japan (BoJ), influencing traders' sentiment.

Adding to the Yen's woes, rising tensions in the China-Japan relationship have further pressured the currency. As the market reacts to these developments, the JPY is likely to face continued volatility, particularly as investors await clearer signals on monetary policy from the BoJ and insights from the NFP release, which could impact the broader forex market dynamics.
