The USD/CHF currency pair is currently trading in negative territory, hovering near the 0.7950 exchange rate during the early European session on Wednesday. The US Dollar has weakened against the Swiss Franc as ongoing geopolitical tensions, particularly the crisis in Venezuela, bolster demand for safe-haven currencies. 

Adding to the pressure on the USD, dovish remarks from US Federal Reserve officials have raised concerns about future monetary policy direction. As traders navigate these developments, the Swiss Franc continues to attract attention, highlighting its appeal amid market uncertainties.
