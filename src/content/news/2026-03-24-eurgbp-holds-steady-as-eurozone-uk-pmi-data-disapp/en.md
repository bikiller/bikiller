EUR/GBP remains steady around 0.8650 on Tuesday as traders react to disappointing economic data from both the Eurozone and the UK. The latest PMI reports for both regions revealed weaker-than-expected growth, suggesting a slowdown in economic activity. This has left investors cautious, with the currency pair showing limited movement despite the underperformance in key economic indicators.

The lack of a clear direction for EUR/GBP follows disappointing readings across the Eurozone and UK manufacturing and services sectors, signaling a potential downturn. As investors digest these figures, the market continues to remain neutral, with the exchange rate trading near the technical level of 0.8650. With no immediate catalysts to drive the pair significantly higher or lower, the currency pair is likely to trade in a tight range unless fresh economic data or geopolitical developments influence the market.
