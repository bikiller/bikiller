The AUD/USD currency pair saw a dip-buying surge after a bearish gap opened near the 0.7115 region on Monday, quickly recovering above the mid-0.7100s during the Asian trading session. This bullish setup comes as market participants show renewed interest in the Aussie, likely driven by broader USD weakness and stronger-than-expected economic indicators from Australia. As the pair pushes higher, attention will turn to key technical levels, with traders eyeing resistance near 0.7150.
