The EUR/USD currency pair is experiencing modest gains, trading around 1.1645 during the early Asian session on Monday. Traders are closely monitoring developments as expectations rise for a softer stance from the Fed. These speculations could lead to further fluctuations in the forex market, with the EUR gaining traction amid a backdrop of anticipated monetary policy adjustments. As the market awaits the Fed's announcement, the 1.1650 level will be a key technical point to watch for potential price movements in the near term.
