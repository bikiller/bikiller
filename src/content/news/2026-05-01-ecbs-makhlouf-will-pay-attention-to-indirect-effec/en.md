ECB Governing Council member Gabriel Makhlouf expressed concern over the indirect impacts of rising energy prices, particularly on production, transportation, and services. In his blog post on Friday, Makhlouf highlighted that the European Central Bank (ECB) would monitor how these factors affect inflation and economic growth, which could influence future monetary policy decisions. The EUR/USD exchange rate remains sensitive to such developments, as traders assess potential ECB actions in response to energy-driven price increases.

The market’s reaction to rising energy costs has been closely linked to fluctuations in the euro. A continued uptick in energy prices could pressure the ECB to take a more aggressive stance on interest rates to curb inflation, potentially impacting the EUR’s value against other major currencies. As the EUR/USD approaches key technical levels, investors will be watching for signals from the ECB that could guide trading decisions and affect broader market sentiment.
