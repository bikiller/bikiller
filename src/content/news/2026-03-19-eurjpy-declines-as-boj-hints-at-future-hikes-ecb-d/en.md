EUR/JPY is trading at 182.70 on Thursday, showing a 0.23% decline as markets react to comments from the Bank of Japan (BoJ). The BoJ's governor hinted at potential interest rate hikes in the future, sparking fluctuations in the yen. This development is keeping traders on edge as they monitor the implications for Japanese monetary policy.

As the EUR/JPY exchange rate faces downward pressure, market participants are also awaiting the European Central Bank's (ECB) upcoming decision. The ECB's stance on interest rates and monetary tightening will be pivotal for the euro's direction in the coming sessions. The uncertainty surrounding both central banks is contributing to cautious trading behavior in the forex market.
