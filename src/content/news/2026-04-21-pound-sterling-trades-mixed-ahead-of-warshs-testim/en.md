The Pound Sterling (GBP) experienced a mixed trading session against major currencies on Tuesday, as investors awaited key economic indicators and Federal Reserve commentary. The GBP/USD pair traded around 1.3680, reflecting a slight decline of 0.15%, while GBP/EUR hovered at 1.1540, showing a modest gain of 0.10%. Traders remain cautious ahead of the upcoming testimony from Fed Governor Christopher Warsh and the release of UK Consumer Price Index (CPI) data, which could influence the GBP's trajectory.

Market sentiment remains fragile, with analysts highlighting the potential impacts of inflation data and central bank signals on the exchange rates. Any unexpected shifts in the UK CPI could lead to increased volatility in the GBP. Meanwhile, the broader forex market anticipates Warsh's insights, as they may provide clues on future monetary policy direction, crucial for determining the pound's strength against the USD and EUR in the coming days.
