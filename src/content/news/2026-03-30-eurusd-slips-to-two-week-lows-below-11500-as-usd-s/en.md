EUR/USD continues to face downward pressure on Monday, dropping to two-week lows below the key 1.1500 level. The US Dollar (USD) remains broadly stronger, keeping the Euro (EUR) on the defensive. At the time of writing, the currency pair is trading around 1.1444, marking the fifth consecutive day of losses for EUR/USD. 

This extended weakness in the exchange rate reflects a sustained USD rally, driven by favorable market sentiment and expectations surrounding the Federal Reserve's monetary policy. As EUR/USD struggles beneath the 1.1500 threshold, further losses could pave the way for additional technical challenges, with traders eyeing the next support levels.
