Gold (XAU/USD) is experiencing a notable increase on Wednesday, buoyed by a risk-off sentiment prevailing in global markets that has reignited demand for safe-haven assets. Currently trading around $4,113, the precious metal has risen nearly 1%, continuing its upward trajectory after dipping below the critical $4,000 mark on Tuesday.

The shift in market sentiment comes as investors closely monitor the upcoming FOMC Minutes, which may provide insights into the Federal Reserve's monetary policy direction. As the exchange rate for XAU/USD climbs, traders are weighing the implications for the USD and other currency pairs, anticipating potential volatility in response to the central bank's signals.
