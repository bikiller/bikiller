The USD/JPY currency pair continued its upward movement on Wednesday, with the exchange rate climbing as oil supply concerns and a strong US dollar kept pressure on the Japanese yen. The yen weakened as the market focused on the potential disruptions in oil supply due to the ongoing US-Iran conflict, which has significant implications for Japan’s energy imports from the Middle East.

Traders are increasingly worried about the potential impact on global energy markets, particularly Japan, which relies heavily on foreign energy sources. The Japanese yen remains under pressure as the USD benefits from its safe-haven status. At the close of trading, USD/JPY was seen testing higher levels, with the exchange rate maintaining its bullish trend, supported by robust demand for the greenback.
