The AUD/USD currency pair dropped to 0.7010 on Thursday, marking a decline of 0.95% for the day. This movement comes as the US Dollar continues to strengthen, buoyed by stronger-than-expected macroeconomic data from the United States. Traders are closely watching economic indicators, which are supporting the USD’s bullish momentum against major currencies.

The drop in AUD/USD is also tied to a shift in market sentiment, with safe-haven flows favoring the US Dollar amid global economic uncertainties. As investors seek stability, the USD remains in demand, further pressuring the Australian Dollar. The pair’s trading below 0.7050 indicates heightened caution in the market, with further downside potential as US data continues to support the greenback.
