The Chinese yuan remains contained within a narrow onshore trading range, with USD/CNH expected to fluctuate quietly between 6.7920 and 6.8060, according to United Overseas Bank’s Quek Ser Leang and Lee Sue Ann. Recent price movements have failed to generate significant momentum, leaving the currency pair in a neutral stance as traders digest recent market developments.

Market participants are monitoring the USD/CNH exchange rate for hints of directional shifts, though current conditions suggest limited volatility. The tight intraday band reflects cautious sentiment amid broader forex trends, as USD strength against major currencies such as the EUR and JPY remains a key influence. Traders may continue to take a wait-and-see approach before committing to larger positions.

Technically, the lack of clear breakout signals indicates that the yuan’s short-term trajectory could remain range-bound. Analysts highlight that unless USD/CNH moves decisively beyond the 6.7920–6.8060 corridor, trading is likely to stay subdued. This environment underscores the importance of monitoring both domestic factors and global USD movements for any sudden impact on the onshore yuan.
