Silver (XAG/USD) is showing a firm upside on Wednesday, buoyed by a broadly weaker USD following news of a two-week ceasefire agreement between the United States and Iran. Traders pushed the currency pair to an intraday high of $77.65, with XAG/USD currently hovering around $74.50, marking an almost 2% gain on the day. The softer dollar has increased the appeal of silver as an alternative store of value.  

Technical analysis indicates XAG/USD is consolidating within a bearish flag on the 4-hour chart, suggesting traders are weighing further upside against potential short-term retracements. Market participants are closely monitoring key support and resistance levels, as any break above the intraday high could trigger further buying, while a reversal might test lower thresholds near $74.00.  

Investor sentiment remains influenced by geopolitical developments and USD performance, with the exchange rate dynamics between major currencies such as EUR/USD also impacting silver flows. Continued volatility in the forex market may encourage short-term trading opportunities, with XAG/USD likely to remain sensitive to both macroeconomic signals and safe-haven demand amid evolving international tensions.
