The escalating conflict in the Middle East has raised concerns over a potential oil supply shock, which is providing strong support for the USD against the EUR. Commerzbank’s Thu Lan Nguyen highlights that this geopolitical risk is contributing to the Dollar's strength, given the United States’ status as a net oil exporter and its role as the global reserve currency. The EUR/USD exchange rate is facing downward pressure, with the Dollar benefitting from a more resilient economic outlook compared to the Eurozone.

As the oil market remains volatile, the Dollar's relative economic stability is enhancing its appeal for traders. This dynamic is particularly evident in EUR/USD trading, where the pair continues to see pressure around key technical levels. Given the geopolitical uncertainties and oil price fluctuations, the Dollar is likely to maintain its upward momentum, further tightening the EUR/USD exchange rate.
