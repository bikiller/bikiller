The USD/JPY pair has regained the 159.00 level after dipping to a weekly low of 158.26. This rebound comes amid mixed economic data from the US, which supported the US Dollar. The greenback strengthened, pushing the US Dollar Index (DXY) to a two-day high of 98.29. At the time of writing, the exchange rate for USD/JPY stands at 159.17, showing a modest gain of 0.11%.

Despite the recovery, upside potential is limited by RSI divergence, suggesting weakening bullish momentum. While the USD remains relatively strong, the divergence hints at potential resistance that could cap further gains in the near term. Traders are likely to remain cautious, closely monitoring US economic data and technical indicators for further direction in the USD/JPY pair.
