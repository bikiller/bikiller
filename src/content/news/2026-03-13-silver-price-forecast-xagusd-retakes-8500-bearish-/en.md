Silver (XAG/USD) found some support during the Asian session on Friday, pushing the price back above the $85.00 level. This recovery helped the metal reverse a two-day losing streak, although it remains poised to close the week relatively unchanged. The price action suggests a slight stabilization, with market sentiment continuing to weigh on further upside potential.

Despite the recent bounce, bearish risks persist for silver, as it faces resistance from the broader market's cautious outlook. The USD's strength remains a key factor, as the exchange rate dynamics between the greenback and other currencies like the EUR could influence investor sentiment toward precious metals. With the week winding down, traders remain cautious, monitoring developments in both the US and global economies for further directional cues.
