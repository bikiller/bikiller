GBP/JPY weakened on Friday, with the British Pound (GBP) falling against the Japanese Yen (JPY) as disappointing UK economic data weighed on market sentiment. The exchange rate for GBP/JPY hovered around 211.50, erasing the gains made earlier in the week. The weaker-than-expected GDP numbers sparked concerns over the UK’s economic outlook, putting pressure on Sterling.

The decline in GBP/JPY came amid broader market concerns, as investors adjusted their positions following the data release. As the exchange rate retraced earlier gains, traders are closely monitoring any further developments in the UK economy. With the pair now under 212, it faces the possibility of further downside, depending on future economic indicators and any shifts in global risk sentiment.
