The GBP/USD pair is trading around 1.3400, down 0.2% during the late Asian session on Friday. The currency pair has come under pressure as the US Dollar Index (DXY) rebounds following a sharp decline of more than 1% the day before. This rebound has contributed to the strength of the USD, pushing the British pound lower.

Technical analysis shows that the 38.2% Fibonacci retracement level near 1.3470 is acting as a significant barrier for the GBP/USD exchange rate. Any move toward this level faces strong resistance, keeping the pair capped in the near term. Market participants are closely watching for any further developments in US dollar strength as the next potential catalyst for the currency pair’s direction.
