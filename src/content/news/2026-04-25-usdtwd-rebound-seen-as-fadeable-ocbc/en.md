A technical rebound in USD/TWD has emerged, with strategists at OCBC noting that it is likely to be short-lived. This movement is largely driven by a broader strengthening of the US Dollar (USD), fueled by risk aversion in the wake of a stalled US-Iran ceasefire agreement. The pair has seen increased demand for USD as market participants seek safety amidst geopolitical uncertainty.

The USD/TWD exchange rate recently bounced back, as the US Dollar continues to gain ground against its counterparts. Despite this uptick, OCBC analysts suggest that this rebound is expected to fade as market sentiment stabilizes. With the USD's recent rally, traders should be cautious, as further upward movement could be limited in the near term unless new catalysts emerge.
