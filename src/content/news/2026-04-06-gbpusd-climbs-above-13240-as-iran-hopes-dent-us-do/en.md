The British Pound (GBP) surged over 0.40% on Monday, with GBP/USD climbing above the key 1.3240 level. This upward movement came after US President Donald Trump stated that the deadline he set for Iran to strike a deal, on Tuesday, is non-negotiable. The announcement raised concerns over potential tensions in the region, prompting a weaker US Dollar (USD) amid market uncertainty.

Meanwhile, speculation surrounding a possible de-escalation in US-Iran tensions added further pressure on the USD. As the greenback softened, the GBP/USD exchange rate found support, maintaining its position above the 1.3240 threshold. Traders remain focused on geopolitical developments and their potential impact on the broader forex market, particularly USD fluctuations.
