AUD/USD is currently trading around 0.6460, holding steady as the market consolidates following last week's drop to three-month lows. The Australian Dollar remains under pressure, facing difficulty in garnering fresh buying interest amid ongoing tensions between China and Japan, which weigh on investor sentiment.

The softening of the USD has provided some support, yet the cautious market backdrop continues to influence trading dynamics. As traders monitor geopolitical developments and their potential impact on the Australian economy, the currency pair may remain range-bound unless a significant catalyst emerges to drive volatility.
