Deutsche Bank analysts predict the European Central Bank (ECB) will maintain its deposit rate at 2% despite market expectations of a potential rate hike in June. The uncertainty surrounding Europe's energy exposure has complicated the outlook for the ECB’s policy tightening. As the energy crisis persists, traders are closely watching the EUR/USD exchange rate, with markets pricing in an increased probability of a rate increase in the coming months.

The energy shock, particularly in the context of rising energy prices, is exerting pressure on the ECB’s decision-making process. While the central bank has been on a tightening path to curb inflation, the ongoing challenges could delay further hikes. The EUR/USD remains a key focus for forex traders, with recent fluctuations influenced by ECB speculation and global energy concerns.
