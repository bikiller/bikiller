GBP/JPY steadied near the 211.00 level, trading around 210.70 during European hours on Wednesday, following two consecutive days of losses. The currency pair exhibited limited volatility as market activity remained subdued due to holiday-thinned trading. 

The Japanese yen (JPY) faced downward pressure as investors analyzed the implications of Japan's expansive fiscal policy. This weakening of the yen contributed to the current exchange rate dynamics, with traders keeping a close eye on economic indicators that may influence further movements in the GBP/JPY pair.
