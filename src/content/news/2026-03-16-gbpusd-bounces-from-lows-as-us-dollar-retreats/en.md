The GBP/USD currency pair rebounded on Monday, rising nearly 0.75% after hitting a low around 1.3220 on Friday. The exchange rate strengthened as the US Dollar softened, allowing the British Pound to make a notable recovery. The pair found support on the lower end of 1.3200, but momentum shifted, pushing it back toward the higher range of 1.3300.

This move was largely driven by a retreat in the USD, which had been strengthening over the past week. As the greenback faced broad selling, the Pound gained ground, navigating between key technical levels. Traders are now looking for further developments in USD sentiment, with attention on upcoming economic data that could influence the outlook for both currencies.
