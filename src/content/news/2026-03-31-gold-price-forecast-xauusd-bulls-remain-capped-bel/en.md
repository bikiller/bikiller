Gold (XAU/USD) remains in a moderate bullish trend on Tuesday, extending its recovery from last week’s dip near $4,100. Despite these gains, the precious metal faces significant resistance around the $4,600 level, which is preventing further upside movement. As a result, the currency pair is struggling to break higher in the short term, consolidating within a tight range.

Market participants are closely monitoring the $4,600 resistance as a key level for potential breakout or reversal. The movement of XAU/USD is largely influenced by broader USD strength, ongoing global economic uncertainty, and investor demand for safe-haven assets. If the $4,600 barrier holds, gold may see a pullback, while a successful break could open the door for further gains.
