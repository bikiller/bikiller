Gold prices (XAU/USD) surged above $4,600, reaching near $4,630 during the early Asian session on Friday. The precious metal’s advance comes as geopolitical tensions in the Middle East prompt renewed demand for safe-haven assets. Gold continues its rally as investors flock to secure investments, driving the price higher despite a firm USD.

This increase in gold's price is attributed to market uncertainty, as traders seek stability amidst global unrest. The XAU/USD currency pair has shown resilience, benefiting from heightened risk aversion. As geopolitical factors remain in focus, gold is expected to maintain its upward momentum, with traders closely watching technical levels around $4,650.
