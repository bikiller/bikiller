Silver (XAG/USD) experienced a sharp decline on Tuesday, plummeting nearly 10% as the US Dollar (USD) gained strength against its counterparts. This significant drop in the silver price reflects a broader trend where rising US Treasury yields dampen investor appetite for safe-haven assets, particularly in the face of the ongoing US-Iran conflict that has generated fragile market sentiment.

The strengthening of the USD has led to a shift in trading dynamics, with the exchange rate for XAG/USD impacted heavily by the changing economic landscape. As traders react to the robust performance of the dollar and the implications of higher yields, the outlook for silver remains uncertain. Investors will need to monitor these developments closely, as the interplay between currency strength and safe-haven demand continues to shape market movements.
