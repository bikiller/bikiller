EUR/USD is maintaining its position above the 1.1750 mark, trading around 1.1770 during the Asian session on Tuesday. This stability follows four consecutive days of modest losses, as traders adopt a cautious stance ahead of the upcoming FOMC Minutes. The anticipation surrounding the Federal Reserve's insights is influencing market behavior, as investors weigh potential implications for the USD.

The current exchange rate reflects a careful balance as the market navigates uncertainties in monetary policy. With the EUR showing resilience against the USD, traders are closely monitoring any shifts that may occur following the release of the FOMC Minutes. This cautious sentiment underscores the significance of upcoming economic indicators in shaping short-term trading strategies for the EUR/USD currency pair.
