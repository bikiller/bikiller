USD/CHF advanced more than 0.58% on Tuesday after forming a bullish engulfing pattern, signaling renewed buying interest in the currency pair. The USD exchange rate strengthened against the Swiss franc as traders pushed the pair above several technical barriers, including the 50-day Simple Moving Average at 0.7868. During trading, USD/CHF climbed to 0.7890, approaching the psychologically important 0.7900 level.

The latest move reflects improving sentiment toward the US Dollar, with investors favoring the greenback amid broader market volatility and shifting expectations around global monetary policy. While EUR and other major currencies showed mixed performance, demand for USD continued to support the pair’s upward momentum. Analysts noted that clearing the 50-day SMA could encourage additional bullish positioning in the near term.

From a technical perspective, maintaining levels above 0.7868 may reinforce the positive outlook for USD/CHF trading. A sustained break beyond 0.7900 could open the door for further gains, while failure to hold current levels may trigger profit-taking after Tuesday’s rally. Market participants will continue monitoring economic data and broader risk sentiment for direction in the currency market.
