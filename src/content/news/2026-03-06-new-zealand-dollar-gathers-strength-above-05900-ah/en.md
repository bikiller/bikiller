The New Zealand Dollar (NZD) is showing resilience, with the NZD/USD currency pair climbing to approximately 0.5910 during the Asian trading session on Friday. Traders are closely watching the upcoming US employment report for February, set to be released later today. The data is expected to influence the exchange rate significantly, as strong job growth could bolster the USD, while weaker figures might support the NZD's current strength. With the forex market reacting to both local and international events, the implications of today's data release could shape trading strategies moving forward.
