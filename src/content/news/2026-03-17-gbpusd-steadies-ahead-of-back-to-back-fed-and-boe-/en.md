The GBP/USD exchange rate steadied on Tuesday as traders prepared for two major central bank decisions this week. The Pound Sterling saw a modest uptick, rising for a second consecutive session. Investors are closely watching Wednesday’s Federal Reserve meeting, where a hold on interest rates is expected, followed by the Bank of England’s rate decision on Thursday. 

The market is cautious ahead of the announcements, with the Pound currently trading at 1.2230 against the USD, maintaining a tight range. Analysts expect the Fed to keep rates unchanged, but any shift in tone could lead to volatility for the USD. Similarly, the BoE's decision will be crucial for the GBP, as expectations linger over its next move amid ongoing economic uncertainty.
