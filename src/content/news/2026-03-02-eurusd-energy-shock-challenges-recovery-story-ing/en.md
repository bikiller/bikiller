Higher energy prices are challenging the Euro’s recovery, according to ING’s Chris Turner. Investors had previously been optimistic about European industry and the EUR/USD exchange rate, leading to a strong overweight position in the Euro. However, rising energy costs are forcing a reevaluation of this sentiment, creating pressure on the currency pair.

As a result, the EUR/USD is facing downward momentum. Despite previous gains, the exchange rate is now encountering headwinds, with investors reassessing their bullish stance on European assets. The pressure is mounting on the Euro as energy concerns weigh heavily on the broader outlook for the Eurozone economy.
