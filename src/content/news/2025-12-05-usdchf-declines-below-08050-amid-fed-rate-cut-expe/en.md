The USD/CHF currency pair is experiencing a decline, trading around 0.8030 during the early European session on Friday. Market attention is now shifting toward the upcoming US PCE inflation data, which could further influence the USD's performance. As inflation figures are released, traders will be closely monitoring the implications for Fed policy, which may impact the USD/CHF exchange rate in the near term. The market's response to these developments will be crucial as it navigates uncertainty surrounding interest rates and economic indicators.
