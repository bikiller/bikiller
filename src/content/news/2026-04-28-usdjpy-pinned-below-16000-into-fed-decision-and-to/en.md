The USD/JPY currency pair remained relatively stable on Tuesday, trading within a narrow 80-pip range. The exchange rate hovered just below the key 160.00 level, closing the session at 159.62. During the day, it touched a low of 158.96 in early Asia before reaching a high of 159.79 in the New York session. 

Market participants stayed cautious ahead of key upcoming events, including the Federal Reserve's policy decision and the release of Tokyo's Consumer Price Index (CPI). These events could drive further volatility in the USD/JPY exchange rate, as traders assess potential shifts in U.S. monetary policy and inflation dynamics in Japan.
