GBP/USD remains steady after a sharp drop of over 0.5% the previous day, currently trading around 1.3230 during the Asian session on Friday. The currency pair has managed to hold onto some of its gains, but technical indicators suggest a continued bearish outlook. The price remains confined within a descending channel, pointing to a prevailing downtrend that could see further downside movement.

Despite recent rebounds, the bearish trend is still intact, with the exchange rate closely monitoring key support and resistance levels. The nine-day exponential moving average (EMA) near 1.3250 acts as an immediate resistance level. Should the pair fail to break above this, additional declines toward lower levels are likely, as the broader market sentiment remains cautious amid ongoing USD strength.
