EUR/GBP is trading within a narrow range on Friday, showing minimal fluctuations between gains and losses as market participants digest recent economic data. The exchange rate is largely influenced by supportive UK economic indicators, which bolster the Pound ahead of the upcoming meetings of the European Central Bank (ECB) and the Bank of England (BoE).

Traders are demonstrating a muted response to the data, maintaining their focus on geopolitical tensions, particularly concerning the US and Iran. As the currency pair hovers near key technical levels, the market remains poised for potential shifts as the central bank decisions loom, with the implications for both the Euro and Pound remaining a central point of interest for forex participants.
