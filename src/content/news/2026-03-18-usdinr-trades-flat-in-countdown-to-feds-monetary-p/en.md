The Indian Rupee (INR) is trading almost flat against the US Dollar (USD) at approximately 92.80 during India's afternoon session on Wednesday. The USD/INR pair is showing signs of hesitation as market participants adopt a cautious stance ahead of the Federal Reserve’s monetary policy announcement later in the day.

With the Fed’s decision expected at 23:30 IST (18:00 GMT), investors are refraining from taking significant positions. The outcome of the policy meeting could have a direct impact on global market sentiment, influencing USD strength and, by extension, the Indian Rupee's exchange rate.
