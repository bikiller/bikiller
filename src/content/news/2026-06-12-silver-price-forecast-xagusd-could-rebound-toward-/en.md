XAG/USD retreated slightly after surging more than 6% in the previous session, trading near $67.00 per troy ounce during Asian hours on Friday. Market participants remain watchful for catalysts that could sustain silver’s rally.

Analysts suggest this setup could push the exchange rate higher toward the $67.00 level, attracting traders looking to capitalize on a rebound. Volatility in other commodities and USD strength are also influencing trading strategies in the spot silver market.  

Investor attention remains on risk sentiment and USD fluctuations, which continue to shape precious metals’ performance.
