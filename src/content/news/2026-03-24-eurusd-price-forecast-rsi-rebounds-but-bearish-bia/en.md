EUR/USD remains under pressure on Tuesday, with the currency pair trading below the 1.1600 level. The Euro (EUR) faces headwinds as the US Dollar (USD) strengthens, driven by growing geopolitical tensions surrounding the US-Israel conflict and the potential escalation involving Iran. The heightened risks have pushed demand for the safe-haven Greenback, weighing on the Euro's performance in the forex market.

Despite a slight rebound in the Relative Strength Index (RSI), the bearish bias persists for EUR/USD. The RSI indicates some short-term corrective action, but the pair remains vulnerable below the key 1.1600 resistance level. Traders are closely monitoring geopolitical developments, as any escalation could further fuel USD demand, keeping downward pressure on the Euro's exchange rate.
