TD Securities strategists believe the US Dollar will maintain its upward momentum as global risk premiums remain elevated. Despite a longer-term bearish outlook for 2026, the USD’s strength continues to benefit from market uncertainty. In particular, currency pairs like EUR/USD are expected to remain under pressure, with the dollar showing resilience due to concerns surrounding geopolitical risks and inflationary pressures.

The risk-on sentiment in global markets is keeping the USD supported, with the greenback strengthening against major counterparts. As the EUR/USD remains below key resistance levels, such as 1.0600, traders are wary of potential fluctuations. The outlook for the dollar remains bullish in the near term, but the longer-term trajectory could shift as global risk dynamics evolve heading into 2026.
