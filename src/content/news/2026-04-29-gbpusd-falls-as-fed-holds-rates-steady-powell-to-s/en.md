GBP/USD remained under pressure on Wednesday, continuing its negative intraday trend as traders absorbed the Federal Reserve's decision to keep interest rates unchanged. The exchange rate held steady after Jerome Powell’s final meeting as Fed Chair, with the central bank opting for a pause in rate hikes. The dollar saw muted movement against major currencies, but the British pound remained vulnerable amid ongoing uncertainties over UK economic performance.

The Fed's hold on rates comes as inflationary pressures in the U.S. remain persistently high, leaving the door open for future hikes. As GBP/USD hovers around key technical levels, market participants are closely watching the implications of Powell’s leadership transition. The decision is expected to have a lasting impact on the dollar’s performance, while the pound’s volatility is likely to persist in the near term due to weak economic indicators in the UK.
