The US Dollar (USD) has gained ground against the Japanese Yen (JPY) on Thursday, pushing the USD/JPY exchange rate back above the 159.00 mark. This move follows a recovery from earlier losses, with the currency pair trading at key levels amid mounting geopolitical tensions in the Strait of Hormuz. As concerns over the US-Iran standoff escalate, market sentiment has been dampened, suppressing risk appetite.

The latest rise in USD/JPY comes as traders react to the ongoing uncertainty surrounding the region. The rift between the US and Iran over the crucial waterway has heightened fears of further instability, adding volatility to the forex market. The exchange rate's movement reflects broader risk-off behavior, with investors flocking to safe-haven assets like the USD, while steering clear of riskier currencies such as the JPY.
