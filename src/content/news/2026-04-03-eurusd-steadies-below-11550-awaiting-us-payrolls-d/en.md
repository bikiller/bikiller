EUR/USD is holding steady below the 1.1550 mark as trading remains subdued amid the holiday-thinned markets on Good Friday. The currency pair is currently oscillating between 1.1530 and 1.1550, with little movement in either direction. Despite this stagnation, EUR/USD is on track to finish the week with a modest 0.3% gain, reflecting the ongoing consolidation.

The price action remains stuck within the broader range seen in March, with the pair unable to break past key technical levels. Market participants are largely awaiting the upcoming U.S. payrolls data, which could offer fresh direction for the exchange rate. Until then, EUR/USD continues to navigate within its tight trading range, with fluctuations remaining minimal.
