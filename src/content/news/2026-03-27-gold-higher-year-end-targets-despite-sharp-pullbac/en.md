Commerzbank has raised its gold price forecasts, now projecting USD 5,000 per ounce by the end of 2026, and USD 5,200 by next year. This upward revision comes despite a sharp 15% pullback in gold's recent performance. The revised targets signal strong long-term expectations, even in the face of short-term volatility in the precious metals market.

The recent correction in gold prices, influenced by shifts in global risk sentiment and tightening monetary policies, hasn't deterred Commerzbank's outlook. As trading continues to fluctuate, with USD and EUR exchange rates playing a crucial role, the bank's revised predictions suggest that gold's fundamentals remain solid. Traders are watching for further developments that could push prices toward these new higher targets.
