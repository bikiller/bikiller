This policy shift improved overall market sentiment, easing trade-related concerns and slightly boosting confidence in the INR. The move also carries implications for EUR-linked trade flows and broader USD positioning in emerging markets.

From a trading perspective, USD/INR remains elevated, signaling persistent underlying demand for the US Dollar amid global uncertainty. However, the tariff decision may limit further upside in the near term, as improved risk appetite encourages selective buying of the Rupee. Traders will continue to monitor policy developments, capital flows, and global USD trends for direction in the currency pair.
