GBP/USD remained in a narrow consolidation range near 1.3500 on Tuesday, closing at 1.3520 with a modest 0.12% decline. The pair has been trading sideways in recent sessions, reflecting indecision ahead of crucial central bank decisions. Traders are eyeing the Federal Reserve's and the Bank of England's upcoming meetings for any potential signals on interest rate adjustments.

With both the Fed and BoE set to announce their latest policies, market participants are cautious, keeping the GBP/USD exchange rate within its current levels. The technical support at 1.3500 continues to hold, while resistance remains just above at 1.3550. The outcome of these central bank meetings could determine whether the pair breaks out of its recent range or maintains its subdued trading tone.
