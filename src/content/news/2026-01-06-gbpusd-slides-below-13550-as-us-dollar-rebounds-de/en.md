The GBP/USD currency pair fell below the 1.3550 level on Tuesday, retreating from a daily high of 1.3567. Despite disappointing US Purchasing Managers Index (PMI) data, the US Dollar managed a rebound, which contributed to the Pound Sterling's decline. At the time of writing, GBP/USD is trading at 1.3519, reflecting a 0.15% drop.

Market sentiment was further influenced by neutral comments from Federal Reserve officials, which did not provide any significant boost to the USD. As traders digest these factors, the exchange rate reflects an ongoing tug-of-war between the GBP and the USD, raising questions about future movements in this key forex pairing.
