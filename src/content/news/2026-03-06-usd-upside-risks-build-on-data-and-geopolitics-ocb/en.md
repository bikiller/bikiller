Recent analysis from OCBC's strategists, Sim Moh Siong and Christopher Wong, indicates that the USD is gaining momentum due to a combination of strong US labor data and escalating geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. These factors have contributed to a bullish sentiment around the Dollar, influencing its exchange rate across various currency pairs. 

As market participants respond to these developments, the USD has shown resilience against the EUR, maintaining levels around 1.05. This upward pressure on the Dollar reflects investor confidence and a potential shift in trading strategies, as traders brace for further volatility driven by global events. The implications for the forex market suggest that the USD may continue to strengthen against major currencies if the current trends persist.
