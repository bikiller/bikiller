Silver (XAG/USD) surged to a near one-month high during the Asian trading session on Wednesday, reaching the $81.00 level before encountering resistance. Despite the initial strength, the precious metal faced challenges in maintaining upward momentum, correcting from its peak. The currency pair's struggle to push past the $81 mark indicates a potential pullback but still retains a generally bullish bias.

The correction comes after a solid rally in silver prices, driven by ongoing demand for safe-haven assets amid broader market uncertainty. While silver's exchange rate remains well-supported above key technical levels, including $80.00, the inability to sustain gains above $81.00 suggests some consolidation could occur in the short term. Traders are likely to monitor price action closely for further signals on direction.
