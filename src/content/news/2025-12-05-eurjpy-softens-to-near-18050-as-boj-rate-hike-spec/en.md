The EUR/JPY currency pair is trading cautiously around 180.60 in the early European session on Friday, reflecting a softer tone. This movement comes as the Japanese Yen appreciates against the Euro, fueled by increasing speculation regarding an imminent interest rate hike by the Bank of Japan (BoJ) at its December meeting.

Market sentiment has shifted as traders adjust their positions based on expectations of tighter monetary policy from the BoJ. A potential rate increase could bolster the Yen further, impacting the exchange rate dynamics with the Euro and creating ripple effects across other currency pairs, including the USD. As traders monitor developments closely, the EUR/JPY remains sensitive to any hints from the central bank's forthcoming communications.
