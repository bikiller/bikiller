AUD/JPY edged lower during Asian trading on Thursday, hovering around 113.60, after rallying more than 0.5% in the previous session. The Australian Dollar struggled to extend its gains against the Japanese Yen as traders digested fresh labor market data from Australia, leaving the currency pair subdued amid broader market activity.

Investors are closely watching the implications of the labor figures, which have tempered optimism for the AUD. With the USD and EUR showing mixed performance across global markets, the AUD/JPY exchange rate reflects cautious sentiment, as traders balance domestic economic signals against international currency trends.

Technical levels around 113.60 may act as a near-term pivot, with market participants likely assessing whether the AUD can regain momentum or if the pair will drift lower. Forex trading volumes remain moderate, highlighting the tentative nature of market positioning ahead of upcoming economic releases and central bank commentary.
