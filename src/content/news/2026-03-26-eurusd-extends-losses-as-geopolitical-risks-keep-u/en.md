The Euro (EUR) continued to decline against the US Dollar (USD) on Thursday, marking a third consecutive day of losses in the EUR/USD currency pair. As geopolitical tensions escalate due to the ongoing US-Israel conflict involving Iran, the USD remains supported, driving the exchange rate lower. Traders are closely monitoring these developments, which are influencing market sentiment and risk appetite.

The EUR/USD pair has faced significant pressure, with fluctuations reflecting the prevailing uncertainty in global markets. As the conflict persists, the Greenback’s strength is evident as it remains firmly bid across various trading sessions. Analysts suggest that unless the geopolitical situation stabilizes, the Euro may struggle to regain its footing against the USD in the near term.
