The Euro (EUR) has shown signs of stability, gaining 0.2% against the US Dollar (USD) as traders react to the latest IFO business sentiment figures, which met expectations. This modest recovery follows last Friday's low, indicating a cautious rebound in market sentiment for the currency pair. 

As the EUR/USD exchange rate edges higher, Scotiabank's FX strategists, Shaun Osborne and Eric Theoret, highlight that the movement reflects a broader response to economic data rather than a definitive trend. The current trading environment suggests that while the Euro is attempting to regain lost ground, uncertainties remain, keeping traders vigilant.
