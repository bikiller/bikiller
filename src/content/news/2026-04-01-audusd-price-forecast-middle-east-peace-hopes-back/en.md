The AUD/USD currency pair is trading at 0.6910, reflecting a modest 0.12% gain during the late Asian session on Wednesday. Although it retraced some early advances, the exchange rate remains supported by investor optimism regarding potential peace progress in the Middle East. This development has bolstered risk sentiment, contributing to the Australian dollar's resilience against the US dollar.

Traders are eyeing the 20-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA) as the next technical hurdle, with further gains towards this level suggesting sustained bullish momentum. However, market participants will be closely monitoring any shifts in geopolitical risks or US economic data, which could influence the USD's strength in the coming sessions.
