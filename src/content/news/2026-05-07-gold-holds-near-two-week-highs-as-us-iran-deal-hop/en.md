Gold (XAU/USD) is maintaining a strong position near two-week highs as it trades around $1,850 an ounce. The precious metal's resilience is largely attributed to ongoing geopolitical tensions and the market's anticipation of a potential peace deal between the United States and Iran. This uncertainty is putting downward pressure on the US dollar, influencing currency pair dynamics across the forex market.

As traders assess the implications of these developments, the exchange rate for the US dollar (USD) remains volatile. A successful resolution could lead to a stronger USD, impacting commodities like gold. However, if tensions escalate, gold may continue to attract safe-haven buying, further solidifying its recent gains and possibly pushing its price higher in the coming sessions.
