Global risk sentiment is under pressure as rising oil prices and declines in equities and bonds have fueled demand for the US Dollar. The USD has gained traction across the board, benefiting from funding-driven support amid heightened geopolitical tensions. The EUR/USD has dropped to a fresh low, approaching 1.0500, as market participants seek refuge in the Dollar.

With oil prices climbing, uncertainty in global markets has intensified, and the USD continues to strengthen in response to risk aversion. In the wake of these market dynamics, investors have been shifting towards the safety of the Dollar, which has pushed major currency pairs lower. The growing divergence between US economic resilience and global turmoil is likely to maintain upward pressure on USD exchange rates in the near term.
