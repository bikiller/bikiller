Silver (XAG/USD) nudges higher on Wednesday, but the precious metal remains within the lower range of Tuesday’s trading session, reflecting a cautious tone in the market. The currency pair is consolidating just below the $78.00 level, as traders weigh short-term momentum against broader USD dynamics in forex trading. Exchange rate activity remains subdued ahead of fresh catalysts.

On the technical side, silver’s advance is being capped by a former support zone that has now flipped into resistance near $78.50. This level is proving significant for XAG/USD traders, limiting bullish attempts and reinforcing a consolidation pattern after recent volatility in USD-denominated assets. Market participants are closely watching whether the exchange rate can break higher or continue sideways.

Looking ahead, XAG/USD may remain range-bound unless a decisive move above $78.50 triggers renewed buying interest. Otherwise, sustained consolidation below $78.00 could signal continued indecision in the silver market, influenced by broader USD strength and shifting risk sentiment across forex markets, including EUR pairs. Traders remain focused on exchange rate momentum for clearer directional cues.
