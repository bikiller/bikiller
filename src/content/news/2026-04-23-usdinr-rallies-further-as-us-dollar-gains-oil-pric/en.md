The USD/INR currency pair continued its upward trajectory on Thursday, as the Indian Rupee (INR) faced mounting pressure for the fourth consecutive trading day against the US Dollar (USD). Strength in the greenback has been bolstered by rising oil prices, which have contributed to inflationary concerns in India, further straining the INR. The exchange rate reached notable levels, highlighting the ongoing volatility in the forex market.

As the USD strengthens, traders are closely monitoring the implications for the Indian economy, particularly regarding import costs and inflation. With oil prices extending their upward trend, the INR is likely to face continued challenges in maintaining its value against the USD. The market remains vigilant as any further shifts in oil prices or US economic indicators could significantly impact the USD/INR exchange rate in the near term.
