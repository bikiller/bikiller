The AUD/USD currency pair extended its bullish momentum on Tuesday, climbing approximately 0.38%. The exchange rate briefly touched a session high of 0.7150, marking a strong intraday rally. However, the pair struggled to hold these gains as investor sentiment weakened, with market participants cautious amid a slump in consumer confidence data.

Despite the initial rally, the Australian dollar faced resistance around the 0.7150 mark, ultimately stalling as broader market concerns took hold. The decline in consumer confidence weighed on the outlook for the Australian economy, putting a cap on further gains for the AUD. Traders will now watch for further economic indicators to gauge the next move in the AUD/USD pair.
