GBP/JPY gained nearly 0.60% on Wednesday, driven by improved market sentiment following speculation about a potential end to the Iran conflict. The currency pair found support at the 100-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) around 210.08, a key technical level that helped stabilize the exchange rate. Despite the rebound, the market remains cautious, weighed down by ongoing geopolitical tensions.

The pair faces significant resistance at 211.00, limiting further upside potential. While the mood in the forex market improved, concerns over broader global instability continue to create uncertainty. As traders monitor developments, any shifts in geopolitical factors or market risk appetite could heavily influence GBP/JPY's short-term direction.
