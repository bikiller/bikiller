The Eurozone's preliminary February Harmonized Index of Consumer Prices (HICP) will be released today at 10:00 GMT. This key inflation indicator is expected to provide fresh insights into the region’s price trends, which are crucial for the European Central Bank’s policy decisions. The data could influence the EUR/USD exchange rate as traders react to potential shifts in inflationary pressures within the eurozone.

If the HICP data shows a rise in inflation beyond market expectations, the euro could gain against the USD, as such a move might prompt the ECB to maintain or even tighten its monetary stance. Conversely, weaker-than-expected inflation data could put downward pressure on EUR/USD, as markets may scale back their outlook for future rate hikes. Watch for possible volatility in the pair around the release time.
