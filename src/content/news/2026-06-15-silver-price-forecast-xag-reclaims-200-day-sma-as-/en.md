XAG/USD climbed more than 3% in trading, with silver prices extending gains as the metal reclaimed its 200-day Simple Moving Average at $68.59. The move strengthens a developing double-bottom pattern, signaling potential trend reversal. Traders in the currency pair XAG/USD reacted to renewed safe-haven demand amid geopolitical tensions linked to the US-Iran conflict.

Geopolitical tensions between the US and Iran boosted safe-haven flows into precious metals, supporting silver alongside gold. The US dollar showed mixed performance in forex trading, influencing broader currency pairs such as EUR/USD. Investors adjusted exposure as volatility increased, with XAG/USD benefiting from defensive positioning and improving technical momentum above key moving average support.

Market participants are now watching whether silver can sustain trading above the 200-day SMA at $68.59 to confirm the double-bottom formation. Continued stability could attract further momentum buying in XAG/USD, while a failure to hold may trigger renewed downside pressure.
