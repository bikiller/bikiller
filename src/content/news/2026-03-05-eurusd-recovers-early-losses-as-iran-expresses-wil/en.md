The EUR/USD currency pair has managed to recover much of its early losses, pushing up to the 1.1625 level during Thursday's European trading session. However, the pair remains slightly lower overall, reflecting a modest decline in the exchange rate. The price action highlights the volatility traders are facing in the current market.

This movement comes as news out of Iran suggests the country's willingness to engage in a potential truce, offering some relief to risk sentiment. Despite the bounce in the EUR/USD pair, the dollar’s strength continues to cap any significant rallies. Traders are likely to remain cautious, eyeing further geopolitical developments and economic data that could influence the USD and EUR in the coming sessions.
