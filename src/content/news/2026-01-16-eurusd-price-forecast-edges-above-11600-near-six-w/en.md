EUR/USD has managed to hold steady after three consecutive days of declines, currently trading around 1.1610 during the Asian hours on Friday. Traders are closely monitoring upcoming economic data releases that could impact the currency pair's trajectory. With momentum indicators pointing toward weakness, the outlook for the Euro against the USD remains cautious as investors weigh potential triggers for future volatility.
