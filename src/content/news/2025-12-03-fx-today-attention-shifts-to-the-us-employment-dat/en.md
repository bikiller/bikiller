The US Dollar (USD) faced significant pressure on Wednesday, slipping to levels not seen since late October as traders positioned for a potential interest rate cut by the Federal Reserve in its upcoming meeting. This shift has heightened volatility across major currency pairs, with the USD/EUR exchange rate reflecting the dollar's weakness.

As the market anticipates the release of crucial US employment data, the focus remains squarely on economic indicators that could influence the Fed's monetary policy. With speculation mounting over another rate cut, the USD's defensive stance could lead to further shifts in trading dynamics, particularly against the Euro (EUR) and other major currencies.
