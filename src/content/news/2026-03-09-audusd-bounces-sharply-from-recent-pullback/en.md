AUD/USD surged by approximately 0.8% on Monday, reaching near the 0.7100 mark and reversing much of last week's pullback. The currency pair closed just below this key technical level, demonstrating a strong recovery after a recent decline. The sharp rebound came as traders reacted to a shift in market sentiment, buoyed by factors such as risk appetite and expectations surrounding U.S. economic data.

The movement in the AUD/USD exchange rate highlights ongoing volatility in the forex market. As the USD faced pressure, the Australian dollar benefited from positive momentum, pushing the pair higher. The recovery to near 0.7100 suggests that traders may now focus on further testing of resistance levels, with the potential for additional upside if bullish sentiment persists across global markets.
