The USD/JPY surged nearly 0.40% on Wednesday, climbing to around 159.60, as the Federal Reserve's decision to keep interest rates unchanged bolstered the US Dollar. The Fed signaled a cautious approach to monetary policy, forecasting just one rate cut in 2026, which provided strength to USD against major peers, including the Japanese Yen.

The pair's volatility remains heightened as it approaches the key psychological level of 160. Traders are closely monitoring Fed statements, with the market anticipating potential shifts in US interest rates throughout the year. This hawkish stance supports expectations for continued USD strength, leaving the USD/JPY exchange rate poised for further movement in the coming sessions.
