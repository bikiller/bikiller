Silver (XAG/USD) is experiencing a notable rally, trading at $89.55, marking a 2.90% increase on Tuesday. The shift in the currency pair reflects a broader market sentiment, with the USD struggling against a basket of major currencies. This has led to increased demand for silver, pushing the exchange rate higher. With silver showing resilience, market participants will be closely monitoring the ongoing geopolitical developments and the USD’s performance in the coming days to gauge the sustainability of the upward momentum.
