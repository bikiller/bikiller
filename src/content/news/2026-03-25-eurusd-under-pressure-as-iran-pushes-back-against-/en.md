The EUR/USD currency pair is facing downward pressure on Wednesday, with the Euro (EUR) struggling against a resilient US Dollar (USD). The exchange rate remains sensitive to ongoing developments in US-Iran ceasefire talks. Despite attempts by the US to broker a ceasefire, Iran has pushed back, adding volatility to the market. The Greenback's strength persists, bolstered by its safe-haven status amid global tensions.

As a result, the EUR/USD exchange rate continues to trade in a narrow range, fluctuating between key technical levels. The pair faces immediate resistance around 1.0900, while support remains near the 1.0800 level. Traders are closely monitoring the US Dollar’s movements, as any new developments in geopolitical tensions could trigger further volatility, affecting the currency pair’s outlook for the short term.
