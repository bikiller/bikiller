The Australian Dollar (AUD) is extending its gains against the US Dollar (USD) as the AUD/USD currency pair continues to build on the previous day’s rebound. After hitting a two-month low earlier this week, the pair has risen for the second consecutive day, trading higher as of Wednesday. The AUD/USD exchange rate is currently showing notable momentum, following a shift in market sentiment.

This surge comes amid political developments, with former President Donald Trump hinting at a potential US withdrawal from the conflict in Iran. The news has led to a weakening of the USD, as investors anticipate reduced geopolitical tensions. The move has resulted in an upward shift for the AUD, with traders closely monitoring any further political updates for continued market direction.
