Silver (XAG/USD) is continuing its downtrend, holding losses near $87.20 per troy ounce during early European trading on Tuesday. Despite an uptick in safe-haven demand due to the ongoing Middle East conflict, the precious metal struggles to gain traction. Market participants remain cautious, with the strengthening USD limiting upside potential.

The current price level reflects a continuation of the losses seen in the previous session. Technical analysis indicates that silver faces substantial resistance, and without a significant shift in market sentiment, further declines may be on the horizon. Traders are closely monitoring geopolitical developments and their potential to affect broader risk appetite, while the USD remains a key driver in the currency pair’s movement.
