Euro area inflation data in February remained in line with the European Central Bank’s (ECB) target, with both headline and core inflation showing little change. Analysts at Nordea, Tuuli Koivu and Anders Svendsen, noted that this stability leaves the broader ECB outlook largely unaffected for now. Despite inflationary pressures, the market's response remained subdued, with the EUR/USD trading within a tight range.

The persistence of energy-related risks has become a critical factor reshaping the ECB’s forward guidance. While inflation remains controlled, Nordea highlights that ongoing energy market volatility could influence future monetary policy decisions. This has added an element of uncertainty, potentially impacting the EUR's performance against the USD, with the exchange rate fluctuating near key technical levels. Traders are closely watching how energy risks evolve and their potential impact on ECB policy.
