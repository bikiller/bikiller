The USD/JPY currency pair encountered resistance just below the 159.00 level during Thursday's Asian trading session, as bulls exercise caution amid declining demand for the US dollar. Market participants are closely monitoring the dynamics of USD demand, which remains softer, influencing trading strategies. As the pair struggles to break through the 159.00 barrier, traders are weighing potential implications for future movements, especially in light of ongoing economic data releases and geopolitical factors affecting the broader currency market.
