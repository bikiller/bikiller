EUR/USD showed a modest recovery on Friday after early losses, as the US Dollar (USD) took a breather following its recent surge. The currency pair edged higher, trading around 1.1545, recovering from a low of 1.1501 earlier in the day. This pullback in USD allowed the Euro (EUR) to regain some ground against its counterpart.

The recent rally in USD had pushed the exchange rate to intraday highs, but the pause in the dollar’s momentum provided some relief for EUR/USD traders. As a result, the pair managed to move back above the 1.1500 mark, indicating a shift in short-term sentiment. Traders are closely monitoring the dollar's performance as it influences broader market trends.
