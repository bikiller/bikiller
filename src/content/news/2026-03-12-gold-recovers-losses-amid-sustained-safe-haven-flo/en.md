Gold (XAU/USD) has managed to recover a significant portion of its intraday losses, currently trading just above the $5,165 level during the early European session on Thursday. The precious metal is down less than 0.20% for the day, reflecting ongoing safe-haven demand as market participants seek refuge amid economic uncertainties.

However, rising US bond yields are likely to cap gold's gains, exerting downward pressure on the currency pair. As traders navigate the volatility in the forex market, the interplay between gold prices and the strengthening USD remains crucial, with implications for broader market sentiment and exchange rates in the coming sessions.
