Silver prices (XAG/USD) have dropped 2% during the Asian trading session on Monday, with the currency pair trading at approximately $75.00. Investors are closely monitoring key data points for signs of direction, particularly in relation to USD performance.

The drop in silver prices reflects broader risk-off sentiment in the markets, where investors are shifting toward safer assets amid uncertainty. As the USD strengthens against other major currencies, such as the EUR, the exchange rate dynamics put downward pressure on precious metals.
