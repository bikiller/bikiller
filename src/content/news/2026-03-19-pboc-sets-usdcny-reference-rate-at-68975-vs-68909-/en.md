The People's Bank of China (PBOC) has set the USD/CNY reference rate at 6.8975 for Thursday’s trading session, slightly higher than the previous day’s fix of 6.8909. The rate also came in marginally above the Reuters estimate of 6.8955, indicating a subtle adjustment in the exchange rate.

This small upward movement in the USD/CNY central rate reflects ongoing market conditions as traders anticipate further guidance from China’s monetary policies. The adjustment could influence short-term trading activity in the currency pair, with the market closely watching for any signs of broader economic shifts or additional interventions from the PBOC.
