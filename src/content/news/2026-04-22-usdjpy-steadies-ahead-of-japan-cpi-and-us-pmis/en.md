USD/JPY remained largely unchanged on Wednesday, trading near 159.50 after a brief rise to 159.64 on Tuesday. The currency pair has been caught in a tight range between 159.10 and 159.60, with little momentum through the midweek session. This price action, marked by small-bodied candles, suggests market indecision ahead of key data releases.

Traders are awaiting Japan's CPI and US PMIs later this week, which could provide fresh direction for the exchange rate. In the meantime, the USD/JPY remains in a narrow consolidation phase, with market participants cautious about major moves until the economic reports are released. The current levels indicate a neutral sentiment, though volatility could increase depending on the data outcomes.
