Pound Sterling (GBP) remained largely unchanged against major currencies, trading at 1.3350 against the US Dollar (USD) during the European session on Wednesday. Investors showed caution ahead of key economic data releases, including the Federal Reserve (Fed) and Bank of England (BoE) policy decisions, as well as UK labor market statistics scheduled for Thursday.

The lack of movement in the GBP/USD currency pair reflects market indecision, with traders hesitant to make significant moves before the data events. The upcoming announcements could offer crucial insights into the future direction of both the Fed and BoE's monetary policies, making Thursday a pivotal day for GBP traders.
