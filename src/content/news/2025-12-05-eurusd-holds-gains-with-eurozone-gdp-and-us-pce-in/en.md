EUR/USD is showing resilience in the forex market, currently trading at 1.1660 after recovering from the 1.1640 support level on Thursday. The currency pair's upward movement reflects limited downside pressure as traders anticipate a potential quarter-point interest rate cut by the Federal Reserve next week, which could influence the exchange rate dynamics between the euro and the US dollar.

With the Eurozone GDP data and US PCE inflation figures on the horizon, market participants are closely monitoring these economic indicators. The interplay between these factors could significantly impact the trading sentiment for EUR/USD, as investors weigh the implications of monetary policy shifts and economic performance in both regions.
