Central European economies are proving resilient despite demographic challenges, including shrinking populations and a declining working-age cohort. BNP Paribas points to continued support from EU funds, which provide crucial backing for growth, alongside strong productivity gains outpacing wage increases. These factors are key to maintaining economic stability and support for currencies like the EUR, which is benefiting from overall growth trends in the region.

While the demographic drag is a concern for the medium-term outlook, the highly skilled and educated workforce helps mitigate potential negative impacts. This dynamic bolsters the EUR, particularly against the USD, as investors remain confident in the region's potential. The exchange rate between EUR/USD remains supported, as growth fundamentals in Central Europe continue to provide a buffer against broader global uncertainties.
