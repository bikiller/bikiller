The Pound Sterling has shown a slight increase against the US Dollar, currently trading at approximately 1.3365 in the Asian session on Friday. This uptick in the GBP/USD currency pair comes as the US Dollar experiences modest declines, reflecting market sentiment ahead of the crucial US Nonfarm Payrolls (NFP) data for February, set to be released at 13:30 GMT.

Traders are closely monitoring the upcoming NFP report, which is expected to influence the exchange rate and overall market dynamics. A strong jobs report could bolster the USD, while weaker data may further support the Pound's recent gains. With the market on edge, the outcome of the NFP release will be pivotal for both currencies moving forward.
