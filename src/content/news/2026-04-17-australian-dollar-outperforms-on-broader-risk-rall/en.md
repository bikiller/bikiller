The Australian Dollar (AUD) outpaced its major peers on Friday, rising 0.16% to reach 0.7175 against the USD as the European session approached the opening of US markets. This move came amid a broader risk rally, with global market sentiment improving and fueling demand for risk-sensitive currencies like the AUD.

Hawkish expectations surrounding the Reserve Bank of Australia's (RBA) monetary policy further supported the AUD. Traders are increasingly betting on tighter monetary conditions, anticipating more rate hikes in the coming months. The AUD’s strength reflects both the positive risk sentiment and confidence in the RBA's aggressive stance. As the US trading day begins, the exchange rate could face further volatility, especially if US economic data alters market expectations.
