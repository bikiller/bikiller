Silver (XAG/USD) steadied on Tuesday after surging more than 7% on Monday, reflecting strong bullish momentum in global trading. The currency pair edged higher by 0.69%, currently trading at $86.58, recovering from daily lows near $83.05. Traders are closely watching how the precious metal responds to continued strength in the USD and potential safe-haven flows amid geopolitical and macroeconomic concerns.

Market participants are increasingly targeting the $90.00 level, with the recent upswing fueled by robust investor demand and technical momentum. The exchange rate movement highlights heightened activity in precious metals, with silver outperforming amid a cautious backdrop for EUR and USD currency pairs. Analysts note that momentum-driven buying could extend gains if support around $83.00 holds, signaling continued appetite among trading desks.

Looking ahead, the silver market faces a mix of opportunities and risks. Sustained buying pressure may push XAG/USD toward near-term resistance, while fluctuations in the USD and broader forex markets remain a key driver. Traders will monitor economic data, central bank signals, and market sentiment to gauge whether this bullish phase can maintain pace, potentially influencing trading strategies across metals and currency pairs.
