Gold (XAU/USD) continues to see modest gains after a solid rebound from the $5,000 psychological level, with follow-through buying observed during Tuesday's Asian session. The precious metal is drawing support as investors seek safe-haven assets, despite a slight uptick in USD strength that is limiting its upside potential. At the time of writing, XAU/USD is holding steady, consolidating its gains within a narrow range.

While gold benefits from persistent risk aversion, the US Dollar's resilience dampens its momentum. The modest strength in USD is a result of stronger-than-expected economic data, which keeps the greenback supported across the board. As the market awaits further US economic reports, traders are likely to keep a close eye on key resistance levels around $5,030 for any potential breakout, as well as USD movements for further direction.
