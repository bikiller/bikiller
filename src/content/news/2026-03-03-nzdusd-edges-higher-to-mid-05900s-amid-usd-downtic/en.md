The NZD/USD currency pair saw a modest rise during the Asian session on Tuesday, edging higher to the mid-0.5900s, up 0.15% for the day. Despite this gain, the pair remains under pressure, staying close to the five-week low it hit the previous day. The uptick comes amid a slight pullback in the USD, but the rally lacks strong momentum.

Although the NZD/USD has managed to recover slightly, its upside potential appears limited. With the pair still trading near a significant support level, further gains may be constrained unless broader market conditions shift. Investors will be watching the USD's performance closely, as any strengthening could weigh on the Kiwi.
