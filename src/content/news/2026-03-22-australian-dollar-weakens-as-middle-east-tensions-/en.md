The Australian Dollar (AUD) faces downward pressure as the AUD/USD currency pair drops near the 0.7000 level during the early Asian session on Monday. Risk-off sentiment is driving the US Dollar (USD) higher, with the escalating tensions in the Middle East fueling a surge in safe-haven demand. 

The geopolitical concerns are prompting traders to seek the relative stability of the USD, causing a noticeable shift in the exchange rate dynamics. The Australian Dollar, which is often more sensitive to global risk sentiment, is weakening as a result, highlighting the impact of Middle East developments on broader forex trading.
