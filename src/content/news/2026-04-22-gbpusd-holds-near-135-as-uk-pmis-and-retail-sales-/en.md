GBP/USD remained stable on Wednesday, holding near the 1.3510 mark after a volatile session. The currency pair saw an early high of 1.3540 during London trading hours, before retreating to 1.3490 as market sentiment shifted. Price action has been contained within a narrow 65-pip range, signaling a lack of clear direction in the market.

With key UK data on the horizon, including PMI figures and retail sales, traders are awaiting further cues. The mixed technical signals, marked by long wicks on both sides, reflect uncertainty, as market participants weigh the potential impact of upcoming economic reports. The exchange rate’s narrow trading range suggests indecision ahead of these critical releases.
