EUR/USD remains under pressure, trading below the 1.1500 mark as the euro struggles to maintain gains. Early attempts to recover were thwarted by the release of disappointing Eurozone Harmonised Index of Consumer Prices (HICP) data. The figure came in below expectations, further dampening market sentiment and preventing the currency pair from breaking past the 1.1490 level.

As the HICP data showed slower-than-anticipated inflation growth, the euro's upside momentum was limited, with traders reassessing expectations for future European Central Bank (ECB) actions. The pair continues to trade cautiously, with the EUR/USD exchange rate hovering near key technical support levels. The soft inflation numbers have raised doubts about the speed of economic recovery, potentially keeping the USD's dominance intact in the near term.
