Gold found support after an early decline, with unresolved geopolitical risks providing a steadying influence. OCBC strategists Sim Moh Siong and Christopher Wong highlighted that the precious metal continues to benefit from strong structural demand. Despite fluctuations, gold remains attractive for traders seeking safe-haven assets, especially amidst uncertainties involving major economies.

With geopolitical tensions and ongoing risks in global markets, gold’s upward potential remains intact. OCBC suggests buying dips, particularly as market volatility surrounding the USD and EUR exchange rates could prompt further shifts in investor sentiment. Gold’s resilience against the dollar’s fluctuations suggests a continued safe-haven appeal in the short term, especially for those holding key positions in major currency pairs.
