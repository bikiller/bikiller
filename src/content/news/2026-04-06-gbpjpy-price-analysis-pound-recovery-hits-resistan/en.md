GBP/JPY saw a solid upward movement on Monday, with the exchange rate climbing higher as the Pound (GBP) gained momentum against the Japanese Yen (JPY). This recovery comes amid growing optimism surrounding a peace plan to resolve the ongoing conflict in Iran, lifting sentiment across global markets. The pair reached a high of 211.45, where it faced significant resistance, limiting further gains.

Traders are closely monitoring the 211.45 level for any breakout potential, as the technical resistance could dictate the next phase of price action. A successful push above this threshold may open the door to further bullish momentum. Conversely, a pullback could signal a retracement, especially if broader market sentiment shifts or if geopolitical developments weigh on investor confidence. With the focus remaining on geopolitical risk, GBP/JPY is likely to experience volatile trading in the short term.
