USD/JPY continues its upward momentum, edging closer to the 158.00 mark on Friday, up by more than 0.20%. The latest US jobs report, which revealed signs of weakness in the labor market, failed to halt the pair's advance. Despite concerns over the economic outlook, the US Dollar (USD) remains in demand, bolstered by broader market risk aversion.

Geopolitical tensions in the Middle East have further contributed to the risk-off sentiment, with traders seeking safe-haven assets, including the USD. As a result, the USD/JPY exchange rate holds firm, maintaining its bullish trend. Should the pair breach the 158.00 level, further gains could be in store, although the ongoing economic uncertainties may keep volatility high.
