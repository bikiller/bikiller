GBP/USD experienced a notable reversal after approaching the 1.3600 mark, as fading momentum curtailed the Pound's strength. UOB strategists Quek Ser Leang and Lee Sue Ann suggest that the currency pair's upside is now capped, with the exchange rate expected to remain within a 1.3495–1.3555 range for the near term.

The shift in market sentiment comes amid a cooling of the bullish trend for GBP, as traders weigh the potential impact of broader economic factors. With the pair struggling to maintain upward momentum, any further movement will likely be constrained by the established range, with market participants looking for new catalysts to drive direction in the coming days.
