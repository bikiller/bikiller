EUR/GBP saw a rebound on Thursday as market participants reassessed the monetary policies of the European Central Bank (ECB) and the Bank of England (BoE). The Euro gained 0.08% against the Pound, pushing the exchange rate to 0.8632. This movement comes amid growing inflation concerns, which have led traders to adjust their expectations for central bank actions in the near future.

The trading environment remains volatile, with both the ECB and BoE likely to continue monitoring inflationary pressures. As the EUR/GBP hovers around the 0.8632 level, traders are closely watching any further policy updates that could influence the pair's direction. The market's response highlights the ongoing uncertainty in the forex market, driven by global inflationary trends and central bank decisions.
