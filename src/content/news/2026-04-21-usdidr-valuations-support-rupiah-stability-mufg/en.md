MUFG's Senior Currency Analyst, Lloyd Chan, highlights the stability of the Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) against the US Dollar (USD) amid ongoing geopolitical tensions. Valuation metrics, particularly the Real Effective Exchange Rate (REER), indicate that the Rupiah is significantly undervalued in comparison to the USD, suggesting potential for a stronger performance in the currency pair.

As traders assess the implications of these valuations, the exchange rate movement may become more favorable for the Rupiah, offering opportunities for strategic positions. Continued vigilance on geopolitical developments will be crucial, as any escalation could impact market sentiment and trading dynamics in the USD/IDR pair.
