The USD/JPY currency pair saw a notable rise on Tuesday, with the US Dollar (USD) strengthening against the Japanese Yen (JPY). At the time of writing, the exchange rate reached 157.77, marking its highest point since January 23. The USD's broad-based strength continues to weigh on the Yen, reflecting ongoing geopolitical tensions, including the conflict involving Iran.

This movement in the USD/JPY pair highlights a shift in market sentiment, driven by the dollar's appeal amid global uncertainty. As investors seek safe-haven assets, the USD remains the dominant choice, pushing the exchange rate higher. If the current trend persists, further gains for the USD could put pressure on the 160.00 resistance level, potentially fueling more volatility in the coming sessions.
