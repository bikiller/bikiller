USD/JPY surged following a quiet Friday session, with momentum building to test the key 160.00 level. United Overseas Bank (UOB) economists Quek Ser Leang and Lee Sue Ann noted that the pair's rapid rise signals a potential challenge to this psychological cap. Despite the bullish movement, the 160.50 level remains a significant resistance zone that could halt further gains.

Traders are closely watching USD/JPY as the exchange rate eyes its next key milestone. The strength of the short-term momentum has raised expectations for the pair to reach 160.00, though a breakout above that level is uncertain due to strong resistance at 160.50. This price action highlights the continued volatility in the forex market, driven by shifts in investor sentiment and broader economic trends.
