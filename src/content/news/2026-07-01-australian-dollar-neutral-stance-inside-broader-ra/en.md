The Australian Dollar (AUD) showed a robust rebound against the US Dollar (USD), climbing from a low of 0.6867 to a peak of 0.6930. According to UOB’s Quek Ser Leang, this upward movement appears somewhat overextended, indicating that traders should exercise caution. The currency pair is expected to navigate within an intraday trading range of 0.6890 to 0.6930, with critical resistance noted at 0.6935.

Market sentiment remains neutral as the AUD/USD continues to oscillate within a broader trading range. The recent price action reflects investors' cautious optimism, but technical indicators suggest that a consolidation phase may be necessary. Traders are advised to monitor these key levels closely, as any significant break above or below could signal a shift in momentum for the Australian Dollar against the US Dollar.
