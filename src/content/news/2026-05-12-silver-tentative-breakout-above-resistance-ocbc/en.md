Silver surged over 7%, breaking through the 83–84 resistance zone, signaling a cleaner technical breakout, according to OCBC’s Christopher Wong. The precious metal’s move has caught the attention of forex traders as it coincides with increased USD and EUR volatility, influencing related currency pairs. Momentum, short-covering, and structural tightness have been key drivers behind this sharp advance.

Market positioning ahead of the Trump–Xi talks has added further fuel to the rally. Traders are recalibrating their exposure to USD-linked assets, while silver’s rally reflects a broader shift in risk sentiment across commodities and forex markets. The breakout above 84 now positions silver for potential gains in subsequent trading sessions, with investors closely watching key technical levels.

Analysts note that the price action underscores how macro events can swiftly impact both metals and currency pairs. Exchange rates, particularly USD/JPY and EUR/USD, have been sensitive to silver’s movements, as traders adjust portfolios. The interplay of momentum trading, geopolitical anticipation, and short-covering has created a dynamic environment where precious metals and forex markets remain tightly interconnected.
