The European Central Bank is expected to maintain its hawkish bias, with analysts at ING arguing that the policy outlook still supports one additional interest rate hike rather than a rapid shift toward easing. For forex trading, this assessment reinforces expectations that the EUR could remain supported against the USD, as market participants continue to evaluate how the ECB’s stance may influence the EUR/USD currency pair and broader exchange rate trends.

According to ING’s Michiel Tukker and Benjamin Schroeder, the reduced sensitivity of interest rates to oil price movements is one of the main reasons the ECB is unlikely to adopt a strongly dovish position in the near term. At the same time, lingering economic uncertainty continues to justify a cautious approach, encouraging policymakers to keep their options open instead of signaling an early change in monetary policy. This combination remains an important driver for currency market sentiment.

For traders, the outlook suggests that expectations surrounding ECB policy will remain a key catalyst for the EUR exchange rate and the EUR/USD currency pair. As investors monitor incoming economic data and central bank communication, the prospect of one more rate hike could continue to shape trading strategies, while persistent uncertainty may keep volatility elevated across major currency markets and influence demand for both the EUR and USD.
