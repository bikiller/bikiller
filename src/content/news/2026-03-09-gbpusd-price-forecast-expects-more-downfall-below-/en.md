The Pound Sterling is experiencing downward pressure, trading down 0.5% against the US Dollar (USD) to around 1.3350 during the European trading session on Monday. This decline suggests a bearish sentiment in the currency pair, with analysts forecasting potential further losses if the exchange rate falls below the critical support level of 1.3250.

Market participants are closely watching this trend, as a breach of the 1.3250 level could trigger additional selling, intensifying the downward momentum for GBP/USD. The broader implications could affect trading strategies across major currency pairs, particularly for those involving the Euro (EUR) and USD, as traders adjust their positions in response to the shifting landscape.
