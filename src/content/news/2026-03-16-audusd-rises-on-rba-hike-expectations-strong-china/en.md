AUD/USD surged on Monday, trading at 0.7060, up 1.16% for the day. The currency pair rebounded sharply after two consecutive days of losses, bolstered by growing optimism surrounding the Australian Dollar. Investors are increasingly pricing in further tightening from the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA), fueling a positive outlook for the AUD.

The Australian Dollar gained support from strong economic data out of China, Australia's largest trading partner. With expectations that the RBA will continue its hawkish stance, traders are closely monitoring potential shifts in interest rates, which could further impact the AUD/USD exchange rate. The movement highlights shifting market sentiment toward riskier assets, strengthening the Australian currency against the USD.
