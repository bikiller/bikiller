The EUR/USD currency pair remains steady above the 1.1500 mark as it holds onto Monday's gains during Tuesday's European session. The pair continues to trade with strength, supported by a weaker US Dollar ahead of the Federal Reserve's upcoming monetary policy decision on Wednesday. As traders await the Fed's announcement, the exchange rate hovers just below the key 1.1530 level, with market participants eyeing any potential hints from the Fed on future rate hikes.

Despite the dollar's pressure, the EUR/USD exchange rate has struggled to extend its recovery beyond the 1.1530 threshold. The upcoming Federal Reserve meeting is expected to be a crucial factor in determining the next direction for the pair, as any dovish signals could weigh on the USD, giving the euro further room to advance. However, a hawkish tone from the Fed could reverse the euro's recent gains and push the pair back below the 1.1500 level.
