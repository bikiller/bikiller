Commerzbank’s Tatha Ghose highlights increasing risks of a sharper depreciation for the Turkish Lira, with the USD/TRY exchange rate approaching the 45.00 level. This comes amid a challenging external environment for Turkey and ongoing disruptions caused by high energy prices. As a result, the Lira faces mounting pressure, and the currency pair shows strong signs of further momentum towards this technical threshold.

The persistent energy price shock, combined with a deteriorating economic backdrop, has triggered concerns over Turkey's financial stability. As USD/TRY pushes closer to the 45.00 mark, market sentiment suggests that a continuation of these trends could result in even faster Lira depreciation. Traders are closely monitoring the pair as it edges towards key levels, with implications for Turkey’s inflationary pressures and broader economic health.
