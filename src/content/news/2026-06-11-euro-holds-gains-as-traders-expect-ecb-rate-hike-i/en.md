The euro steadied gains on Thursday as EUR/CAD rebounded from the previous session’s losses, trading near 1.6110 during early European hours. The currency pair drew support amid expectations that the European Central Bank will announce a rate hike later in the day, reinforcing the EUR’s appeal against the Canadian dollar. Traders monitored the exchange rate closely, weighing the potential impact on broader forex positions.  

Market participants highlighted the euro’s resilience despite mixed signals from global USD movements, noting that the ECB’s anticipated policy action could tighten liquidity and influence trading strategies. Technical analysts pointed to key support levels, emphasizing that a sustained move above 1.6100 could encourage further upside for EUR/CAD while underscoring volatility ahead of the central bank’s decision.  

The broader forex landscape reflected cautious positioning, with EUR/USD and other major pairs responding to geopolitical and economic data. Investors balanced the outlook for euro strength against USD trends, observing that the euro’s recovery may signal confidence in the European growth narrative. As the ECB prepares to set policy, traders remain alert to shifts in exchange rates that could ripple across global markets.
