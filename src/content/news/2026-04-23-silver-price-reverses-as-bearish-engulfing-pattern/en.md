Silver prices (XAG/USD) faced a significant downturn on Thursday, dropping 3% to trade at $75.40 after failing to break above the 50-day Simple Moving Average at $78.73. This bearish reversal, indicated by a bearish-engulfing pattern, suggests the potential for further losses in this currency pair as market sentiment shifts under the weight of prevailing geopolitical tensions.

The rejection at the key technical level of the 50-day SMA underscores the volatility in the silver market, influenced heavily by external news flows. Traders are now closely monitoring the exchange rate movements, as continued pressure could lead to deeper declines in XAG/USD, particularly if broader risk sentiment remains fragile amid ongoing geopolitical developments.
