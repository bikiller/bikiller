Gold prices (XAU/USD) have dipped to approximately $4,450 during early Asian trading hours on Thursday as safe-haven demand wanes. As the USD remains under scrutiny, fluctuations in the exchange rate could impact gold's attractiveness as an investment. The market is keenly watching for any signs of economic slowdown or changes in the labor market that could affect the dollar's strength and alter the dynamics of currency pairs involving the EUR and USD. With profit-taking driving the recent decline, traders will be closely monitoring developments that may signal further movements in the gold market.
