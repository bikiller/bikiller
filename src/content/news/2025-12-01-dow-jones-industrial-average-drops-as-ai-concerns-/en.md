As the markets reacted, traders turned their focus to key currency pairs. The USD faced pressure against the EUR, impacting exchange rates and trading strategies. With major indices showing weakness, market participants are cautious, analyzing potential implications for future trading sessions as economic indicators continue to fluctuate.
