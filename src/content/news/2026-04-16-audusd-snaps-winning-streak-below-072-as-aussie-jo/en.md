AUD/USD ended its three-day winning streak on Thursday, settling just above 0.7165 after struggling to break through the 0.7200 resistance level. Despite initial attempts to push higher, the pair faced strong selling pressure as Aussie employment data came in below expectations, dampening optimism surrounding the Australian dollar.

The disappointing jobs report weighed on market sentiment, as traders reassessed the outlook for the Australian economy. As a result, the currency pair failed to sustain momentum above the 0.7200 mark, with the exchange rate retreating toward 0.7165. The US dollar remained broadly supported, maintaining its dominance in global forex markets amid ongoing economic uncertainties.
