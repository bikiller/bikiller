The USD/CAD currency pair continued its upward trajectory, trading around the 1.3750 mark during the Asian session on Monday. The exchange rate's movement underscores the market's reaction to uncertainty, prompting traders to seek safety in the U.S. dollar.

As the geopolitical landscape remains volatile, the demand for USD persists, influencing the trading dynamics of the USD/CAD pair. Analysts suggest that continued fluctuations may hinge on developments in global affairs, potentially impacting the Canadian dollar. With the exchange rate hovering near critical technical levels, market participants will be closely monitoring further price movements for indications of future trends.
