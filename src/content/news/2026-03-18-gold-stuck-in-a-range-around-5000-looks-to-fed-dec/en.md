Gold (XAU/USD) remains in a narrow range near the $5,000 mark, holding steady for the third consecutive day as traders await the upcoming Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) decision. Market participants are taking a cautious stance, keeping the precious metal's price locked in its current sideways consolidation pattern, with no significant movements observed so far.

The focus now shifts to the FOMC's policy decision, which is expected to provide new direction for gold prices. With the USD remaining under pressure ahead of the Federal Reserve's statement, traders are keen to assess any signals of future interest rate hikes or dovish tones. The outcome could trigger volatility in both gold and the broader currency markets, particularly for the EUR/USD and other major pairs.
