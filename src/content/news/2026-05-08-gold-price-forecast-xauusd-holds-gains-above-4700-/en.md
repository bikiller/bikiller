Gold (XAU/USD) maintains its upward momentum, holding steady above $4,700 on Friday. The precious metal benefits from a weakening US Dollar, as global markets remain cautious. While investors grapple with geopolitical tensions following the recent exchange of fire between the US and Iran, concerns over the stability of a fragile ceasefire also weigh on broader market sentiment.

Despite the risk-off mood in the market, the softer USD is providing solid support for gold, keeping the currency pair elevated. As XAU/USD remains above key technical levels, the outlook for further gains hinges on continued Dollar weakness. Traders will likely monitor developments in the Middle East closely, as any escalation could shift sentiment and impact both the USD and gold prices in the near term.
