The EUR/USD exchange rate saw notable gains, rising to nearly 1.17 as the Dollar weakened. The move came amid a broader improvement in risk sentiment, following news of a ceasefire agreement. This shift in market mood sparked a selloff in the USD, allowing the Euro to capitalize on the positive developments. 

Commerzbank highlights the Euro's positive momentum, with expectations for the European Central Bank (ECB) influencing the market outlook. Traders are increasingly focused on the ECB's next steps, adding further support to the EUR. With the Dollar under pressure, the EUR/USD pair remains near key resistance levels, suggesting potential for further upside if the risk-on environment continues.
