Gold (XAU/USD) remains steady around the $5,350 mark after some volatility in the Asian session, holding its position near the highest level seen since late January. The precious metal's resilience comes amid escalating tensions in the Middle East, which continues to support its appeal as a safe-haven asset.

Despite fluctuations, bulls retain control of the market, with gold staying well within striking distance of recent highs. The ongoing geopolitical uncertainty has contributed to the bullish momentum, keeping demand for gold robust. Traders are closely watching these developments, as further volatility could influence the price movement of the yellow metal, potentially pushing it higher in the coming sessions.
