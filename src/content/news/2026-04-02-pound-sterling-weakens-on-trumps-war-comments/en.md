The GBP/USD pair saw a decline during the early European trading hours on Thursday, with the exchange rate dropping to around 1.3215. The Pound Sterling weakened against the US Dollar following remarks from US President Donald Trump that dampened hopes for a de-escalation in ongoing geopolitical tensions. As a result, the currency pair attracted selling pressure, pushing the Pound lower.

Trump's comments have reignited concerns about potential economic impacts, which weighed heavily on investor sentiment. The US Dollar gained traction amid heightened risk aversion, causing further pressure on the GBP/USD exchange rate. Traders will closely monitor any developments that could influence the pair’s movement, particularly in relation to US geopolitical policies and broader market sentiment.
