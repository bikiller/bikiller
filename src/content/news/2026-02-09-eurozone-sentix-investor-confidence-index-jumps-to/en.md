Eurozone investor sentiment strengthened markedly in February as the Sentix Investor Confidence Index jumped to 4.2 from -1.8 in January. The improvement marked a third straight monthly gain and delivered the strongest reading since July 2025. The data signaled a clear shift in risk appetite, offering fundamental support to the euro across FX trading desks.

The rebound in confidence reflected easing recession fears, stabilizing growth expectations, and optimism around inflation dynamics in the bloc. For currency markets, the release helped underpin the EUR, with traders reassessing near-term exchange rate prospects for major currency pairs such as EUR/USD.
