Standard Chartered analysts Pietro Righi and Christopher Graham have raised concerns over political instability in Romania, warning that it could derail fiscal consolidation efforts and delay critical reforms. As political uncertainty rises, the Romanian leu (RON) could face increased volatility, affecting trading dynamics. The EUR/RON exchange rate could see upward pressure, with traders eyeing potential resistance levels as uncertainty lingers.

The analysts emphasized that political risks are high, potentially undermining investor confidence and delaying Romania’s long-term growth plans. In turn, this could impact the EUR/USD and USD/RON currency pairs. With Romania's fiscal policy under scrutiny, traders will need to monitor the political landscape closely, as further unrest may lead to shifts in exchange rates and overall market sentiment.
