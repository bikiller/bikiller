The GBP/USD pair faces downward pressure, slipping toward the 1.3300 mark during the early Asian session on Monday. The US Dollar (USD) gains strength, benefiting from a flight to safety as tensions escalate in the Middle East. Investors are flocking to the USD as a secure haven, pushing the British Pound (GBP) lower amid the rising geopolitical uncertainty.

This shift in sentiment has created a bearish outlook for GBP/USD, with the pair now trading close to the key 1.3300 level. The ongoing conflict in the region is driving risk-averse behavior, which traditionally boosts demand for the US Dollar. As long as the Middle East crisis persists, the USD is likely to maintain its dominance, putting additional pressure on GBP, which faces its own domestic challenges.
