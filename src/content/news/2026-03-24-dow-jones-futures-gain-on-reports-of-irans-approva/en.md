Dow Jones futures saw a modest 0.9% rise, reaching just above 46,550 during European trading hours on Tuesday. This movement came in advance of the US market opening, fueled by reports of Iran's approval to engage in negotiations. Meanwhile, S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 futures also experienced slight gains of 0.14% and 0.23%, bringing their respective levels to 6,650 and 24,460. 

The positive sentiment in global equities supported the USD against other major currencies, while market participants remained cautious ahead of US data releases. EUR/USD traded near its recent technical levels, as traders evaluated the implications of rising geopolitical developments. With the US market set to open, investors are eyeing potential market shifts driven by these negotiations and upcoming economic indicators.
