The EUR/USD currency pair has fallen below the 1.15 level as market sentiment weakens amid ongoing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. Safe-haven demand for the USD has pushed the exchange rate lower, with the dollar strengthening on the back of risk aversion. This shift in market dynamics has prompted renewed focus on interest rate differentials between the EUR and USD.

As the conflict in the region continues to weigh on investor confidence, the USD has outperformed the EUR, further solidifying its position as a safe-haven currency. The impact of geopolitical instability is evident in EUR/USD's movement, signaling potential further weakness if global risk appetite remains suppressed.
