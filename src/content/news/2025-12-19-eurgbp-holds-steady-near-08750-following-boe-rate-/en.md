The EUR/GBP currency pair is exhibiting a stable performance, trading around 0.8760 in the early European session on Friday. As traders digest this development, the exchange rate remains resilient despite the adjustments in the UK monetary policy.

Simultaneously, expectations for a steady stance from the European Central Bank (ECB) are contributing to the current trading dynamics. With the EUR holding firm against the GBP, market participants are closely monitoring the implications of both central banks' actions. The interplay between these monetary policies is likely to shape future movements in the EUR/GBP exchange rate, highlighting ongoing uncertainty in the forex market.
