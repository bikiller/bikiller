AUD/USD remains in positive territory following two days of losses, with the currency pair trading around 0.6910 during the Asian session on Monday. The pair recently broke above the critical 0.6900 level, marking a significant shift in market sentiment. The move suggests the possibility of further upside momentum, especially with the price testing the nine-day exponential moving average (EMA).

The strength of AUD/USD is underpinned by a weaker USD, as the greenback continues to face headwinds amidst ongoing global economic uncertainty. While the pair has shown resilience, traders will closely watch for a sustained move above the 0.6900 mark, which could open the door for further gains. The next key resistance level for AUD/USD lies near 0.6950, where fresh buying interest could emerge.
