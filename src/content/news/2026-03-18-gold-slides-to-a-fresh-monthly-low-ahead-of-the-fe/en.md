Gold (XAU/USD) is under pressure on Wednesday, hitting a fresh monthly low as the US Dollar (USD) stabilizes ahead of the Federal Reserve's upcoming interest rate decision. The yellow metal is trading with a downside bias, hovering near key support levels. Investors are positioning themselves ahead of the Fed's policy update at 18:00 GMT, with market sentiment leaning towards cautious optimism for a potential hawkish stance.

The exchange rate between gold and the US Dollar reflects broader market uncertainty as traders await further clues on future monetary tightening. At the time of writing, XAU/USD is trading lower by approximately 0.4%, with the price of gold testing support at $1,960. A decisive move below this level could trigger further declines, particularly if the Fed signals additional rate hikes. Meanwhile, the EUR/USD remains steady as markets look to the Fed for clarity.
