The EUR/USD currency pair concluded the week near the 1.1640 level, marking a 0.7% decline as the US Dollar continued to dominate the market. On Friday, the pair fell an additional 0.20%, driven by a mix of economic data from the US that failed to provide a significant boost to the Euro. 

While retail sales data from the European Union surpassed expectations, traders remained focused on the broader dynamics surrounding the USD. The persistent strength of the Dollar has overshadowed Eurozone developments, leaving the exchange rate under pressure and traders cautious as they assess the outlook for both economies.
