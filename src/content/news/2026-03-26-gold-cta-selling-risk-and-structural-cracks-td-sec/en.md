Commodity Trading Advisors (CTAs) are poised to sell Gold unless prices experience a significant rebound in the coming week, warns TD Securities’ Senior Commodity Strategist Daniel Ghali. The yellow metal’s recent downtrend has triggered concerns about further selling pressure, with CTAs becoming increasingly cautious. If Gold fails to stage a strong recovery, this risk of selling could weigh heavily on its performance in the short term.

Gold’s price action has shown structural cracks, with the risk of a deeper decline in play unless the market sees a sharp reversal. This potential move could impact the broader forex market, influencing currency pairs like USD/EUR. Traders are closely watching key technical levels for signs of stabilization. A failure to regain upward momentum could strengthen the USD as risk-off sentiment takes hold, while the outlook for Gold remains precarious.
