The EUR/USD currency pair sees modest gains during the early European session, trading near 1.1520. The Euro (EUR) is benefiting from growing optimism surrounding US-Iran ceasefire talks, which is weighing on the US Dollar (USD). Market sentiment has shifted positively, with investors hopeful that the diplomatic efforts will ease global tensions, providing support for the single currency.

Later today, the US March ISM Services Purchasing Managers Index (PMI) report is expected to influence the pair's direction. Traders will be closely watching this data for clues about the strength of the US economy and potential shifts in the Federal Reserve's monetary policy. As the EUR/USD exchange rate hovers above the key 1.1500 level, any deviation from expectations in the upcoming economic data could lead to increased volatility in the pair.
