The EUR/USD currency pair saw a sharp decline of 0.85% during the European session on Tuesday, dropping to around 1.1585. The pair's downturn comes amid a widespread risk-aversion sentiment in the market, with traders seeking the safety of the US Dollar. As a result, the exchange rate is approaching its three-month low of 1.1575, intensifying pressure on the Euro.

The rally in the USD, driven by global uncertainty, has overshadowed the Euro's performance, pushing the EUR/USD lower. With the pair testing key support levels, a breach of 1.1575 could open the door to further downside, signaling potential weakness for the EUR in the near term. Traders are closely monitoring any changes in risk sentiment as they look to assess future price movements.
