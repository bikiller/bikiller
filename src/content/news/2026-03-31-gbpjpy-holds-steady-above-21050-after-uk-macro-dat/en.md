The GBP/JPY currency pair saw a brief uptick to 211.22 early in the European session on Tuesday, but fresh selling pressure pushed it lower, maintaining its position just above the 210.50 mark. This price action keeps the pair near a multi-week low, reflecting cautious sentiment in the market.

The pound remains under pressure following the release of UK macroeconomic data, which has yet to provide any significant support for the currency. As GBP/JPY holds steady, traders are focused on any potential shifts in sentiment, with the pair closely tracking broader risk appetite and economic developments in both the UK and Japan.
