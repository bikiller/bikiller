EUR/GBP is holding steady around the 0.8680 mark on Monday, with minimal movement observed in early trading. The currency pair is seeing limited volatility as investors adopt a wait-and-see approach amidst growing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. These concerns are adding a layer of uncertainty to the market, overshadowing typical trading activity.

Further caution is building ahead of Germany's upcoming inflation data, which could significantly influence the euro's performance. As traders position themselves for potential shifts in the EUR/USD exchange rate, the current lack of momentum in EUR/GBP reflects broader market hesitance. The pair’s technical levels remain under pressure, as investors remain wary of any economic surprises that could impact the eurozone's economic outlook.
