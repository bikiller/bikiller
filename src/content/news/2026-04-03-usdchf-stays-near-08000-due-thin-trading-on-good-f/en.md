USD/CHF continues to trade near the 0.7980 level after gaining more than 0.5% the previous day. The pair saw solid upward momentum on Thursday, but movement has been limited during the Asian session on Friday. The subdued trading can largely be attributed to thin market conditions, as many investors are off for the Good Friday holiday.

With reduced activity across major markets, the USD/CHF currency pair is expected to experience a lack of volatility in the short term. As a result, traders may find little opportunity for significant price movements until markets return to full capacity next week. The current exchange rate of 0.7980 reflects the cautious sentiment in global forex markets.
