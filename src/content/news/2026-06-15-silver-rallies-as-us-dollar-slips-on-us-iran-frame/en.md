Silver (XAG/USD) is trading around $70.70 on Monday, surging 3.94% on the day as the currency pair extends its rebound. The exchange rate reflects renewed buying interest in precious metals, with silver gaining momentum after recent consolidation. Trading activity shows firm bullish sentiment, as market participants position for continued upside across commodities linked to USD pricing.

Pressure on the US Dollar (USD) has supported the move, as markets react to a tentative US-Iran framework that has eased some geopolitical tensions. Softer USD conditions have improved demand for silver and other commodities, while broader forex pairs such as EUR/USD also reflect shifting risk appetite. Investors are increasingly focused on the upcoming Federal Reserve (Fed) policy meeting, which is shaping expectations across exchange rate markets.

Looking ahead, traders expect heightened volatility in XAG/USD as the Fed decision approaches, with interest rate expectations likely to determine near-term direction. While the silver rally may face profit-taking, underlying support from safe-haven demand and macro uncertainty remains in play. Overall, trading conditions in precious metals and USD-linked currency pairs are likely to remain highly sensitive to policy signals and geopolitical developments.
