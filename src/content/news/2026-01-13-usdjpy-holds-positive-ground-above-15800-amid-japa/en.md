Market sentiment has shifted, favoring the USD as traders react to uncertainties in Japanese governance. The sustained movement above the key level of 158.00 suggests a potential for further gains if these political issues are not resolved, affecting investor confidence in the JPY and providing a favorable backdrop for the USD's performance.
