The EUR/USD currency pair shows increased upside potential, supported by hawkish rhetoric from the European Central Bank (ECB). According to ING’s Francesco Pesole, the ECB's tone has resulted in Euro front-end rates remaining resilient against the downside, particularly in comparison to the US, where Federal Reserve easing is more easily priced in. This has created a stronger foundation for the Euro relative to the US Dollar.

With the ECB’s stance favoring tighter monetary policy, the EUR/USD exchange rate is finding support at key levels. As market participants continue to price in the possibility of a more hawkish ECB, the Euro’s strength against the USD remains intact. This dynamic reflects contrasting central bank policies, with the Fed's potential for further easing keeping the downside pressure on the Dollar.
