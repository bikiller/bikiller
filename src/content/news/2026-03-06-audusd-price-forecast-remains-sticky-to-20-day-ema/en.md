The AUD/USD currency pair is trading 0.55% higher, reaching around 0.7050 during the European session on Friday. This marks a continuation of its movement near the 20-day exponential moving average (EMA), which has held the exchange rate in a tight range for nearly a week. The pair's resilience around this key technical level indicates some underlying support, even as global risk sentiment fluctuates.

The shift in AUD/USD can be attributed to a slight pullback in the US dollar, following mixed economic data from the US and fluctuating Treasury yields. As market participants reassess the outlook for the USD, the Australian dollar has found some strength, maintaining its position against the greenback. Traders will closely monitor upcoming data releases for any signs of further volatility, which could impact the currency pair's near-term direction.
