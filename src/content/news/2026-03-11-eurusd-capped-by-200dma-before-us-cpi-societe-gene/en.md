EUR/USD has remained range-bound, with the exchange rate capped by the 200-day moving average at 1.1676 ahead of the upcoming US Consumer Price Index (CPI) release. Trading activity has been relatively subdued, with the pair consolidating between support at 1.1560 and resistance at 1.1735. The market is positioning itself for potential volatility depending on the CPI outcome.

Option expiries around these technical levels could influence the next move in the EUR/USD pair. The focus is on how US inflation data might impact future Federal Reserve policy and, by extension, the strength of the USD. Traders are watching for any potential breakout beyond these boundaries, which could signal further price action.
