Gold (XAU/USD) currency pair rebounded on Friday, extending gains above the $4,700 level as trading sentiment improved. Market participants reacted to headlines suggesting Iran and the US are preparing to resume second-round talks aimed at ending the ongoing conflict. The move reflects shifting expectations across risk assets and softer demand for the USD as a safe-haven driver.  

At the time of writing, XAU/USD trades at $4,726, marking a 0.47% daily gain as the exchange rate continues to strengthen. Broader forex market flows show mild USD softness, supporting the upside in gold despite easing geopolitical tensions. Investors remain attentive to developments in Iran-US negotiations, which could further influence safe-haven demand and short-term trading direction across major currency pairs.  

Technically, the currency pair holds firm above the $4,700 support zone, keeping bullish momentum intact in the near term. If optimism around diplomatic progress persists, gold may continue to attract inflows even as USD volatility stabilises, reinforcing its role within diversified trading strategies across global forex markets. Market participants will monitor whether sustained moves above this level signal further upside potential in XAU/USD trading sessions.
