The EUR/USD currency pair fell below the key technical level of 1.1650, trading at approximately 1.1635 during the early Asian session on Thursday. This decline is primarily attributed to the strengthening of the US Dollar (USD), driven by heightened tensions in the Middle East that have led to increased demand for safe-haven assets.

As the conflict escalates, market participants are gravitating towards the USD, impacting the EUR (Euro) negatively. The current exchange rate reflects a broader trend of risk aversion in the forex market, suggesting that traders may continue to seek shelter in the dollar amid geopolitical uncertainties, potentially leading to further downward pressure on the Euro in the near term.
