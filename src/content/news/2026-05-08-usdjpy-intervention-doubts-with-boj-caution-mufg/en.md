USD/JPY remains steady despite recent intervention by the Bank of Japan (BoJ). The central bank's efforts, estimated at approximately JPY 10 trillion, have not yet led to a lasting rebound for the Yen. As of now, the exchange rate remains stable, with USD/JPY continuing to trade around current levels. This suggests that the BoJ's intervention has had a limited impact on the broader market dynamics.

The lack of sustained movement in the Yen underscores the challenges faced by the BoJ in reversing the Yen's prolonged weakness. Despite the massive intervention, traders are still cautious, and USD/JPY remains well-supported. This ongoing stability hints that further action may be needed for a significant shift in the currency pair's trajectory. The market's focus will likely stay on future BoJ actions and any shifts in global risk sentiment.
