The Australian Dollar (AUD) has seen notable support as expectations for further tightening by the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) drive investor sentiment. MUFG’s Head of Research, Derek Halpenny, highlights that the AUD is benefitting from the anticipation of additional rate hikes, which is pushing the AUD/USD pair higher. The currency is currently trading near its recent highs, with markets pricing in further tightening as a response to persistent inflationary pressures.

Furthermore, the Australian Dollar has found additional support through rising energy prices, contributing to a positive terms-of-trade impulse. As global oil prices increase, Australia's export-driven economy stands to gain, further strengthening the AUD’s position. This dynamic is especially evident in AUD/JPY and AUD/EUR pairs, where the currency is trading resiliently against its major counterparts. With global energy prices continuing to rise, the AUD’s outlook remains bolstered, and the focus will shift to upcoming RBA decisions for any additional volatility.
