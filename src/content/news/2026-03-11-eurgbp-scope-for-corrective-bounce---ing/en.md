EUR/GBP has dropped approximately 1.5% since the escalation of the Iran conflict, as the British pound (GBP) continues to outperform the euro (EUR). This decline has been driven by a robust GBP exchange rate profile and a resilient equities market, which has kept the currency pair under pressure. The EUR/GBP pair's movement reflects broader market sentiment, where the pound benefits from stronger risk appetite.

Despite the current downward trend, ING's Francesco Pesole suggests that EUR/GBP may be poised for a corrective bounce. The technical outlook indicates potential support levels for the pair, which could encourage a short-term recovery. Traders are closely watching this currency pair as the market digests geopolitical risks and the ongoing economic landscape, with the EUR/GBP exchange rate likely to remain volatile in the near term.
