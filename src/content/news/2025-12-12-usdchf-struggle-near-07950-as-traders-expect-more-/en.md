As market sentiment shifts, the Swiss Franc continues to gain traction, offering a safe-haven alternative amid growing concerns about U.S. monetary policy. With expectations of at least two rate cuts on the horizon, the outlook for the USD remains uncertain, suggesting potential volatility for the USD/CHF pair in the coming months.
