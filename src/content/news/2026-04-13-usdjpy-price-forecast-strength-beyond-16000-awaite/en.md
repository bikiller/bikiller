The USD/JPY currency pair extended its recent upward momentum, opening the week with a bullish gap and climbing to the 159.85 area during the Asian session. Traders remain focused on the technical setup, which signals potential strength beyond the 160.00 level, yet caution persists as intervention fears from Japanese authorities temper any aggressive appreciation in the spot exchange rate. Market participants are weighing short-term momentum against policy risks.  

Trading activity in the broader forex market shows USD strength against major counterparts, while the Japanese yen faces continued pressure. Analysts highlight that technical indicators favor further upside, with momentum supporting a possible push above 160.00 if resistance levels hold. The outlook for USD/JPY suggests a delicate balance between bullish trading opportunities and intervention concerns. Traders eyeing long positions may find entry points near support, while those hedging exposure are closely monitoring price action around key levels.
