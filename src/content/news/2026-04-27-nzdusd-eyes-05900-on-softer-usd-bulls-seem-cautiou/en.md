The NZD/USD pair has seen some buying interest at the start of the week, with traders capitalizing on Friday's rebound from the 200-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) support around 0.5840. As the exchange rate edges higher, it now targets the 0.5900 level, benefiting from a weaker USD. 

Despite this upward momentum, the currency pair's bulls remain cautious as geopolitical tensions, particularly between the US and Iran, continue to weigh on market sentiment. The ongoing uncertainty in global markets could limit further upside potential for the NZD/USD, with traders closely monitoring any developments.
