The USD/CAD currency pair has stabilized near the 1.3800 mark during Monday's Asian trading session, reflecting minimal movement after a modest gain in the previous session. As traders monitor the fluctuating exchange rate, the implications of oil's upward trajectory may put pressure on the USD/CAD pair. A stronger Canadian dollar, fueled by increased oil prices, could lead to a continued consolidation around this critical technical level, impacting future trading strategies and decisions in the forex market.
