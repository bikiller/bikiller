The GBP/USD rose on Friday, buoyed by a shift in risk sentiment following the commencement of US-Iran talks in Pakistan. The exchange rate gained 0.20%, reaching 1.3461 as investors reacted positively to the potential for de-escalation in the Middle East. This optimism outweighed concerns over the latest US inflation report, which showed a slight increase in March, as traders remain confident it’s a temporary spike.

Despite the inflation uptick, market participants are focusing on the geopolitical developments, which have helped sustain risk-on sentiment. The US Dollar's outlook softened slightly, while the British Pound benefited from the positive risk environment. With the exchange rate at 1.3461, traders are watching for further signs of stability in the region, with hopes of a lasting diplomatic resolution between the US and Iran.
