EUR/GBP is testing key support at 0.8700, marking its third consecutive day of decline as the Euro (EUR) struggles against the stronger British Pound (GBP). The currency pair continues to face downward pressure, with bears eyeing this critical level amid overall market optimism. Risk-on sentiment, bolstered by a ceasefire agreement in Iran, has strengthened the Pound, keeping it resilient against the Euro.

The current price action in EUR/GBP highlights the impact of global events on currency markets, particularly in a positive environment for the British currency. The ceasefire news has injected optimism into risk assets, favoring the GBP over the EUR. Traders are now watching the 0.8700 support closely, with a break below this level potentially signaling further downside for the pair in the short term.
