The GBP/USD surged above 1.3300 on Wednesday, climbing over 0.70% as market sentiment improved. Investor risk appetite strengthened after US President Donald Trump’s comments, suggesting a swift exit from Iran. The remarks fueled optimism regarding a potential resolution to the Middle East conflict, driving the Pound higher against the USD. GBP/USD traded well above the 1.3300 mark, recovering from daily lows near 1.3250.

The Pound’s rally came amid speculation of easing geopolitical tensions, giving traders confidence. The currency pair’s upward move reflects market optimism, with GBP/USD maintaining its position above key technical levels. With the US Dollar on the back foot, the Pound capitalized on the shift in risk sentiment, signaling potential for further gains if the positive sentiment persists in the coming sessions.
