The NZD/USD pair recovered from early losses, finding support near the 0.5950-0.5945 range during Monday's Asian session. This pullback comes after the pair faced downward pressure but managed to attract buyers around the lower boundary of a more than week-old range. Despite this recovery, the exchange rate remains below the critical 0.6000 psychological level.

The modest pullback in the USD contributed to the NZD/USD's recovery, but the broader risk-aversion sentiment in global markets has kept the pair from reclaiming the 0.6000 mark. With market sentiment shifting, traders will likely keep an eye on further movements in the USD as well as any shifts in risk appetite to gauge the next direction for the currency pair.
