The US Dollar Index (DXY), which gauges the performance of the USD against a basket of six major currencies, has stabilized around the 99.00 mark following a slight retreat in the previous session. As traders await upcoming inflation data, the DXY's stability suggests a cautious sentiment in the market, reflecting ongoing concerns about economic trends.

In the early hours of Tuesday, the exchange rate of the DXY remains critical as market participants analyze potential impacts on currency pairs, particularly USD/EUR and USD/JPY. The impending inflation figures could shape future trading strategies, influencing investor confidence and the dollar's trajectory in the coming days.
