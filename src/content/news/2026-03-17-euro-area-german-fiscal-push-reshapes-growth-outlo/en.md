Societe Generale analysts highlight the impact of Germany's fiscal reforms, including the restructured debt brake and the approved budgets for 2025-2026, which will significantly boost public spending. These measures are expected to support Germany's economic growth, potentially strengthening the EUR against its major counterparts. The increased fiscal spending could shift investor sentiment, influencing the EUR/USD exchange rate and offering a short-term lift to the currency.

The German government's fiscal push is likely to provide a positive ripple effect across the Eurozone, enhancing market confidence in the Euro. With German growth expectations on the rise, the EUR may experience upward momentum in the forex markets, especially against the USD. Traders will be watching key technical levels, with the EUR/USD pair potentially testing resistance near 1.0800 as the market digests these fiscal shifts. The long-term effects on the Eurozone’s economic landscape could further solidify the EUR's position in the global forex market.
