The EUR/USD currency pair struggled to gain momentum on Monday, as traders weighed lingering uncertainties surrounding US-Iran peace talks. The Euro pared earlier advances against the US Dollar, with the exchange rate hovering near recent intraday highs. Investors remain cautious, keeping a floor under the USD amid limited clarity on geopolitical developments that could influence global risk sentiment.  

Market participants noted that technical levels around 1.0950 provided initial resistance for the Euro, while support near 1.0900 continues to anchor trading. The pair’s muted movement reflects hesitancy among traders to commit amid fragile sentiment, with any shift in the US-Iran negotiations likely to spark sharp reactions. Liquidity remains moderate, and trading volumes have yet to signal a sustained directional trend.  

Analysts suggest that the outlook for EUR/USD remains closely tied to broader risk appetite and geopolitical updates. A breakthrough in talks could relieve pressure on the Greenback, offering the Euro room to regain lost ground, whereas prolonged stalemate may reinforce defensive USD positioning. For now, currency markets are treading cautiously, with the exchange rate oscillating within a narrow range as traders await clearer cues.
