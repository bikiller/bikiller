The USD/JPY currency pair is exhibiting a negative bias as it navigates through intraday losses. As the European session approaches on Tuesday, the exchange rate reflects a trend influenced by a positive sentiment in equity markets, which typically dampens the appeal of safe-haven currencies like the Japanese Yen (JPY). 

Market participants are also weighing potential interest rate hikes by the Bank of Japan (BoJ), which could serve to limit further declines in the JPY. This backdrop creates a complex trading environment where the interplay of risk appetite and monetary policy expectations could impact the USD/JPY exchange rate in the near term.
