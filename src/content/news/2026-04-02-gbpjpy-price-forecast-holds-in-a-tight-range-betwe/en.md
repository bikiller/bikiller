GBP/JPY trades cautiously on Thursday, maintaining a slight downside bias as the pair struggles to find momentum. The exchange rate hovers within a tight range, held between the 50-day and 100-day simple moving averages (SMAs). Despite a minor dip, there has been little follow-through selling, with the pair recovering part of its earlier losses as market volatility keeps traders on edge.

Market sentiment remains fragile, with the British pound facing pressure amid ongoing uncertainty. The lack of significant movement in GBP/JPY could reflect a broader market indecision, as traders weigh potential economic developments from both the UK and Japan. While the pair remains trapped in its current range, any strong breakouts could signal more decisive directional shifts.
