The USD/JPY currency pair is nearing a critical point in its multi-year trading range, with the exchange rate pressing up against resistance at 159.90. Analysts at Societe Generale have pointed out that this level is crucial for determining the next move in the market. Should the pair break through this barrier, a bullish momentum could follow, potentially signaling a continuation of the yen’s weakness.

On the downside, key support is located around the 50-day moving average near 156.50. This level is vital for traders to monitor as it could mark a reversal if breached. With the USD/JPY testing these important technical levels, traders are on edge, watching for any signs of breakout that could lead to increased volatility in the currency markets.
