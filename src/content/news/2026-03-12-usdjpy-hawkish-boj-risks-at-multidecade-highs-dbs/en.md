The USD/JPY exchange rate is testing the 159–160 resistance zone following the recent surge triggered by Operation Epic Fury. DBS Group Research’s Philip Wee advises caution, noting that the pair’s current level poses risks as the market awaits the Bank of Japan's policy stance on March 19. The pair's movement suggests heightened sensitivity to potential BOJ actions.

Wee anticipates a hawkish hold from the BOJ, with the central bank likely to emphasize its differentiation between temporary supply-driven inflation and sustained demand pressures. This approach could affect market expectations, influencing the USD/JPY pair’s outlook in the coming days.
