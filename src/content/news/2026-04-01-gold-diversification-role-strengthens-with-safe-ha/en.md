HSBC analysts Willem Sels and Lucia Ku maintain an optimistic six-month outlook on gold, continuing with an Overweight position. They note that recent market headwinds are expected to be short-lived, with strong fundamentals still in place. As a result, gold is expected to play a key role as a safe-haven asset, especially amid ongoing global uncertainties.

Despite recent volatility in currency markets, including fluctuations in the USD/EUR exchange rate, gold has maintained its strength, supported by increased demand for diversification. This reinforces its position as a go-to asset during times of market stress, with investors looking for stability amid concerns over inflation and geopolitical risks.
