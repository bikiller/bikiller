The AUD/JPY currency pair is experiencing a strong recovery, climbing 0.38% on Tuesday despite the prevailing risk aversion in global markets due to escalating tensions in the Middle East. The exchange rate is currently trading at 111.62, nearing its year-to-date high around 112.00. The Aussie Dollar's advance comes as a result of hawkish remarks from Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) Governor Bullock, boosting market optimism.

As the Australian Dollar continues to gain momentum, traders are closely monitoring key levels, with 112.00 now a critical resistance zone for AUD/JPY. The ongoing geopolitical tensions have created an undercurrent of caution, but the bullish sentiment on the Aussie, driven by stronger-than-expected central bank signals, has provided support to the pair. If the uptrend persists, traders may see further challenges for the pair near this technical resistance.
