The currency pair's recent performance reflects a lack of bullish momentum for the euro, as traders assess the implications of upcoming economic indicators. With the USD maintaining its strength, the market remains focused on how the German inflation data will impact the overall sentiment towards the euro. As trading unfolds, the interplay of these factors will be key in determining the next direction for EUR/USD.
