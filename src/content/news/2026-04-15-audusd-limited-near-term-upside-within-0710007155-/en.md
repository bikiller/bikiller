UOB strategists Quek Ser Leang and Lee Sue Ann maintain a cautiously optimistic outlook on the AUD/USD currency pair. After the pair recently rose to 0.7147, they foresee limited short-term upside, with the exchange rate expected to fluctuate between 0.7100 and 0.7155. A move towards the upper range could occur if buying momentum strengthens.

The near-term outlook suggests that the Australian Dollar may face resistance as it tests the 0.7155 level. While the pair's upward potential remains constrained within this range, a break above could signal further gains. Traders will be closely watching for any shifts in momentum that might push the exchange rate toward the next resistance zone.
