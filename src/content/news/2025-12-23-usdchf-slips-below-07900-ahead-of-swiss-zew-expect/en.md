The USD/CHF currency pair has continued its downward trend, slipping below the key level of 0.7900 during the Asian trading session on Tuesday. Market dynamics suggest that uncertainty surrounding economic forecasts and geopolitical factors are contributing to the downward movement of the USD/CHF. As the pair hovers around this critical technical level, traders will be closely monitoring upcoming data releases for potential volatility and directional cues in the exchange rate.
