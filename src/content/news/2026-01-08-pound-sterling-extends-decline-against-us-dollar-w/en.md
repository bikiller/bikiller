The Pound Sterling (GBP) has continued its downward trajectory against the US Dollar (USD) for the third consecutive day, trading around the exchange rate of 1.3450 during Thursday's European session. This persistent decline reflects ongoing market concerns about the UK economy amid a backdrop of rising inflation and interest rate uncertainty.

Traders are now closely monitoring the upcoming US Non-Farm Payrolls (NFP) report, which could significantly influence the USD's strength and overall market sentiment. A robust NFP report may further bolster the USD, putting additional pressure on the GBP as it navigates this challenging trading environment. The shift in focus to US labor data highlights the interconnected nature of currency movements and economic indicators.
