NZD/USD has reversed its recent rally, trading around 0.6010 during the Asian session on Wednesday, after reaching six-month highs earlier this week. The currency pair, which had been on a strong upward trend since January 16, is now experiencing a pullback as traders adjust their positions.

The decline comes as the US Dollar (USD) recovers from significant losses, having fallen by more than 1% in the previous session. Market participants are positioning themselves ahead of the upcoming Federal Reserve (Fed) policy decision, which is adding uncertainty to market sentiment and influencing currency movements.
