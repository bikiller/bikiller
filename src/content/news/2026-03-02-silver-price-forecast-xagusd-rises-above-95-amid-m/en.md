Silver (XAG/USD) surged above the $95 mark on Monday, trading at $95.30, marking a 1.68% gain for the day. The increase comes amid heightened geopolitical tensions in the Middle East, which have prompted a surge in demand for safe-haven assets like silver. As investors seek refuge from market uncertainty, silver's appeal grows in the face of global instability.

The XAG/USD exchange rate has benefited from a shift in risk sentiment, with broader market volatility pushing traders to hedge against potential economic fallout. This upward movement is likely to continue, especially as ongoing geopolitical conflicts keep investors cautious. Silver’s performance is drawing attention from both retail and institutional traders, as it competes with other safe-haven assets like gold in times of crisis.
