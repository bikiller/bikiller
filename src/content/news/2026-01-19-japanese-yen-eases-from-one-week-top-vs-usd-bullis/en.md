As the currency pair approached key technical levels, bullish potential for the Yen appeared intact. While the exchange rate showed resilience, the ongoing market dynamics suggest traders should remain vigilant, as fluctuations could arise from upcoming economic data releases and geopolitical developments. The JPY's recent performance highlights its role as a safe haven amidst a fluctuating market landscape.
