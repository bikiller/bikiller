The EUR/USD currency pair is holding steady above the 1.1770 mark, trading at 1.1782 as of Friday. The Euro remains almost unchanged against the US Dollar, with traders awaiting critical developments from US-Iran peace talks. These talks are expected to influence broader market sentiment, especially regarding geopolitical tensions and potential changes in US foreign policy.

The exchange rate's tight range reflects cautious trading in anticipation of any significant news that could shift market sentiment. With the 1.1770 level providing immediate support, a break below this could signal further downside for the EUR. However, the pair's resilience above this level suggests a wait-and-see approach, with traders focusing on potential developments from the ongoing talks.
