The USD/JPY currency pair is holding steady near the 160.00 level during Friday’s European session, showing limited movement as traders anticipate key US economic data. Market participants are focused on the May Nonfarm Payrolls (NFP) report, scheduled for release at 12:30 GMT, which is expected to influence USD momentum and set the tone for broader forex trading. Exchange rate fluctuations remain subdued as investors weigh potential impacts on interest rate expectations.

Technical trading indicates the pair is consolidating within a narrow range, with support near 159.80 and resistance just above 160.20. This range-bound behavior reflects cautious sentiment ahead of the NFP release, as USD strength or weakness could prompt rapid shifts against the JPY. Analysts suggest that any surprise in employment figures may trigger a breakout from this tight corridor, affecting USD/JPY positioning and broader currency markets.

The broader forex landscape shows mixed dynamics, with USD sentiment influenced by recent economic releases and global risk appetite. Investors are also monitoring EUR/USD and other major pairs for cross-market implications. A stronger-than-expected NFP could reinforce USD gains, pressuring safe-haven currencies like the JPY, while weaker data may bolster risk-on flows, impacting trading strategies across the FX market.
