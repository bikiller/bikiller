The resilience of the USD is influencing trading dynamics and affecting the exchange rate of gold. As investors weigh economic data and central bank policies, the interplay between gold and the USD remains crucial for market sentiment. With gold consolidating near these levels, traders will be closely monitoring any further developments that could impact this key currency pair.
