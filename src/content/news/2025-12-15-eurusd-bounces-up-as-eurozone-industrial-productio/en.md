EUR/USD has made a notable recovery during Monday's European session, bouncing back to 1.1745 after earlier losses. The positive economic data has invigorated trading in the Eurozone, driving demand for the euro as investors seek to capitalize on the currency's momentum. As EUR/USD hovers near critical technical levels, traders will be closely monitoring the exchange rate for further signs of strength or resistance in the coming sessions. The performance of this currency pair remains pivotal as global markets react to ongoing economic developments.
