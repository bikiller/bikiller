The USD/CHF currency pair is trading lower around 0.7870 on Monday, reflecting a decline of 0.12%. The US Dollar (USD) has softened following a brief reduction in geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. As concerns over the region ease, risk sentiment has improved, leading to a pullback in the USD. 

Market participants are now closely monitoring the ongoing geopolitical developments, which could further influence the exchange rate of USD/CHF. A stable or improving situation in the Middle East may provide the Swiss Franc (CHF) with additional support, potentially pushing the pair lower. The USD remains sensitive to global risk factors, with any shifts in sentiment likely to impact its strength in the near term.
