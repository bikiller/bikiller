Strong Australian jobs data has led to heightened expectations for rate hikes, significantly impacting the AUD/USD exchange rate. Despite the Australian dollar's resilience against other currencies, ING's FX analyst Francesco Pesole warns that these rate hike anticipations may be overly optimistic, especially in light of current inflation trends. 

As traders react to the robust employment figures, the AUD remains stable on crosses, but the potential disconnect between rate expectations and inflation realities could leave the AUD/USD vulnerable in the short term. The market will need to monitor upcoming economic indicators closely to gauge the sustainability of the Australian dollar's strength against the USD and EUR.
