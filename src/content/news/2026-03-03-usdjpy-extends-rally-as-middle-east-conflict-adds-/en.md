The USD/JPY currency pair extended its upward momentum on Tuesday, rising approximately 0.15% and approaching the key exchange rate of 157.60. This increase follows a substantial rally from the previous week, highlighting the ongoing strength of the US dollar against the yen.

Market analysts attribute the yen's weakness to the escalating Middle East conflict, which has added a layer of uncertainty in global markets. As traders react to geopolitical tensions, the demand for the USD remains robust, supporting its continued ascent against the Japanese currency. The trading environment suggests that further fluctuations in this pair could be expected as investors monitor developments in the region.
