Gold (XAU/USD) maintained its position above the critical $4,750 mark during the Asian session on Wednesday, showing modest intraday gains. This rebound comes after a significant drop that pushed prices to a one-week low around the $4,669-$4,668 region. The softer USD played a key role in supporting gold's price action as traders adjusted their positions following the previous day's losses.

Despite the recovery, the lack of strong bullish conviction raises concerns about the sustainability of this rally. Market participants are closely monitoring the exchange rate movements of the USD and other currencies, particularly the EUR, as they influence gold trading dynamics. As the week progresses, traders will look for further signals that could define gold's trajectory in the wake of the recent volatility.
