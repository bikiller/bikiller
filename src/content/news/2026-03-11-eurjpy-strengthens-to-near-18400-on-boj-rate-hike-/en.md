EUR/JPY surged to nearly 183.90 during the early European session on Wednesday, as the Japanese Yen (JPY) weakened against the Euro (EUR). The movement comes amid growing uncertainty about the Bank of Japan's (BoJ) approach to tightening monetary policy, with traders questioning the speed at which the BoJ may normalize interest rates.

The market's doubt about the BoJ's rate hike plans has led to a shift in the EUR/JPY exchange rate, boosting the Euro. As the JPY softens, the currency pair finds resistance near the 184.00 level, highlighting the ongoing volatility in forex markets. Traders remain cautious, awaiting further clues on Japan’s future monetary policy stance.
