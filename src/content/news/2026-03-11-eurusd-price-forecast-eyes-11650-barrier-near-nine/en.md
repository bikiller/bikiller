EUR/USD has recovered from its recent losses, trading around 1.1630 during the Asian session on Wednesday. After a pullback, the currency pair is eyeing the key 1.1650 resistance level, which lies near the nine-day exponential moving average (EMA). Despite this bounce, the overall trend remains bearish, with EUR/USD continuing to trade within a descending channel pattern.

The pair's technical outlook indicates that downward pressure persists as the exchange rate struggles to break through resistance levels. Market sentiment remains cautious, as traders focus on the broader USD strength and potential shifts in economic indicators. The EUR/USD remains vulnerable to further declines, with support levels still distant, leaving the 1.1650 barrier as a crucial point to watch in the near term.
