The EUR/USD currency pair retraced a significant portion of its earlier gains, trading near 1.1415 during European hours on Monday. After rising in early trading, the pair faced downward pressure as the US Dollar (USD) bounced back, recovering around half of its previous losses. The exchange rate hovered around the 1.1415 level, showing limited movement as market participants awaited further direction.

As the USD strengthened, investors turned their focus to upcoming central bank policies, with the Federal Reserve and European Central Bank in the spotlight. Market expectations of potential policy shifts from both the Fed and the ECB are keeping traders cautious, as any changes in interest rate outlooks could significantly influence the EUR/USD exchange rate in the coming sessions.
