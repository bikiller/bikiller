GBP/USD held steady near the 1.3460 level on Monday as traders reacted to disappointing talks between Iran and the US. The discussions, which failed to meet market expectations, sparked a response from the White House, dampening investor sentiment. The lack of progress has left traders cautious, contributing to the currency pair’s muted performance.

Meanwhile, market attention shifted to recent reports indicating that Iran might be willing to scale back its uranium enrichment activities, a key demand from the US. This shift could have far-reaching implications for ongoing geopolitical tensions. The news, while somewhat positive, failed to generate any significant upside for the Pound, as investors remain wary of global uncertainties affecting the broader forex market.
