The recent diplomatic rift between China and Japan is raising concerns over the potential impacts on tourism and trade, as highlighted by Commerzbank's FX analyst Volkmar Baur. This discord was notably triggered when Chinese President Xi Jinping broke with tradition by withholding congratulations to Japan's new Prime Minister, Sanae Takaichi, following her election.

Market reactions have been observed in the JPY exchange rates, as traders assess the implications of strained relations. The USD/JPY pair is closely watched, with fluctuations reflecting investor sentiment towards the geopolitical landscape. Should tensions escalate, further volatility in the currency pair may occur, affecting broader trading strategies against major currencies like EUR and USD.
