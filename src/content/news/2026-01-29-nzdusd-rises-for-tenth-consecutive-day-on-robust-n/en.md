The NZD/USD currency pair continued its upward momentum on Thursday, trading around 0.6075, reflecting a 0.40% gain for the day. This marks the tenth consecutive day of gains, driven by strong trade data from New Zealand. The robust economic figures have fueled positive sentiment towards the Kiwi dollar, helping it maintain its position against the USD.

With the pair's consistent rise, the New Zealand dollar has seen impressive stability, supported by favorable trade balances and an overall strengthening of the local economy. Traders are closely watching the pair as it approaches key technical levels, with further movement dependent on upcoming economic reports and any shifts in broader market sentiment towards the USD.
