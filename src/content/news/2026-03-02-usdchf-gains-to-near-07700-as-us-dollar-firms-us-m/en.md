The USD/CHF currency pair saw notable gains during early European trading on Monday, reaching a high of 0.7695. The US dollar firmed up against the Swiss franc as market participants awaited key economic data. A stronger greenback, fueled by recent risk-on sentiment, helped drive the exchange rate higher, testing important technical levels.

Attention now shifts to the US Manufacturing PMI, with investors bracing for potential volatility. A positive PMI reading could bolster the USD further, while a weaker-than-expected figure might lead to some profit-taking in the currency pair. As the market digests this data, the USD/CHF exchange rate is likely to remain sensitive to broader economic signals.
