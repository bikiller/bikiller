EUR/JPY rebounded during the Asian trading session on Monday, climbing back above the 182.50 level. After experiencing two consecutive days of losses, the currency pair gained momentum, trading near 182.60. This movement came amid growing optimism surrounding a potential resolution of the ongoing conflict in Iran, boosting market sentiment.

The positive sentiment helped the EUR/JPY exchange rate recover, as investors showed renewed interest in risk assets. The rise above the key 182.50 mark signals potential for further upward movement, especially if the market continues to price in a favorable geopolitical outcome. The market’s focus remains on global events, with the USD and EUR also being influenced by broader risk appetite.
