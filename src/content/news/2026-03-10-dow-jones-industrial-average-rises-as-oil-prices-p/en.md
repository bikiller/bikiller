The Dow Jones Industrial Average surged past 48,000 on Tuesday, continuing Monday’s sharp rebound. The index's gains came as oil prices fell sharply, fueling optimism that pressure on Iran might ease. This sentiment helped support the broader equity market, with the S&P 500 and Nasdaq Composite also posting significant advances. The movement in global stocks spurred a rise in risk appetite, benefiting currencies such as the EUR/USD.

In forex markets, the USD strengthened against several major pairs as the positive equity sentiment weighed on safe-haven demand. The EUR/USD saw slight gains, reflecting a shift towards riskier assets. Meanwhile, oil’s dramatic drop, along with expectations of geopolitical easing, contributed to shifts in energy-linked currencies like the CAD and NOK. The market's volatility remains high, with traders eyeing potential shifts in oil dynamics and broader geopolitical developments.
