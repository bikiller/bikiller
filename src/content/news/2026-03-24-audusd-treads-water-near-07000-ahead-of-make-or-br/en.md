The AUD/USD currency pair is flat for the week, trading around 0.7000 after experiencing significant volatility. The exchange rate swung from a high above 0.7120 to a low of 0.6910, reflecting a turbulent few sessions. The Australian dollar's movement is largely dictated by shifting market sentiment and broader risk appetite.

As the pair consolidates near the 0.7000 mark, traders are awaiting critical US inflation data, with the upcoming CPI report expected to drive further price action. A stronger-than-expected inflation reading could boost the USD, potentially pushing AUD/USD lower, while softer data may offer the Australian dollar some relief. The market remains on edge as the CPI print approaches.
