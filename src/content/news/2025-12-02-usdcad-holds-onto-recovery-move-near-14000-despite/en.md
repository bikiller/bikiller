Traders remain attentive to the implications of the Fed's stance, as it could influence the exchange rate dynamics between the USD and CAD. The continued resilience of USD/CAD near the 1.4000 mark suggests that market participants are weighing broader economic signals while navigating through volatility in the forex market.
