EUR/USD continued its downward trajectory on Thursday, extending losses for a third consecutive day. The pair fell as the US Dollar (USD) strengthened amid heightened geopolitical tensions surrounding the escalating US-Iran conflict. With traders flocking to safe-haven assets, the USD remains firmly supported, putting pressure on the Euro (EUR) across global markets.

As of the latest trading session, the EUR/USD exchange rate stands at 1.0735, marking a significant drop from earlier in the week. The ongoing conflict has spurred a surge in demand for the US Dollar, with markets responding to the risk-off sentiment. With geopolitical uncertainty likely to persist, the pair is expected to remain under pressure, potentially testing further support levels in the coming sessions.
