The improving sentiment in risk assets has contributed to gold's resilience against the backdrop of shifting Fed policies. As the market digests these developments, the exchange rate of gold continues to reflect investor positioning amid fluctuating economic indicators and ongoing assessments of the USD's strength against other currencies.
