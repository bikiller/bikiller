GBP/JPY continues its upward trajectory, marking a fifth consecutive day of gains. The currency pair is edging closer to a breakout above the critical resistance zone between 214.00 and 215.00. As the Japanese Yen (JPY) remains under pressure against major currencies, including the British Pound (GBP), GBP/JPY's bullish momentum persists. 

The Yen's weakness is largely driven by rising oil prices, which continue to impact Japan, a major net importer. This has caused the JPY to soften against most major peers, including the USD and EUR. Traders are closely monitoring the 214.00-215.00 resistance area for a potential breakout, which could pave the way for further upside in GBP/JPY.
