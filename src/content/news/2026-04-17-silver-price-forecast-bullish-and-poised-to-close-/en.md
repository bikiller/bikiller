Silver (XAG/USD) surged over 4% on Friday, pushing the price back above $81.00 per troy ounce. As silver continues to outperform, the implications for the broader forex market are clear: the USD's vulnerability may drive additional demand for precious metals. Traders will be closely watching silver's ability to hold these gains in the coming sessions.
