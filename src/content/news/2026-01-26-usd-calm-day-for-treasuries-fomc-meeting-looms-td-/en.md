Treasury yields remained steady on Tuesday, with US rates finding support ahead of the upcoming Federal Reserve meeting. The USD saw limited movement as traders await the FOMC's decision, with expectations that the central bank will maintain its current policy stance. This cautious environment led to subdued trading activity across the broader forex market.

The EUR/USD exchange rate held relatively steady, with the pair staying close to recent levels amid the calm in US Treasuries. Despite this, market participants are closely watching for any potential shifts in Fed rhetoric during the meeting, which could affect USD sentiment. The overall lack of volatility underscores the market's anticipation of future policy moves, keeping trading volumes light.
