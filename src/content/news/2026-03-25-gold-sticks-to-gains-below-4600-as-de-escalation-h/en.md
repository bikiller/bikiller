Gold (XAU/USD) maintains a positive outlook during the first half of the European session, holding steady below the weekly high of $4,600 reached earlier on Wednesday. Traders have been adjusting their expectations regarding future rate hikes, as de-escalation hopes soften the outlook for aggressive monetary tightening. With a stable gold price below $4,600, attention is shifting to broader market factors, including the ongoing strength of the USD and potential shifts in global risk sentiment.
