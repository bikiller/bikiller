Gold prices are climbing amid rising geopolitical tensions between the US, Israel, and Iran, with investors flocking to the precious metal as a safe haven. As of today, gold is trading at $1,960 per ounce, reflecting a 1.2% gain. The heightened risk environment is driving demand for assets perceived as stable, such as gold, while also impacting currency pairs like USD/EUR, as the USD weakens.

The ongoing conflict in the Middle East has intensified market uncertainty, causing shifts in global risk sentiment. With the US dollar under pressure, investors are increasingly seeking refuge in gold, bolstering its upward momentum. Technically, gold has breached key resistance levels at $1,950, suggesting further gains are possible if tensions continue to rise. This movement is likely to keep market participants cautious, especially within the USD and EUR currency pairs.
