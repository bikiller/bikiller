Silver (XAG/USD) extended its rally for the sixth consecutive day, trading near $86.80 per troy ounce during Wednesday’s Asian session. The currency pair has shown resilience amid rising industrial demand, as investors factor in supply pressures and ongoing market volatility. Traders are eyeing the $87.00 level, which could serve as a short-term resistance if buying momentum persists.

The bullish trend in XAG/USD comes as USD exchange rates remain somewhat mixed against major currencies like EUR and JPY. This dynamic is influencing traders’ hedging strategies, with precious metals often acting as a safe-haven amid currency fluctuations. Market participants are increasingly watching industrial demand signals, which are driving stronger trading volumes and contributing to the ongoing price appreciation.

Analysts suggest that continued demand from manufacturing and technology sectors could sustain silver’s upward trajectory. If XAG/USD breaks above $87.00, it may attract additional speculative interest, potentially boosting liquidity across forex markets. Meanwhile, volatility in USD and other key currencies could amplify short-term swings, keeping traders attentive to both macroeconomic data and technical support levels.
