The Euro (EUR) slipped below the 1.1750 mark against the US Dollar (USD) on Tuesday, as traders weighed mixed Economic Sentiment data alongside rising geopolitical tensions. The currency pair had earlier faced resistance near the 1.1790 level on Monday, prompting renewed selling pressure and a downward shift in the EUR/USD exchange rate.

Market participants highlighted that uneven economic indicators, combined with global uncertainties, are prompting cautious trading. Analysts note that investor sentiment remains fragile, with the Euro struggling to maintain gains despite intermittent support. The reaction underscores the sensitivity of the currency pair to both economic data and geopolitical developments.

Technically, EUR/USD continues to hover below key resistance zones, suggesting potential for further weakness if the broader market environment does not stabilize. Traders are watching the 1.1750 floor closely, while positioning in USD strengthens as safe-haven flows gain traction. The current movement hints at heightened volatility, emphasizing the need for careful risk management in forex portfolios.
