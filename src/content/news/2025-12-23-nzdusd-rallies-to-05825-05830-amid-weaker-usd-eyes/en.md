Market participants are closely watching this upward momentum, particularly in light of upcoming US economic data that could influence the USD's performance. If the NZD/USD continues on this trajectory, it could signal a potential break through recent resistance levels, indicating a shift in market sentiment towards the New Zealand dollar amid broader trading dynamics.
