USD/TWD has been steadily declining, with the pair finding support from Taiwan's strong equity market, robust foreign inflows, and healthy tech exports. These factors have kept the Taiwan Dollar (TWD) in a favorable position, as it recouples with the ongoing technology cycle. As a result, the exchange rate has been moving lower, with traders observing a potential reversal risk based on the chart pattern.

Technical analysis from OCBC strategists Sim Moh Siong and Christopher Wong points to a falling wedge formation in USD/TWD, suggesting the possibility of a reversal. The pair’s price action indicates that the Taiwan Dollar remains fundamentally supported by the country's thriving tech sector. Investors should keep an eye on this trend, as any shift in the tech cycle or capital flows could impact the currency pair's future direction.
