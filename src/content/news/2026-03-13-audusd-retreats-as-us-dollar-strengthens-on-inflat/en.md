AUD/USD is trading lower at around 0.7040, down 0.46% on the day, following a high of 0.7187 earlier this week. The pullback comes as the US Dollar strengthens on growing inflation concerns, overshadowing mixed economic data. Risk sentiment in the financial markets has also deteriorated, contributing to the pressure on the Australian Dollar.

The USD's rise stems from renewed fears over inflation, with investors seeking safe-haven assets. This has led to a reversal in the AUD/USD exchange rate, despite the release of mixed data that typically would have offered some support. As markets react to these developments, the focus remains on how inflationary pressures could influence future monetary policy decisions.
