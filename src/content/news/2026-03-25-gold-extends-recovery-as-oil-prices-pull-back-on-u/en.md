Gold (XAU/USD) continues its recovery on Wednesday after hitting a four-month low earlier this week. As the market rebounded, early buyers seized the opportunity, pushing the price higher following a sharp selloff. Gold’s recovery comes amid a pullback in oil prices, with market optimism fueled by hopes of a US-Iran ceasefire. At the time of writing, XAU/USD has gained ground as investors look for safe-haven assets.

The pullback in oil prices, driven by geopolitical developments, has provided support for gold, which is traditionally seen as a hedge against uncertainty. The current price movement in gold has also influenced the broader forex market, with the USD facing pressure as risk sentiment improves. Traders are closely monitoring any further news regarding the US-Iran situation, which could shift market sentiment and influence the EUR/USD and USD/JPY exchange rates in the coming days.
