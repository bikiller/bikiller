The USD/JPY currency pair is trading 0.5% higher at around 157.00 during Monday's European session, closing in on its monthly high of 157.30. The pair has shown resilience as the US Dollar continues to outperform its global counterparts, driven by geopolitical tensions surrounding the ongoing conflict between the United States, Israel, and Iran.

The strengthening USD is boosting the exchange rate, highlighting the dollar's safe-haven status in times of uncertainty. As traders monitor developments in the Middle East, further upside potential for USD/JPY remains on the horizon, with the 157.30 level serving as a key resistance point. The pair's performance will depend on the broader market sentiment and any shifts in US economic data.
