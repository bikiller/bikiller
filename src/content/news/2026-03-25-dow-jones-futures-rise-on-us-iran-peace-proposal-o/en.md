Dow Jones futures are up 0.7%, approaching 46,750 during European trading hours ahead of the US market open on Wednesday. Investor sentiment is buoyed by optimism surrounding a potential US-Iran peace proposal, helping to drive the positive momentum in global equity markets. The gains suggest a calm before the official US session kicks off, with the broader market set for further volatility depending on the announcement.

Meanwhile, S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 futures saw smaller gains, rising by 0.6% and 0.63%, respectively, to 6,650 and 24,360. The movement across major indices reflects cautious optimism, with the US Dollar (USD) holding steady in currency trading. Currency pairs like EUR/USD and USD/JPY could see fluctuations as traders react to any developments in geopolitical negotiations, potentially impacting the broader exchange rate environment.
