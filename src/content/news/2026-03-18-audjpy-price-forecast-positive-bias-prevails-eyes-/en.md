AUD/JPY trades modestly higher around 113.00 in early European trading on Wednesday, supported by a positive outlook for the Australian Dollar. The pair's gains come as the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) raised interest rates, maintaining a hawkish stance. This move boosts investor confidence in the AUD, sending the currency higher against the Japanese Yen.

With the exchange rate now holding near 113.00, traders eye potential resistance around 113.50. If the AUD/JPY manages to break this level, further gains could be expected, pushing the pair towards 114.00. The RBA’s continued tightening suggests a bullish bias for the Australian Dollar, while the Japanese Yen remains under pressure amid ongoing global economic uncertainties.
