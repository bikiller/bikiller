The US Dollar Index (DXY) continues its upward momentum, nearing the 100.00 mark, trading at approximately 99.80 during early European hours on Monday. This marks a second consecutive session of gains, driven by the strengthening of the Federal Reserve's hawkish stance. The index, which tracks the USD against six major currencies, reflects growing market expectations for tighter monetary policy.

The dollar's rise has been particularly noticeable against the EUR, with the EUR/USD pair dipping lower as investors adjust to the Fed's more aggressive outlook. As the central bank signals potential rate hikes, demand for the USD increases, pushing its value higher in the forex market. Traders will be closely monitoring any further comments from the Fed for clues on future policy moves.
