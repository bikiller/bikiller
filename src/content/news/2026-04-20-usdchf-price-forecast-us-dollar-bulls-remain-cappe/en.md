The USD/CHF currency pair saw a bounce from 0.7775 lows on Friday, driven by short-term demand for the US Dollar. However, recovery efforts stalled on Monday as the exchange rate remained capped below the key 0.7845 resistance level. This price action suggests that bearish momentum persists, with the immediate trend continuing to favor sellers unless the pair can break above this critical threshold.

Despite attempts to recover, the USD remains under pressure, struggling to gain sustained traction against the Swiss Franc. The inability to clear the 0.7845 level keeps traders cautious, with the exchange rate at risk of further declines toward support levels near 0.7775. If the USD fails to reclaim the 0.7845 mark, downside risks could dominate in the near term.
