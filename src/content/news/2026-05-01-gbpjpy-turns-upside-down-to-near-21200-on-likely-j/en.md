The GBP/JPY currency pair reversed its earlier gains during Friday’s European trading session, dipping to around 212.00. Initially buoyed by positive momentum, the pair came under pressure as market sentiment shifted, likely influenced by speculation surrounding potential Japanese intervention. The yen’s strength weighed on the exchange rate, pushing the pair lower after hitting intraday highs earlier in the session.

Traders are now closely monitoring the 212.00 technical level, as further fluctuations could signal increased volatility. Market participants are eyeing any official response from Japan, which could trigger additional moves in the currency pair. Given the current environment, market watchers are also factoring in broader risk sentiment and shifts in other major pairs like the EUR/USD and USD/JPY, which remain central to ongoing trading strategies.
