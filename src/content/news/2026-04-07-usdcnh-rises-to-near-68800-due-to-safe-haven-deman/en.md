The USD/CNH exchange rate edged higher, nearing the 6.8800 level during Tuesday’s Asian session, as safe-haven demand boosted the U.S. dollar. After experiencing minor losses in the previous trading day, the pair saw renewed strength, with the greenback gaining momentum amid heightened market uncertainty.

The rise in USD/CNH comes as global risk sentiment remains cautious, with investors turning to the dollar for stability. As the pair approaches key resistance at 6.8800, further movement depends on broader market trends, including developments in global economic indicators and geopolitical events. The currency pair's performance suggests that the market remains wary of risk, with USD acting as a preferred safe-haven asset.
