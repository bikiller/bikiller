Nordea's Chief Analyst, Jan von Gerich, anticipates that the European Central Bank (ECB) will maintain its policy rates at the upcoming April meeting, while keeping the door open for potential adjustments in June. This decision is expected to be accompanied by a hawkish stance, reinforcing market speculation about an interest rate hike later in the year.

As traders monitor the EUR/USD currency pair, the ECB's communication may drive expectations and influence the euro’s exchange rate against the USD. With current market conditions reflecting caution, any indications of a June hike could lead to significant volatility in trading, impacting not just the euro but broader forex market dynamics.
