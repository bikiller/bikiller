The US Dollar Index (DXY), which gauges the USD's value against six major currencies, is currently trading around 98.00 after showing resilience in recovering from earlier losses. During European trading hours on Wednesday, the index fluctuated near 97.90, reflecting a stable exchange rate as investors await fresh economic data.

This stability comes as traders analyze market sentiment and potential influences on the USD, particularly against pairs like EUR/USD. The recent recovery may indicate a cautious optimism among forex participants, who are keenly watching geopolitical developments and economic indicators that could sway the dollar's trajectory in the near term.
