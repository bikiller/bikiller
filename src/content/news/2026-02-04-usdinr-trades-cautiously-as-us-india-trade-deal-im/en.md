The Indian Rupee (INR) showed resilience against the US Dollar (USD) on Wednesday, with the USD/INR currency pair trading cautiously around 90.55. The pair struggled to recover after recent losses, as market sentiment remained cautious following the positive developments in the US-India trade deal. 

The agreement has sparked optimism among Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs), strengthening demand for the INR. With the pair maintaining its position near 90.55, traders are closely watching any potential shifts in the exchange rate. The improving trade relations are expected to keep investor sentiment bullish towards Indian assets, providing further support to the Rupee.
