EUR/JPY has rebounded from recent losses, trading at approximately 183.20 during the Asian trading session on Friday. This steady momentum suggests that while the market may be stabilizing, traders should remain vigilant for potential fluctuations in the EUR/JPY exchange rate as broader market trends unfold.
