GBP/USD is experiencing an upward trend during the North American session, climbing towards the key resistance level of 1.3600. This movement comes in the wake of Iran's announcement to reopen the Strait of Hormuz following a ceasefire agreement in Lebanon, which has weakened the US Dollar. Currently, the currency pair is trading at 1.3567, reflecting a gain of 0.36%.

The reopening of the Strait of Hormuz is significant, as it bolsters market confidence and diminishes geopolitical tensions in the region. This shift has allowed the British Pound to gain traction against the USD, highlighting the interconnectedness of forex markets and geopolitical events. Traders will be closely monitoring this development as they seek opportunities in the evolving exchange rate landscape.
