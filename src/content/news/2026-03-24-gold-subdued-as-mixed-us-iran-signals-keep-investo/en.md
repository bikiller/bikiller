Gold (XAU/USD) remains subdued on Tuesday, struggling to maintain momentum after a brief rebound. The precious metal trades around $1,960, as conflicting signals from the United States and Iran continue to create uncertainty in the market. Investors are cautious amid the ongoing Middle East conflict, weighing the likelihood of diplomatic talks and their impact on global risk sentiment.

The uncertainty surrounding the geopolitical situation keeps gold prices locked in a narrow range, with technical levels at $1,955 acting as support and $1,970 as resistance. Despite the potential for further market volatility, the USD's strength remains a key factor, impacting gold's appeal as a safe-haven asset. As the situation unfolds, traders are expected to closely monitor any developments related to US-Iran relations and broader market sentiment.
