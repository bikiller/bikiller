EUR/USD climbed modestly during Friday’s European trading session, edging closer to the 1.1800 mark. The currency pair saw an uptick as the US Dollar (USD) lost ground, impacted by a broader shift towards a moderate risk appetite in the market. This movement reflects the market's anticipation ahead of upcoming US-Iran peace talks, with traders eyeing potential geopolitical developments.

As the Euro (EUR) strengthens, the exchange rate approaches the key 1.1800 level, signaling potential for further upside. The USD’s pullback came as global investors balanced risk factors, leading to a softer demand for the dollar. Market participants are closely monitoring the progress of diplomatic talks between the US and Iran, which could influence the broader market sentiment in the coming days.
