The GBP/USD currency pair gained 0.78% on Thursday, climbing to 1.3581 as traders reacted to expectations of further Bank of England tightening despite the central bank holding rates steady earlier in the week. Market participants appeared focused on the prospect of sustained monetary support, pushing the exchange rate higher against the USD and reinforcing bullish sentiment in trading sessions.  

Technical charts highlight a looming bullish engulfing pattern, signaling potential momentum for the pound. Analysts suggest that if this formation confirms, the GBP/USD could test the 1.36 level, attracting short-term trading interest and encouraging positions among forex traders seeking to capitalize on upward moves. The pattern adds a layer of technical conviction to fundamental drivers already influencing the market.  

Traders are closely monitoring the broader USD environment and cross-currency dynamics, including EUR/USD flows, as they assess risk and positioning. The combination of central bank expectations and chart-based signals suggests a potentially active period for the pound, with exchange rate swings likely to respond to both macroeconomic developments and technical cues in global forex markets.
