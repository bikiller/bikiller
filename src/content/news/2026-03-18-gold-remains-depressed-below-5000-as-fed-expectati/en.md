Gold (XAU/USD) continues to hover below the key $5,000 mark, maintaining a subdued trading range during the Asian session on Wednesday. Despite ongoing tensions in the Middle East, the yellow metal struggles to gain momentum as traders remain cautious ahead of the crucial Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) decision. With market sentiment focused on the potential outcome of the U.S. central bank's meeting, gold's movement has been constrained.

The uncertainty surrounding the FOMC’s stance on interest rates has overshadowed geopolitical risks, keeping the gold price in a tight range since the start of the week. The USD continues to show strength against other currencies, including the EUR and GBP, limiting the appeal of safe-haven assets like gold. As traders await clearer direction, any significant moves will likely follow the FOMC’s policy announcement, which could determine the next major trend in XAU/USD.
