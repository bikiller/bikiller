The Philippine Peso (PHP) came under pressure as political tensions affected investor sentiment, with the central bank at the heart of controversy. A legal complaint against Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) Governor Eli Remolona surfaced, tied to an impeachment probe involving the vice president. This has raised concerns about the potential impact on the BSP’s stability and its ability to manage the currency.

The USD/PHP exchange rate saw notable volatility amid the news, with the pair pushing towards 56.00, reflecting market anxiety over the escalating political rift. Traders are closely watching developments, as any disruption at the BSP could influence the central bank’s monetary policy stance. The Peso's weakening trend may continue unless the political situation stabilizes.
