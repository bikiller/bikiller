Gold (XAU/USD) continues its positive momentum for the third consecutive day, maintaining trading levels above $5,350 as it enters the European session on Tuesday. The precious metal has found support from persistent safe-haven demand, as global uncertainty keeps investors seeking refuge in gold. Despite this, a firmer USD has limited further upside, capping its gains for the time being.

The exchange rate for XAU/USD remains firmly above the key $5,350 mark, showing resilience amid ongoing market volatility. With the U.S. dollar holding its ground, gold's upward movement is constrained, reflecting a delicate balance between risk-off sentiment and the strength of the greenback. As traders await further developments, gold’s performance will continue to be influenced by both geopolitical tensions and the direction of USD strength.
