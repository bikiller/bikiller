TD Securities has issued a bearish outlook on the USD, citing robust global growth and a diminishing appeal for safe-haven currencies. With the USD under scrutiny, the potential for further declines against major currencies could have significant implications for forex strategies, particularly for those trading USD/EUR and USD/JPY pairs. As the market evolves, traders should remain vigilant on economic indicators that could shift sentiment towards the dollar.
