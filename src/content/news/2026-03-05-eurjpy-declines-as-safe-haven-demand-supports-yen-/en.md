EUR/JPY is trading lower around 182.60, down 0.14% on the day as of Thursday. As global uncertainties rise, investors are flocking to JPY, pushing the exchange rate lower.

Meanwhile, the Euro (EUR) is receiving mixed signals in the market, with its strength somewhat overshadowed by the broader risk-off sentiment. Despite ongoing challenges, EUR traders are watching for further direction from economic data, while geopolitical developments continue to weigh on the currency pair’s performance.
