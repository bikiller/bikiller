Silver (XAG/USD) is experiencing a steady trading session, maintaining its position above the $84.00 threshold for the second consecutive day. Despite the positive momentum, silver appears vulnerable as it confines its exchange rate movements. Traders are closely monitoring the market for potential shifts, with the current lack of strong buying pressure raising questions about the sustainability of this upward trend. The stability above the $84.00 mark may offer a critical support level, but without increased buying interest, a reversal could be on the horizon.
