The US Dollar Index (DXY) has seen its rebound lose momentum, stalling just below the 100 mark. After declining from 100.6 to just under 98 earlier in April, the index has struggled to maintain upward momentum. This pause signals a potential shift in the USD’s short-term strength, impacting key currency pairs.

In particular, the EUR/USD exchange rate may face further fluctuations as DXY stalls. The lack of sustained strength in the dollar is affecting market sentiment, with traders keeping an eye on potential resistance levels and broader economic indicators. This uncertainty could lead to more range-bound trading in USD-linked pairs.
