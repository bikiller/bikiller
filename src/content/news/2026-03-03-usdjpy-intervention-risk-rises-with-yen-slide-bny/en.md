The USD/JPY exchange rate has surged past 157, drawing heightened scrutiny from Japan's Finance Minister Satsuki Katayama. Katayama stated that the Ministry of Finance is closely monitoring market developments and remains prepared to intervene in response to sharp fluctuations in the currency pair. This marks a significant shift as the yen continues to weaken, signaling potential risks for further instability.

As USD/JPY trades near these elevated levels, concerns about Japan's growing intervention risk are mounting. A sustained rise in the yen's depreciation could trigger official action, with the Ministry on alert for excessive volatility. The current exchange rate dynamics underscore the ongoing pressure faced by the yen, and any potential measures taken could have profound effects on global forex trading and investor sentiment.
