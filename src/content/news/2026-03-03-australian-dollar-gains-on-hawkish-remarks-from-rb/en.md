The Australian Dollar (AUD) continued its upward momentum for the second consecutive session, with AUD/USD trading around the 0.7100 mark during the Asian trading hours on Tuesday. The pair's recovery was primarily driven by hawkish remarks from Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) Governor Michelle Bullock, which provided strong support to the currency.

The RBA's tone hinted at potential further tightening of monetary policy, sparking optimism for the Australian Dollar. As a result, the AUD gained ground against the US Dollar (USD), with the pair advancing through key technical levels. Traders will closely monitor any additional comments from RBA officials, as further bullish signals could propel AUD/USD even higher in the short term.
