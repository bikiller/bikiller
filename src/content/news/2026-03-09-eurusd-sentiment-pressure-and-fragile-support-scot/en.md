EUR/USD remains under pressure as the Euro weakens against the US Dollar, despite a more supportive rate outlook for the European currency. Market sentiment is heavily influenced by escalating geopolitical risks, overshadowing the Euro's relatively positive rate prospects. The pair is currently trading near fragile support levels, and analysts caution that further downside could be on the horizon unless the geopolitical landscape stabilizes.

Scotiabank strategists, Shaun Osborne and Eric Theoret, highlight that despite the Eurozone’s more favorable rate expectations, the EUR/USD exchange rate has been unable to capitalize on this. With the geopolitical risks weighing on sentiment, the EUR/USD pair is vulnerable to further declines. Traders are advised to watch key support levels as any break below these could see the Euro depreciate further against the stronger USD in the coming sessions.
