The EUR/USD currency pair is struggling to hold ground, trading near 1.1430 in early Asian session activity on Monday. Investors are cautious as geopolitical tensions in the Middle East escalate, supporting demand for the safe-haven USD. The pair remains below the critical 1.1450 resistance level, signaling that upward momentum may be constrained in the near term.  

Trading dynamics suggest that any rebound in the euro could face resistance, with traders closely watching the exchange rate for signs of renewed volatility. Market participants are balancing eurozone economic data against heightened risk sentiment, which continues to favor the dollar. As a result, EUR/USD has limited room for gains unless tensions ease.  

Analysts note that the defensive posture of the EUR/USD reflects broader market caution. Traders are likely to remain sensitive to news from the Middle East, which could drive safe-haven flows into the USD. With the euro under pressure and trading below 1.1450, the outlook points to a cautious trading environment, emphasizing risk management for currency traders navigating these choppy conditions.
