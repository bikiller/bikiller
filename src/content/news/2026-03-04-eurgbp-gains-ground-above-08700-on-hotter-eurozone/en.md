The EUR/GBP currency pair climbed above the 0.8700 mark, reaching around 0.8710 during the early European trading session on Wednesday. This upward movement follows the release of Eurozone inflation data that exceeded expectations, prompting the Euro (EUR) to strengthen against the Pound Sterling (GBP). 

The hotter-than-anticipated inflation figures are likely to influence the European Central Bank's monetary policy decisions moving forward. Traders are closely watching the exchange rate, as sustained gains in the EUR/GBP could signal a shift in market sentiment toward the Euro amid ongoing economic developments.
