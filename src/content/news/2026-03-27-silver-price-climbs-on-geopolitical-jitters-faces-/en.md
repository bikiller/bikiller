Silver (XAG/USD) is trading around $68.50 on Friday, gaining 0.59% on the day as investors show renewed interest in the precious metal. Geopolitical uncertainties are driving demand for silver, as market participants seek safe-haven assets amid global tensions. However, despite the uptick, the currency pair remains in a broader sideways trend, reflecting cautious sentiment.

Higher bond yields are putting downward pressure on silver prices, limiting further gains. The strength of the USD, supported by rising yields, continues to weigh on silver's upside potential. With the market navigating an uncertain macroeconomic landscape, silver faces challenges in breaking out of its current range. The outlook for the white metal remains clouded as traders closely monitor developments in global markets.
