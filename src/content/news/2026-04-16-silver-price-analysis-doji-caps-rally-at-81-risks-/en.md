Silver (XAG/USD) faced a setback on Thursday, declining by 0.30% as the US Dollar regained strength. The currency pair failed to break the crucial $81.00 resistance level, despite initially reaching a high of $80.86. The Greenback's resurgence dampened the bullish momentum, leaving silver vulnerable to further downward pressure.

At the time of writing, XAG/USD is trading at $78.73, as the market assesses the next potential moves. With the failure to surpass the $81.00 barrier, the risk of a pullback looms, and traders are closely monitoring the evolving USD dynamics. The price action suggests that silver may struggle to maintain upward momentum without a clear catalyst for renewed buying.
