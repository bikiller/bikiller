EUR/CAD continues its downward trend, extending a fourth consecutive day of losses as the currency pair hovers around the 1.5910 mark during European trading hours on Tuesday. The exchange rate has been under pressure as traders await the release of the Eurozone's preliminary Harmonized Index of Consumer Prices (HICP) data for February, which could provide fresh insights into inflation dynamics.

Market participants are keenly focused on the upcoming HICP report, set to be published later today. Expectations for the data could impact the euro's performance against the Canadian dollar, with inflation figures potentially influencing the European Central Bank’s future monetary policy stance. Traders will be closely monitoring the outcome for signs of price pressures in the Eurozone, which could shift market sentiment and impact EUR/CAD further.
