The US Dollar Index (DXY), which tracks the greenback against six major currencies, continues to hold its ground above 97.00 for a second straight day. As of Tuesday's European session, the index is trading near 97.20, reflecting ongoing strength in the USD. Market participants are closely watching the release of the Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC) minutes, which may provide further clarity on the Federal Reserve's future monetary policy.

The USD's robust performance is evident across multiple currency pairs, with the EUR/USD trading lower around 1.0580, highlighting the dollar's dominance. Traders are positioning themselves ahead of the FOMC minutes, anticipating potential insights into the Fed's stance on inflation and interest rates. This has led to increased demand for the USD, supported by broader market expectations of continued tightening.
