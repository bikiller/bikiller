EUR/JPY pulled back after two consecutive days of gains, with the currency pair trading around 183.40 in the Asian session on Wednesday. The exchange rate recently tested the upper boundary of a descending triangle near 183.50, indicating potential resistance. The pair's price movement highlights the challenges it faces in pushing higher, especially given its ongoing downward trend.

The recent fluctuations in EUR/JPY reflect broader market uncertainty, with global economic concerns weighing on investor sentiment. Traders are closely monitoring the pair's ability to break through technical resistance at 183.50, which could signal a shift in momentum. Failure to do so may reinforce the bearish outlook, keeping the EUR under pressure against the JPY in the near term.
