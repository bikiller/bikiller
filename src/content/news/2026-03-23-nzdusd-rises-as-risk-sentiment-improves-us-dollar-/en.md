The NZD/USD currency pair is trading around the 0.5860 mark as the Asian session begins, after experiencing a pullback of nearly 50% of its earlier gains during the US session. As the market digests recent developments, traders are watching the price action closely for potential direction. The USD is holding steady against major currencies, while the NZD gains ground on improving investor sentiment.
