The Australian Dollar (AUD) made significant gains against the US Dollar (USD) on Friday morning, with the AUD/USD pair climbing towards the 0.6900 level during early European trading. The uptick comes amid a hawkish stance from the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA), which continues to support the Aussie Dollar. The market sentiment remains optimistic about further RBA tightening, helping the AUD stay resilient against its major counterparts.

Market activity is expected to remain subdued due to the Good Friday holiday, leading to thinner trading volumes. The lack of major catalysts could limit significant price movement, although traders are likely eyeing the upcoming US jobs report for potential market impact. Any surprise in the data could shift the focus back to the USD and influence the exchange rate in the coming sessions.
