The Pound Sterling (GBP) surged to revisit a three-month high against the US Dollar (USD), trading around 1.3535 during the European session on Wednesday. The exchange rate's resilience against the USD illustrates the currency's appeal amid a backdrop of fluctuating market sentiment.

As the GBP continues to gain traction, traders will be closely monitoring upcoming economic indicators that may influence future movements in the currency pair. Any shifts in monetary policy from the Federal Reserve or the Bank of England could further impact the exchange rate dynamics. With the GBP testing these key technical levels, market participants are keenly aware of potential volatility as they navigate this bullish trend.
