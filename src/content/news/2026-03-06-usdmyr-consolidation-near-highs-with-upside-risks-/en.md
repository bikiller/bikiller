USD/MYR is consolidating near recent highs, following a notable surge earlier in the week. The currency pair has been buoyed by the strength of the USD and broader market risk aversion. The exchange rate continues to hold above key levels, reflecting cautious sentiment in global markets. Analysts point to geopolitical developments, particularly concerning Iran, as contributing factors to the subdued risk appetite.

The broader USD strength, coupled with persistent uncertainty in the energy markets, has led to limited downside for the pair. The current consolidation in USD/MYR indicates potential upside risks, with traders remaining vigilant on any shifts in geopolitical dynamics. The market is likely to stay sensitive to news from the Middle East and energy sectors, as these developments will continue to shape USD/MYR trading in the near term.
