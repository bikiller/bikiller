The US Dollar Index (DXY) remains robust, holding steady near the 99.00 level as geopolitical tensions rise, particularly between the US and Iran. On Friday, the DXY maintained its position in the upper range of the 98.00s, reflecting a 0.4% gain for the week. This strength in the USD is prompting traders to seek safety, resulting in reduced risk appetite across the market.

As the USD strengthens, major currency pairs are showing mixed movements. The Euro (EUR) and other currencies are facing downward pressure against the dollar. Investors are closely monitoring the evolving geopolitical landscape, which could further influence exchange rates and trading strategies in the coming days. With uncertainty prevailing, the USD's safe-haven appeal continues to draw attention amid fluctuating market conditions.
