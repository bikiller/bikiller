Gold (XAU/USD) remains steady above the $4,200 mark as it attempts to maintain gains following a bounce from the $4,175 level, which aligns with this week's lows. During the Asian trading session on Friday, the precious metal is exhibiting limited movement, trapped in a narrow range as traders await critical economic data.

Market participants are eyeing the upcoming US PCE Price Index, which could provide significant direction for the gold market. Any unexpected shifts in inflation data may influence investor sentiment and affect the exchange rate of gold against the USD, potentially sparking a breakout from its current consolidation phase.
