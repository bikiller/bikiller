Gold (XAU/USD) is experiencing a rebound on Wednesday, trading around $4,335, up nearly 0.70% for the day. As the exchange rate between gold and the US dollar fluctuates, traders are weighing the impacts of monetary policy and economic indicators. The current dynamics suggest that while gold is gaining traction, the persistent strength of the USD could pose challenges for further price increases.
