The USD’s role in Q2 market flows is set to play a pivotal role in diversification strategies, according to Bob Savage, Head of Markets Macro Strategy at BNY. He notes that the correlation between the Rest of World equity index and the USD index is significant, signaling that decisions in global trading may be strongly influenced by the greenback’s performance. Traders should watch how the USD impacts cross-currency pairs as portfolio adjustments unfold in the second quarter.

As Q2 progresses, any shift in USD strength could impact key forex pairs, particularly the EUR/USD. The broader currency landscape will feel the effects of USD price movements, especially if volatility increases amid ongoing global economic trends. Traders are advised to focus on technical levels and correlations to navigate potential risks and opportunities in the forex market.
