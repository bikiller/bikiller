The EUR/USD currency pair remains steady at 1.1590 during the early Asian trading session on Thursday, as market participants adopt a cautious stance ahead of US President Donald Trump’s anticipated speech on the Iran conflict. This level has kept the exchange rate below the key 1.1600 mark, reflecting a wait-and-see approach as traders brace for potential volatility.

In addition to the geopolitical concerns, the release of the US weekly Initial Jobless Claims report later today adds further uncertainty. Analysts expect the data to influence the USD, potentially driving the EUR/USD pair either higher or lower. As traders closely monitor these events, the exchange rate continues to trade within a narrow range, highlighting market indecision ahead of critical US economic data.
