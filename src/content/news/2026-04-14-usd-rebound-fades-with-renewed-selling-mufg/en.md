The US Dollar (USD) has seen its early-week rebound fade as it returns to pre-conflict levels, reflecting a shift in market sentiment. The US Dollar index (DXY) has retreated from gains, impacted by growing optimism surrounding US-Iran talks and de-escalation in the Middle East. This shift has led to a weakening of the greenback across major pairs.

EUR/USD has benefitted from the USD's pullback, rising towards key technical resistance levels. Traders are closely monitoring the exchange rate for further signs of a breakdown in the USD's momentum. The renewed optimism in global markets has allowed the euro to regain strength, while broader risk sentiment remains cautiously positive. As market participants digest the latest geopolitical developments, USD trading volatility is expected to persist.
