USD/CHF extended its gains for a second consecutive session, trading at 0.7810 during Tuesday's Asian hours. The currency pair benefitted from the US Dollar's strength, driven by increased demand for safe-haven assets amid escalating tensions in the Middle East. The USD's surge in response to risk aversion pushed the pair above the 0.7800 mark, signaling broader market uncertainty.

The US Dollar's rise has impacted the broader forex market, with the EUR/USD slipping below 1.0600 as investors flock to the USD for stability. As global risks intensify, traders are likely to remain focused on safe-haven flows, keeping USD/CHF elevated. The pair's ability to hold above 0.7800 could signal further bullish momentum if geopolitical risks persist.
