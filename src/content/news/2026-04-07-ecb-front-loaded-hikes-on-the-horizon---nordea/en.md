Nordea analysts have revised their outlook for the European Central Bank (ECB), forecasting a series of front-loaded rate hikes as inflation continues to take precedence over growth. With the ongoing Middle East conflict and rising price pressures, the ECB is expected to prioritize tackling inflation, potentially leading to a tighter monetary policy. This shift could significantly impact the EUR/USD currency pair, as the market adjusts to the possibility of faster rate increases.

As inflation concerns intensify, the ECB's decision to adopt a more aggressive stance on rate hikes could strengthen the euro in the near term. Traders will be closely monitoring EUR/USD exchange rates, with key technical levels coming into focus as the market adjusts to these developments. The move is likely to stir increased volatility, with the USD remaining in focus as investors assess the broader economic implications.
