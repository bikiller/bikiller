Dutch TTF Natural Gas Futures for April 2026 have seen a dramatic surge, rising 33.97% to €59.62 on Tuesday. This marks an extension of the rally that has seen prices soar more than 85% since Friday's close. The price spike is being fueled by supply concerns following disruptions in the Strait of Hormuz and an unexpected move by Qatar LNG, which has sent shockwaves through European energy markets.

The surge in natural gas prices is also influencing forex markets, particularly in EUR/USD trading. With rising energy costs, the EUR faces pressure against the USD, as European economic concerns intensify. This shift in the natural gas market, along with geopolitical instability in the Middle East, is likely to keep volatility high, especially for energy-related currency pairs, as traders react to the ongoing market turbulence.
