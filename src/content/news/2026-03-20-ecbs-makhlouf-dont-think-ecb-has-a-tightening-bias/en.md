ECB policymaker Gabriel Makhlouf, also the Governor of the Central Bank of Ireland, stated on Friday that the European Central Bank (ECB) does not have a pre-determined rate path. This comment came during European trading hours, as the EUR/USD exchange rate saw modest fluctuations. According to Makhlouf, ECB decisions on interest rates will be made on a meeting-by-meeting basis, signaling that any tightening bias remains uncertain for the time being.

The lack of clarity around the ECB’s future policy decisions caused some uncertainty in the forex market, with the EUR/USD trading near 1.0700. While the central bank's stance suggests a flexible approach, traders are closely watching upcoming meetings for further signals. The euro’s performance against the USD will depend on how inflation and economic data unfold in the coming weeks, influencing market expectations for any shifts in the ECB’s monetary policy.
