HSBC analysts Willem Sels and Lucia Ku are optimistic about the outlook for US equities and USD investment-grade credit over the next six months. They point to the United States’ growing status as an energy exporter, which supports the economy’s resilience. Additionally, robust projected earnings growth and significant investments in technology and AI provide a solid foundation for the market's performance, particularly for the USD.

In the forex market, this positive outlook strengthens the USD, with traders continuing to favor it against other major currencies like the EUR. The USD/EUR exchange rate remains stable as US economic strength, driven by technological advancements and earnings, balances global uncertainties. As AI-related investments continue to grow, the USD's appeal could increase, influencing future trading patterns in the currency market.
