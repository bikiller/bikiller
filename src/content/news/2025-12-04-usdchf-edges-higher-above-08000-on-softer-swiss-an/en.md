The USD/CHF currency pair has moved into positive territory, trading around 0.8010 during early European hours on Thursday. The Swiss Franc (CHF) has weakened against the US Dollar (USD) following the release of softer annual inflation data from Switzerland, which has prompted traders to reassess their positions.

Market participants are now focusing on the upcoming US weekly Initial Jobless Claims report, set to be released later today. This data could influence the exchange rate further, as traders weigh the implications for the Federal Reserve's monetary policy in the context of improving labor market conditions.
