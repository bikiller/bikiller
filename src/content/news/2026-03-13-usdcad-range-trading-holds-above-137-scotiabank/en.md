USD/CAD remains in a tight range above 1.37 as the Canadian Dollar faces downward pressure. Weaker-than-expected Canadian employment data and rising geopolitical tensions have weighed on the loonie, despite similar softness in U.S. economic figures. The exchange rate has managed to hold above key technical levels, with traders closely monitoring these developments for potential shifts.

As U.S. data also shows signs of cooling, the USD is not immune to market concerns, but the Canadian Dollar’s weakness has helped maintain a firm range for the currency pair. With geopolitical risks and domestic economic factors continuing to influence market sentiment, the USD/CAD remains range-bound, showing limited movement above the 1.37 level. Traders are awaiting further developments before making significant moves.
