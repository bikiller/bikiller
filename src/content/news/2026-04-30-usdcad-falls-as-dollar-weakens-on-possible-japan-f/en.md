USD/CAD is under pressure as the US Dollar weakens, with the currency pair trading lower on Thursday. At 1.3612, the pair has dropped nearly 0.53% on the day, reflecting a broader decline in the USD. This shift follows the release of US economic data, which failed to provide sufficient support for the Greenback, allowing the Canadian Dollar (CAD) to gain ground.

The weakening USD is also influenced by speculation surrounding potential intervention by Japan in the foreign exchange market. As USD/CAD struggles to hold higher levels, traders are closely watching key technical levels and broader market sentiment, with the pair's movement suggesting ongoing volatility. The exchange rate is expected to remain sensitive to further developments in US economic indicators and global market conditions.
