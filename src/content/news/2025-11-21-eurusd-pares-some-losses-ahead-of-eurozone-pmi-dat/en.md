As the Euro continues to find support, fluctuations in the USD also play a critical role in the trading dynamics. Anticipation surrounding the PMI figures may drive additional volatility in the forex market, impacting the EUR/USD exchange rate in the coming sessions. Investors are closely monitoring these developments, as they could signal shifts in economic momentum for the Eurozone.
