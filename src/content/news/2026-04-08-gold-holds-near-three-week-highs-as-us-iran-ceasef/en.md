Gold (XAU/USD) remains steady near three-week highs, reflecting market reactions to a newly announced two-week ceasefire between the United States and Iran. As traders digest this geopolitical development, the yellow metal has faced challenges in extending its gains, currently hovering around key resistance levels. 

The weakening of the US Dollar has bolstered gold's appeal, as investors seek safe-haven assets amidst ongoing uncertainties in global markets. With the USD showing signs of softness against major currencies, including the EUR, gold's exchange rate has gained traction, signaling a potential shift in trading dynamics as market participants reassess their positions in light of the evolving geopolitical landscape.
