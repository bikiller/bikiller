The EUR/GBP currency pair continues to face downward pressure, trading in negative territory for the fourth consecutive day. The sustained weakness in the EUR/GBP reflects broader market sentiment as traders react to economic indicators. With the euro under pressure against the pound, traders are closely monitoring technical levels to gauge potential support. As the session unfolds, the focus will remain on how these economic signals may influence future trading strategies in this currency pair.
