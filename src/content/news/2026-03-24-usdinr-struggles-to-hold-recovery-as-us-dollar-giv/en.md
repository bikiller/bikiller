The USD/INR pair faces difficulties maintaining its recovery as the US Dollar loses early momentum. After a weaker start in Tuesday's session, the Indian Rupee is attempting to regain ground against the greenback, with the exchange rate fluctuating around 82.75. Early trading saw the USD rise, but gains are quickly fading as the market adjusts to mixed global economic signals. 

At the same time, the broader forex market shows volatility, with the USD showing signs of weakness against major currencies like the EUR. As the INR struggles to break key resistance levels, the near-term outlook for the Indian Rupee remains uncertain. Traders are keeping an eye on upcoming economic data that could sway market sentiment, particularly in relation to US inflation figures and the Reserve Bank of India's stance on interest rates.
