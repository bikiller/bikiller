The Pound Sterling (GBP) has gained traction, rising 0.5% against the US Dollar (USD) as market sentiment shifts positively ahead of key Bank of England (BoE) speeches. Traders are closely watching the exchange rates as the BoE prepares to address monetary policy, which could further influence the GBP's momentum. With the current trading dynamics, the GBP's rally signals growing confidence among investors, setting the stage for potential volatility in the currency pair as market participants react to upcoming economic insights.
