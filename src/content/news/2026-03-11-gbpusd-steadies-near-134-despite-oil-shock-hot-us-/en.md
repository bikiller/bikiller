The GBP/USD exchange rate held steady around 1.3400 during the North American session on Wednesday, despite rising volatility in the global markets. While the ongoing Middle East conflict entered its twelfth day, oil prices surged, causing a ripple effect on risk sentiment. However, the Pound demonstrated resilience, keeping its ground amid the market turmoil.

The U.S. Dollar gained strength from hotter-than-expected inflation data, which increased expectations of more aggressive Fed tightening. Despite the pressure from USD strength, GBP/USD remains relatively unchanged, showing limited movement as traders balance global risks and the economic outlook. The currency pair’s technical support remains at 1.3400, offering stability for the time being.
