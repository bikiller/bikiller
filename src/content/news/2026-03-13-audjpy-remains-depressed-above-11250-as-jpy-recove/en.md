AUD/JPY remains under pressure for a second consecutive day, trading just above the 112.50 mark as the Japanese yen (JPY) shows slight recovery. The currency pair is experiencing limited gains during Friday’s Asian session, hovering in the mid-112.00s. The subdued performance reflects ongoing concerns over potential intervention by Japanese authorities to stem further depreciation of the yen.

The cautious trading sentiment stems from fears that the Bank of Japan may take action to prevent excessive weakness in the yen. Despite this, the Australian dollar (AUD) is struggling to gain momentum against the yen, with the exchange rate unable to break decisively above 113.00. Market participants are closely watching developments for any hints of intervention or policy shifts that could impact the JPY’s value and the AUD/JPY trading range.
