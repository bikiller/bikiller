Geopolitical tensions in the Middle East are weighing heavily on the Euro, particularly against the U.S. Dollar, according to Commerzbank’s Antje Praefcke. Europe’s heavy reliance on energy imports and its ongoing economic sluggishness make the EUR/USD currency pair vulnerable. As global unrest deepens, concerns over potential disruptions in energy supplies have kept the euro under pressure, with the exchange rate struggling to gain momentum.

The current market outlook shows EUR/USD trading in a narrow range, with the pair finding support around 1.0700 but facing resistance near 1.0800. This technical range highlights the challenges facing the euro as traders remain cautious amid global instability. As long as the geopolitical risks persist, the Euro may continue to face downward pressure, while the U.S. Dollar benefits from its safe-haven status in these uncertain times.
