Volatility is set to increase in the forex market as traders prepare for the release of the US Nonfarm Payrolls data. A strong Nonfarm Payrolls report could bolster the greenback, pushing the pair higher, while a disappointing outcome may lead to a reversal. The market's reaction to the data will likely shape trading strategies in the coming days.
