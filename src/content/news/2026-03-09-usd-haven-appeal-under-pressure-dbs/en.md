The US Dollar's traditional safe-haven appeal is under scrutiny as it fails to respond to risk aversion, even with rising oil prices and geopolitical tensions. Despite a volatile market, the USD has not seen the expected demand, undermining its position as a refuge during uncertain times. This shift has seen the EUR/USD pair trading near 1.0700, signaling a weakening of the USD against major currencies.

Oil’s surge and heightened geopolitical risks have typically fueled demand for the greenback. However, the current market dynamics reveal a lack of support for USD, with its performance against peers like the Euro and Yen showing mixed results. The USD/JPY exchange rate has also struggled to maintain its upward momentum, highlighting investor hesitation in seeking the USD as a safe-haven asset. This trend could suggest a broader shift in forex market sentiment and risk appetite.
