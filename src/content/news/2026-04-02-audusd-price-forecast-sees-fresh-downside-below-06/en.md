The AUD/USD currency pair dropped 0.7% to around 0.6870 during the late Asian trading session on Thursday, reflecting a broader risk-off sentiment. The Australian Dollar (AUD) faces significant selling pressure as traders shift to safe-haven assets amid growing concerns in global markets. This shift is pushing the exchange rate lower, with the pair struggling to maintain a foothold above key support levels.

The renewed risk aversion has intensified bearish momentum for the AUD/USD, especially as the USD remains a preferred safe haven in uncertain times. The pair is now poised to face additional downside risks if it falls below the 0.6830 mark, a critical technical level. Market sentiment will likely continue to play a major role, as any escalation in global uncertainty could prompt further downside pressure on the Australian Dollar.
