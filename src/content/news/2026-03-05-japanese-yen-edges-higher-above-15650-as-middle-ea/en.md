The Japanese Yen climbed above the 156.50 level as safe-haven demand surged, driven by the escalating conflict in the Middle East. During the Asian trading hours on Thursday, the USD/JPY pair traded in negative territory, hovering around 156.85. This movement highlights a shift in investor sentiment as traders seek stability amidst geopolitical tensions.

As the conflict intensifies, the JPY is benefiting from its status as a traditional safe haven, prompting a notable impact on the exchange rate with the US Dollar. The increased demand for the Yen reflects broader market concerns, suggesting that further volatility in the region may continue to influence currency trading dynamics in the coming sessions.
