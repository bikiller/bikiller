USD/JPY is hovering near the 159.50 level, edging higher during the American session as traders position ahead of the Federal Reserve’s policy announcement. The currency pair’s modest gains reflect steady demand for the USD, with market participants closely watching whether the central bank maintains its current stance amid persistent inflation pressures.

Expectations are firmly anchored around the Fed holding interest rates unchanged, but the tone of its guidance remains critical for near-term exchange rate direction. Elevated inflation concerns continue to underpin the USD, limiting downside in USD/JPY while keeping volatility contained. Traders are also monitoring U.S. yields, which have provided additional support to the pair in recent sessions.

Looking ahead, any hawkish signals from the Fed could push USD/JPY beyond the 159.50 zone, potentially testing higher resistance levels. Conversely, a more cautious outlook may trigger a pullback as profit-taking emerges. The broader forex market, including EUR/USD and other major currency pairs, is likely to react in tandem as investors reassess global monetary policy trajectories.
