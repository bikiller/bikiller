The change in the USD/CNY reference rate may influence trading strategies, as investors assess the implications for China's economic outlook and the broader forex market. With the yuan hovering around this level, traders are likely to keep a close eye on further PBOC interventions and potential shifts in global sentiment towards the Chinese currency.
