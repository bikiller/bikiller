The EUR/USD currency pair gained momentum, trading around 1.1720 during the Asian session on Wednesday, reflecting a positive shift in market sentiment. The pair's rally was fueled by a boost in risk appetite as investors reacted to US President Donald Trump's announcement regarding Project Freedom. This pause, along with the suspension of ship movements through the Strait of Hormuz, helped to alleviate geopolitical concerns.

The strengthened EUR/USD exchange rate marked a significant move above the 1.1700 technical level, highlighting the market's response to the latest developments. As traders digested the news, the US dollar weakened, allowing the euro to gain ground. With risk sentiment improving, the currency pair's outlook remains sensitive to global political factors, particularly surrounding tensions in the Middle East.
