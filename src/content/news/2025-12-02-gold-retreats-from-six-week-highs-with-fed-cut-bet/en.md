Gold (XAU/USD) is experiencing downward pressure on Tuesday, retreating from its recent six-week highs. The fluctuations in gold prices are indicative of broader trends in the forex market, where the USD continues to influence trading dynamics across various currency pairs, including EUR/USD. Investor sentiment is being shaped by the Fed's monetary policy outlook, which may limit the downside potential for gold as it navigates through profit-taking and market adjustments. As traders assess the implications of these developments, gold's resilience amid a shifting landscape will be closely monitored.
