The US Dollar Index (DXY) continued its upward momentum on Monday, approaching the ten-month highs seen earlier this month. As tensions escalate in the Middle East, demand for the USD remains strong, driving the index higher. The DXY’s solid performance reflects investor preference for the dollar as a safe-haven asset amid geopolitical uncertainty. 

Currency pairs like EUR/USD saw pressure as the Greenback strengthened. The exchange rate for EUR/USD traded near 1.0600, reflecting a marked shift in market sentiment. Traders are also factoring in the Federal Reserve's steady outlook, with expectations of a hawkish stance supporting the USD in the near term. This mix of geopolitical risk and a stable Fed policy has solidified the USD's position in the global markets.
