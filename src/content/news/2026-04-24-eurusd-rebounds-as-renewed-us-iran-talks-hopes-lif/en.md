EUR/USD experienced a rebound on Friday, breaking a three-day losing streak as renewed hopes for US-Iran peace talks bolstered market sentiment. The currency pair is currently trading at approximately 1.1715, reflecting a 0.27% increase on the day. This recovery comes after dipping to two-week lows earlier in the week, indicating a shift in investor confidence.

The optimism surrounding diplomatic discussions has weighed on the US Dollar (USD), encouraging traders to seek riskier assets, while the Euro (EUR) benefited from the positive sentiment. As the exchange rate stabilizes, market participants will be closely monitoring developments in US-Iran relations, which could further influence the dynamics of the EUR/USD pairing moving forward.
