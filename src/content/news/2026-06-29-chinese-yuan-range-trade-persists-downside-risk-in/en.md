USD/CNH continues to trade within a narrow range as the Chinese yuan shows limited directional conviction against the US dollar. According to UOB Senior Technical Strategist Quek Ser Leang, the currency pair is expected to stay confined between 6.7950 and 6.8100 in the near term, reflecting subdued volatility in the exchange rate and cautious trading conditions across the forex market.

Easing upward momentum has done little to alter the broader technical picture, with analysts noting that while gains have softened, upside potential toward 6.8300 remains in play as long as key support at 6.7900 holds firm against the US dollar. Market participants continue to monitor price action for confirmation of a sustained breakout beyond the current consolidation zone.

Overall, the currency pair remains locked in a consolidation phase, with range trading strategies likely to dominate as forex traders await clearer US dollar direction. With resistance capped near 6.8100 and support anchored at 6.7900, short-term movements are expected to stay contained, limiting volatility across broader USD-linked Asian currency trading.
