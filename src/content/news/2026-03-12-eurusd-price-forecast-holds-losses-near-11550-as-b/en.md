EUR/USD remains under pressure, extending its losses for the third consecutive day, trading near the 1.1550 mark during Thursday’s European session. The currency pair is seeing a steady decline, driven by a bearish market sentiment that continues to overshadow the euro’s performance. The US dollar, supported by a strong economic outlook, maintains its dominance over the EUR, pushing the exchange rate lower.

Technical analysis suggests that EUR/USD is trapped within a descending channel pattern, reinforcing the prevailing negative bias. The pair’s downward trajectory is evident, with key support levels being tested around 1.1550. Traders will closely monitor the 1.1500 level, as a break below this could signal further losses for the euro against the USD in the near term.
