Gold (XAU/USD) surged to a five-week high, approaching $4,264, as market sentiment reacted to expectations of a Federal Reserve interest rate cut in the coming week. The precious metal gained over 0.40% during Monday's trading session, reflecting a broader trend driven by speculation surrounding monetary policy adjustments.

This rally in gold prices comes amid a fluctuating exchange rate environment, where traders are closely monitoring the implications for the USD. As the Fed's potential easing becomes more likely, investors may continue to pivot toward safe-haven assets like gold, influencing currency pair dynamics and potentially impacting the broader forex market.
