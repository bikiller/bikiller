Geopolitical uncertainties, coupled with changes in trader positioning, led to a notable drop in the greenback's value. The USD's downward movement is particularly evident in its trading against major currencies, highlighting broader market concerns. This shift in the USD's performance underscores the growing influence of external factors on its value in the forex market.
