USD/CNH is currently consolidating near its recent lows, with the exchange rate showing diminishing bullish momentum. The currency pair is trading cautiously as concerns over persistently high oil prices and geopolitical risks surrounding Iran weigh on broader market sentiment. Despite the International Energy Agency’s (IEA) release of oil reserves, these fears continue to exert pressure on global risk appetite, keeping the USD/CNH in a tight range.

Market participants are closely monitoring the evolving situation in the oil market, as elevated prices may push inflationary pressures and increase global uncertainty. The USD remains under mild pressure, while the Chinese yuan is facing resistance, suggesting that volatility in the oil market and ongoing geopolitical risks could influence the trading direction of USD/CNH in the near term. Traders will be looking for further clues from global events to gauge the next potential move for the pair.
