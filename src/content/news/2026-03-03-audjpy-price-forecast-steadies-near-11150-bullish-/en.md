The AUD/JPY currency pair is holding steady around 111.45 during the early European session on Tuesday, maintaining a cautious tone. The Australian Dollar (AUD) remains supported by hawkish rhetoric from the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA), which could continue to bolster its appeal against the Japanese Yen (JPY). The price action indicates that the market is awaiting further developments for direction.

A key technical level to watch is 111.50, with the bullish bias intact above this threshold. As long as AUD/JPY stays above the 50-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA), the outlook remains positive for the pair. Traders will be monitoring the broader market sentiment and potential RBA signals for further cues.
