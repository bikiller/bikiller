The US Dollar surged on Wednesday, with the US Dollar Index (DXY) climbing near the 99.00 level after the Federal Reserve held interest rates steady. The decision came during Chairman Jerome Powell's final meeting at the helm of the Fed. The market’s reaction reflects expectations of a hawkish stance, with the central bank signaling that it remains committed to its inflation-fighting strategy.

In currency pair movements, the EUR/USD traded lower as the USD strengthened, reflecting the broader dollar rally. The Fed's decision to maintain rates without any significant dovish hints supported the dollar’s rise, reinforcing market speculation that further tightening may be on the horizon. Traders are now eyeing any future signals from the central bank that could drive the USD even higher.
