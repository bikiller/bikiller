The EUR/JPY currency pair is experiencing modest losses, trading around 183.90 in the early European session on Tuesday. The Japanese Yen has gained strength against the Euro as rising geopolitical tensions in the Middle East have heightened demand for safe-haven assets. This shift reflects a broader market sentiment that favors stability amid uncertainty.

Despite the recent decline below the 184.00 threshold, the underlying uptrend for EUR/JPY remains intact, especially as it holds above the exponential moving average (EMA). Traders will be closely monitoring these levels, as a sustained movement below 183.90 could signal further bearish pressure, while a rebound could reinforce the bullish trend in this exchange rate.
