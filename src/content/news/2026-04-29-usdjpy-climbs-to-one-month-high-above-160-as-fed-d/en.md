The USD/JPY currency pair rose to a one-month high on Wednesday, trading around 160.25, marking a 0.40% gain on the day. This increase brings the exchange rate close to the March 30 peak of 160.46, reflecting continued strength in the U.S. dollar ahead of the Federal Reserve's upcoming decision. Traders are positioning for potential moves that could influence market sentiment.

The recent uptick in USD/JPY is tied to speculation surrounding the Fed's policy stance. As the central bank’s decision approaches, expectations of further tightening have bolstered the dollar's appeal against the yen. With the pair near key resistance levels, market participants will be watching closely for any hints of rate changes that could drive additional volatility in the exchange rate.
