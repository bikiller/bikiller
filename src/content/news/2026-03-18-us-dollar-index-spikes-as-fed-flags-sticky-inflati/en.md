The US Dollar Index (DXY) saw little movement early in the session, but surged sharply in the final hours following the Federal Reserve's latest interest rate decision. The Fed's stance on persistent inflation and uncertainty in crude oil markets drove the index to session highs, reflecting market concern over future rate hikes. The DXY closed near its peak, marking a significant shift in market sentiment.

In forex trading, the USD saw gains against key currencies, with the EUR/USD slipping below the 1.0700 mark, hitting lows not seen in several weeks. The dollar's strength was amplified by Fed Chair Jerome Powell’s comments, reinforcing expectations of tighter monetary policy to combat inflation. Traders closely monitored the impact on other major currency pairs as the US Dollar gained momentum, with implications for global market stability.
