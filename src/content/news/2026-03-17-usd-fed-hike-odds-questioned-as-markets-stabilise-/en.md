US rates saw a rally as market conditions stabilized, with the focus shifting to Federal Reserve policy and geopolitical developments. Although expectations for a Fed rate hike have increased, TD Securities analysts caution that a hawkish outcome is not as likely as a prolonged pause in tightening. The USD/EUR exchange rate held steady, while market participants remain cautious about the potential impact of Fed actions on trading in the near term.

Despite rising hike probabilities, TD Securities argues that the more probable scenario is a Fed pause rather than immediate tightening. This view contributed to a tempering of USD strength, as traders adjust positions in response to mixed economic signals. As geopolitical headlines continue to unfold, market participants are keeping an eye on any developments that could influence the Fed's next move.
