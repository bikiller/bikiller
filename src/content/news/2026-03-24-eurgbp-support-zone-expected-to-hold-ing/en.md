EUR/GBP remains under pressure despite significant drops in UK short-dated interest rates. ING's Chris Turner highlights that the currency pair is holding at a crucial technical support zone between 0.8600 and 0.8620, which traders anticipate will remain intact in the near term. Despite a weaker pound, this key support level is proving resilient in current trading conditions.

The EUR/GBP exchange rate has faced selling pressure, even with a decline in UK rates, showing a complex relationship between market sentiment and technical factors. With clear support levels at 0.8600/0.8620, the currency pair is expected to stabilize here unless there are significant shifts in either the Eurozone or UK economic data. The outlook remains cautious as traders monitor broader economic developments.
