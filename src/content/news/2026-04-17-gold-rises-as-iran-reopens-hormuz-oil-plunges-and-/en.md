The currency pair gains traction as investors reassess risk across USD-linked assets in global forex markets.

Lower oil prices reduce inflation expectations, strengthening bets on the Federal Reserve rate cuts. Traders in forex markets adjust USD outlook as easing price pressures improve odds of policy loosening. This shift supports non-yielding assets like gold, while influencing broader currency pairs and risk-sensitive flows across global markets. EUR and USD positioning remains sensitive to Fed expectations.

In FX trading, softer inflation dynamics and improved geopolitical supply outlook weigh on the USD while supporting gold against major currencies.
