The EUR/USD currency pair is experiencing a limited rebound following a gap lower on Monday, according to strategists from United Overseas Bank (UOB). After the initial dip, the exchange rate has regained some ground, but upward momentum appears constrained. The pair is expected to face resistance near 1.1805, with a key hurdle at 1.1840 that may limit further gains.

UOB's analysts maintain a neutral outlook on EUR/USD, noting the broad range-bound trading pattern. While there is potential for further recovery, any advance towards the 1.1805–1.1840 zone is likely to encounter significant selling pressure. Market participants are watching these technical levels closely, as a breakthrough above 1.1840 could signal a shift in the current range-bound trend.
