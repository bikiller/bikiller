The AUD/JPY currency pair has gained significant momentum, climbing to 101.70 following the release of stronger-than-expected Australian consumer inflation data. As traders react to the robust inflation figures, market sentiment has shifted, supporting the Aussie against the yen. This upward movement not only reflects current economic conditions but also sets the stage for potential challenges as the pair approaches key trading range hurdles. Observers will be keenly watching for how these developments influence overall market dynamics in the coming sessions.
