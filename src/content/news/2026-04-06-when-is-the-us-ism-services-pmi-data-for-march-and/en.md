The US ISM Services PMI data for March will be released today at 14:00 GMT, a key event that could impact the EUR/USD exchange rate. Analysts expect the figure to provide fresh insights into the strength of the US economy, which has been showing mixed signals recently. A stronger-than-expected reading may strengthen the USD, pushing EUR/USD lower, while a weaker result could put downward pressure on the greenback, giving the euro a potential boost.

Traders will be closely watching the PMI release, as it is a critical indicator of economic health, influencing market expectations for future Federal Reserve policy. A higher PMI could reinforce expectations of continued tightening from the Fed, supporting the USD. On the other hand, if the data disappoints, it could fuel speculation about a slowdown in US growth, potentially causing EUR/USD to recover toward key resistance levels. Market volatility is expected as traders position themselves ahead of the release.
