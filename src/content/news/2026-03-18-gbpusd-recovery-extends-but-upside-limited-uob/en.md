GBP/USD has shown signs of recovery, climbing to 1.3364. The currency pair may edge higher toward the 1.3410 resistance, but UOB’s Quek Ser Leang suggests that a decisive break above this level is unlikely, as the upward momentum remains modest. Traders are likely to see limited gains as the market lacks significant catalysts to push the pair beyond its current range.

On the downside, support levels for GBP/USD are seen at 1.3335 and 1.3320, with intraday price action expected to stay below 1.3390. As the exchange rate struggles to maintain bullish momentum, traders should remain cautious, with modest fluctuations anticipated. The pair’s movement largely reflects cautious sentiment amid ongoing market uncertainty.
