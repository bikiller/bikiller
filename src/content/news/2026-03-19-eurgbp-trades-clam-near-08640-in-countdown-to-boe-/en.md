The EUR/GBP currency pair is holding steady around 0.8640 during Thursday’s Asian session, with traders remaining cautious ahead of key central bank announcements. The exchange rate has been consolidating in a tight range, as market participants prepare for monetary policy decisions from both the Bank of England (BoE) and the European Central Bank (ECB). 

Investors are closely watching for any signals that could impact the EUR/GBP outlook, especially as both institutions are expected to make important statements regarding interest rates and economic outlooks. Any surprise moves from either central bank could lead to volatility in the currency pair, particularly in light of recent global economic uncertainty.
