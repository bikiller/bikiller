The Canadian Dollar (CAD) weakened slightly against the US Dollar (USD) ahead of key central bank decisions. The USD/CAD currency pair traded with modest gains around 1.3700 during early European hours on Wednesday. Market participants are adopting a cautious stance as they await the Federal Reserve (Fed) and Bank of Canada's (BoC) interest rate decisions later in the day. 

Traders are closely monitoring both central banks for any clues about future monetary policy direction. A potential rate hike by the Fed could support the USD further, while the BoC's stance remains pivotal in determining the CAD's near-term outlook. With volatility expected to increase, market sentiment will likely remain cautious until the outcome of both meetings is clear.
