The Pound Sterling strengthened during the European session on Tuesday, rising 0.17% to nearly 1.3200 against the US Dollar (USD). This movement comes as global markets adopt a risk-on sentiment following former President Donald Trump's call for peace, boosting investor confidence. The GBP/USD pair benefited from the broader positive market tone, maintaining upward momentum throughout the trading day.

While the US Dollar faced some pressure, the Pound's resilience was evident against both the USD and its European counterparts. The EUR/GBP exchange rate also saw some volatility, with the Euro struggling to keep pace with the Pound's gains. As market sentiment continues to favor riskier assets, the Pound’s performance stands out, potentially signaling further strength in the near term.
