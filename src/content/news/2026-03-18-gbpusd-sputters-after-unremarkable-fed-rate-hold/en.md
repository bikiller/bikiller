The US Dollar Index (DXY) showed little movement following the Federal Reserve's decision to hold interest rates steady on Wednesday. The highly anticipated announcement led to a brief period of stability in the currency markets, as traders digested the Fed's stance. As a result, the GBP/USD exchange rate struggled to make significant headway, holding steady around 1.2440. 

Market participants were left with little to fuel a directional move, with the Fed's neutral stance reinforcing the prevailing uncertainty in the market. The EUR/USD currency pair, similarly, remained range-bound, hovering near 1.0900 as traders awaited further signals. With the Fed signaling caution amid persistent inflation concerns, traders will likely stay cautious, keeping an eye on future economic data for any changes in market sentiment.
