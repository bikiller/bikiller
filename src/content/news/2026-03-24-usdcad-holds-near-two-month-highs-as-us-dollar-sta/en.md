The USD/CAD currency pair remains elevated, holding near two-month highs as the US Dollar strengthens despite weaker PMI data. On Tuesday, the Greenback made a slight recovery, offsetting some of the previous day’s losses. As of now, USD/CAD is trading at 1.3765, just shy of its recent peak, signaling ongoing strength in the USD.

The Canadian Dollar (CAD) continues to face pressure as the USD edges upward, benefiting from broader market dynamics. Despite the softer PMI numbers, which typically indicate a slowdown, the US Dollar's resilience supports the upward trend in USD/CAD. With the pair hovering near 1.3765, traders will be closely watching for any shifts in market sentiment or economic data that could influence further movement.
