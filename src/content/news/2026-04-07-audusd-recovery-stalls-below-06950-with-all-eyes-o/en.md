The Australian Dollar (AUD) saw a modest recovery against the US Dollar (USD) on Tuesday, rising for the second consecutive day. However, the currency pair struggled to maintain momentum, failing to break above the key resistance level of 0.6950. Despite the positive movement, the AUD/USD exchange rate faced a lack of strong bullish momentum, suggesting that traders remain cautious.

The market remains focused on global developments, particularly the ongoing tensions surrounding the Iran conflict, which are impacting risk sentiment. With investors eyeing geopolitical events, any significant news from the region could drive further volatility in the forex market. As a result, the AUD/USD pair is expected to remain sensitive to external factors, with market participants closely monitoring for signs of a breakout or reversal around the 0.6950 level.
