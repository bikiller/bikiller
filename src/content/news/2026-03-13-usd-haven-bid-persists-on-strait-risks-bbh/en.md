The US Dollar remains buoyed as geopolitical tensions in the Strait of Hormuz continue to elevate risks, particularly for global shipping. Coupled with rising oil prices, which have pushed Brent crude above $100, the USD is benefiting from a haven bid. Stagflation concerns further intensify the pressure on stocks and bonds, reinforcing the Dollar's strength near its cyclical highs.

In the forex market, the USD's resilience is evident against major currencies like the EUR and JPY. Trading near these elevated levels, the USD's dominance is likely to persist as the economic outlook remains uncertain. Investors are focusing on the interplay between energy prices and broader market sentiment, with the Dollar poised to hold its ground in the face of global risks.
