The US Dollar (USD) is currently under pressure against major currencies, reflecting a defensive trading stance. Analysts indicate that the USD could drift toward the lower end of its trading range established from June to December. This shift comes amid fluctuating investor sentiment and market uncertainties that have influenced currency pairs.

As the USD weakens, the euro (EUR) and other key currencies are gaining ground, impacting exchange rates across the board. The current market dynamics suggest that traders should closely monitor these movements, as a sustained decline in the USD might lead to increased volatility in currency pairs, affecting overall forex trading strategies.
