Silver edged higher in the North American session, with the XAG/USD currency pair climbing to $75.85, marking a gain of more than 3%. Traders noted the formation of a bullish harami candlestick pattern, signaling potential short-term strength and a possible continuation toward the $80 resistance level. The exchange rate reflects renewed appetite for metals amid a softer USD backdrop.  

Market participants are eyeing technical cues, with the bullish harami suggesting that silver’s upward momentum may persist. The recent trading action comes as investors weigh economic data and currency movements, particularly fluctuations in the EUR/USD and broader USD indices. Momentum traders are positioning for further gains, using the pattern as confirmation of a near-term trend reversal.  

A break above $80 could trigger additional buying, reinforcing the currency pair’s bullish outlook. Analysts are monitoring how the USD behaves in response to interest rate expectations, which could influence silver’s appeal as a hedge. With volatility in global markets, the interplay between metals and currency trading remains a key factor for XAG/USD, shaping both short-term swings and strategic positions.
