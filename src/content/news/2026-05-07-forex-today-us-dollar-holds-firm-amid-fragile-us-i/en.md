The US Dollar maintained a firm stance near the 98.10 level on the US Dollar Index (DXY) as traders navigated the volatile landscape of US-Iran peace discussions. Despite softening slightly from earlier highs, the USD held resilience against major currencies, with the EUR/USD hovering just below key resistance near 1.0850. Market participants weighed headlines on a potential agreement, which continue to drive short-term swings in the forex market.  

In currency pair trading, the dollar’s relative strength reflected cautious positioning amid uncertainty. Investors monitored USD/JPY, which remained anchored near 150.20, and GBP/USD, showing limited upside as broader risk sentiment fluctuated. Analysts note that even tentative developments in diplomatic talks can prompt sharp reactions across exchange rates, especially for pairs sensitive to geopolitical risk.  

Technically, the DXY’s support near 97.90 is seen as a crucial level, while resistance around 98.30 could cap further gains if headlines fail to deliver clarity. Forex traders remain alert, balancing hedges and speculative positions, as any confirmed US-Iran progress could trigger rapid adjustments across USD, EUR, and other major currency pairs, underscoring the interconnectedness of global trading flows.
