Market participants are closely monitoring the Fed's stance, as any changes in monetary policy could impact the USD's strength against the Australian Dollar. As the anticipation grows, the trading landscape for the AUD/USD remains uncertain, with investors ready to react to potential shifts in the Fed's outlook.
