This sideways movement may reflect market participants reassessing their positions after the recent gains. The Australian dollar's performance has been influenced by broader economic trends and trader sentiment towards the USD. Investors now watch closely for any catalysts that could reignite volatility and drive the currency pair back towards its recent high or initiate a new trend.
