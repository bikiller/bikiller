The Australian Dollar (AUD) surged against the US Dollar (USD) on Wednesday, gaining 0.72% as market sentiment turned more positive. The AUD/USD exchange rate climbed above the key 0.7170 level, bolstered by rising hopes of a de-escalation in global conflicts. This shift in risk appetite supported broader market optimism, with oil prices stabilizing, as WTI crude held firm above $91 despite a 0.80% decline.

At the time of writing, the AUD/USD pair was trading at 0.7173. The upbeat sentiment has helped the Australian Dollar maintain its upward momentum, supported by strong demand for riskier assets. While oil's relatively stable price capped losses, investors continued to react to the shifting geopolitical landscape, which remains a crucial driver for both the AUD and broader currency markets in the short term.
