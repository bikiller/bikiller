Gold (XAU/USD) has held steady above the $5,000 mark as geopolitical tensions in the Middle East continue to weigh on the market. After a brief dip earlier in the session, the precious metal regained its footing, trading just above this key psychological level during the first half of Wednesday's European session. The ongoing uncertainty surrounding the region has prompted traders to seek safe-haven assets like gold, contributing to its resilience.

The market is also bracing for the Federal Reserve's upcoming decision, which could add further volatility to the XAU/USD pair. The potential for rate hikes or dovish signals from the Fed remains a key focus for investors, influencing the exchange rate between gold and the USD. As traders await more clarity, gold's ability to maintain levels above $5,000 underscores its role as a safe-haven asset amid broader market uncertainties.
