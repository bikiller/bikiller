AUD/USD has broken out of a period of consolidation, entering a strong uptrend supported by its 50-day moving average (DMA). The currency pair's bullish momentum has sparked optimism, with analysts predicting a potential return toward the 0.72 level. The steady advance indicates confidence in the Aussie dollar's performance against the USD.

Key upside targets for AUD/USD are now positioned at 0.7220/0.7250, with a more ambitious forecast suggesting a move towards 0.7400. These levels represent critical resistance zones that could define the next phase of the pair's trading trajectory, influenced by ongoing market sentiment and global economic factors. The bullish outlook remains intact as long as the pair stays above its 50-DMA.
