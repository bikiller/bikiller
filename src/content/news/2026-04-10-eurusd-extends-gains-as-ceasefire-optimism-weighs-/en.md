EUR/USD continued its upward trajectory on Friday, extending gains for a fifth consecutive session as the currency pair benefited from a shift in market sentiment. Traders reacted positively to the announcement of a US-Iran ceasefire, which bolstered risk appetite and weighed on the US Dollar. Despite firm US CPI data, the Euro managed to edge higher, with the exchange rate hovering near recent multi-week highs.  

The currency pair found support amid broad USD weakness, reflecting investors’ focus on geopolitical developments over domestic inflation signals. Market participants noted that while US inflation remained resilient, the Greenback struggled against a backdrop of easing tensions, prompting increased trading activity in the Euro. Technical analysts highlighted key resistance levels that may influence EUR/USD momentum in the coming sessions.  

Looking ahead, the Euro’s performance against the USD could remain sensitive to both risk sentiment and economic data releases. Traders are closely watching how further developments in international relations and US inflation metrics impact the exchange rate. Momentum in the EUR/USD currency pair suggests continued trading interest, with market positioning likely to adjust as investors balance geopolitical optimism against the backdrop of firm US economic indicators.
