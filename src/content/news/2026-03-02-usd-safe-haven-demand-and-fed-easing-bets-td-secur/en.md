The US Dollar (USD) is seeing increased demand as a safe-haven currency amid rising geopolitical tensions linked to the Iranian conflict. TD Securities’ Global Strategy Team forecasts that these uncertainties will drive risk-off sentiment, with investors flocking to the USD. Early trading has shown broad-based, though measured, buying of the Dollar, particularly in pairs such as USD/JPY and EUR/USD.

In addition to geopolitical concerns, expectations for further easing by the Federal Reserve are growing, fueling speculation around lower interest rates. This outlook could put downward pressure on the Euro (EUR) and support the USD in the near term. Traders will be closely monitoring key technical levels for EUR/USD, with a potential test of the 1.0800 support level should the Dollar continue to gain traction.
