The Bank of England's March decision is now finely balanced, according to Nomura’s European rates team. Higher oil prices and stronger-than-expected UK data have shifted market expectations, making the possibility of a rate cut more uncertain. The GBP/USD exchange rate, which had been under pressure in recent weeks, is likely to experience heightened volatility as traders adjust positions ahead of the BoE's meeting.

Stronger UK economic data, combined with rising oil prices, has supported the pound, pushing the GBP/EUR exchange rate to test key levels. Markets are now recalibrating, weighing the likelihood of the central bank acting against a backdrop of mixed economic signals. A rate cut from the BoE, once considered a certainty, now seems less clear, which could lead to increased uncertainty in GBP trading in the coming weeks.
