This retreat comes amid fluctuating market sentiment and cautious trading patterns, prompting investors to closely monitor the CAD's performance against major currency pairs. The CAD's ability to maintain its strength will be tested as it approaches these technical levels, with potential implications for broader market dynamics in the coming weeks.
