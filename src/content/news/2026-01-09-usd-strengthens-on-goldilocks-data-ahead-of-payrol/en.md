The US Dollar (USD) is gaining momentum against major currencies, bolstered by a slight upward revision in expectations for US interest rates. As a result, the USD has shown resilience in currency pairs, notably against the Euro (EUR) and British Pound (GBP). Analysts at BBH highlight this trend, indicating that the USD's exchange rate remains favorable, setting the stage for potential further gains ahead of upcoming payroll data. This shift in market dynamics reflects a broader confidence in the US economy, positioning the USD firmly in the spotlight for traders.
