The US Dollar remains steady just below the 100 level as market sentiment fluctuates amidst heightened geopolitical tensions. Initial reports of potential ceasefire talks eased market anxiety, causing a brief uptick in risk appetite. However, uncertainty returned when Iran expressed reluctance to engage with the United States, keeping traders cautious. The USD/JPY hovered near 132.50, maintaining its firm stance while traders awaited further developments.

The EUR/USD traded with limited volatility, holding just above the 1.0600 mark, as concerns over US-Iran relations capped significant movements. Geopolitical risks continue to weigh on market sentiment, preventing a clear directional shift. As the dollar holds its ground, the broader FX market remains sensitive to any news regarding the evolving situation, with traders focused on any potential escalation or diplomatic breakthroughs that could sway the trading landscape.
