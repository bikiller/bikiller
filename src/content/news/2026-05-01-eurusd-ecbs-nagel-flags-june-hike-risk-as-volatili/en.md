Bundesbank President Joachim Nagel has signaled a likely rate hike from the European Central Bank (ECB) in June unless the economic outlook improves. His comments underscore a hawkish stance from the ECB, which has kept the EUR/USD currency pair on edge. Currently, the exchange rate for EUR/USD is holding steady as market volatility remains muted, with traders cautiously positioning themselves ahead of potential ECB action.

The prospect of a rate hike has strengthened the euro against the USD, though the lack of significant market volatility suggests traders are waiting for further signals before taking larger positions. With the euro still hovering near key technical levels, traders will be closely watching upcoming ECB meetings for further guidance. If the rate hike materializes, it could provide further support to the EUR/USD exchange rate.
