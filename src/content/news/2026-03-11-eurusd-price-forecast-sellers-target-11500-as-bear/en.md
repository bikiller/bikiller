The EUR/USD currency pair continued to weaken on Wednesday, with the Euro slipping against the US Dollar as the Greenback maintains strong support. The ongoing geopolitical tensions, particularly the US-Iran conflict, have fostered a cautious market sentiment, bolstering demand for safe-haven assets like the USD. This has put downward pressure on the EUR/USD exchange rate, now hovering near key support levels.

Bearish momentum is growing, and traders are eyeing the next significant technical target at 1.1500. If the pair breaks through this level, it could signal further downside potential. With market sentiment still driven by uncertainty and the USD holding firm, the Euro faces challenges in regaining strength against its American counterpart in the near term.
