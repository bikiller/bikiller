The USD/CAD currency pair edged slightly higher during Tuesday’s European trading session, hovering around 1.3750. Traders appear cautious as the pair stabilizes near the 50% Fibonacci retracement level at 1.3760, suggesting a potential pivot point for further moves. Market participants are closely monitoring price action after recent volatility, with the exchange rate showing a modest upward bias against the backdrop of broader USD trading dynamics.

Investor attention is turning to Canada’s upcoming CPI data, which could drive significant fluctuations in the USD/CAD exchange rate. Analysts highlight that technical support and resistance levels are increasingly guiding short-term trading decisions.

Across global markets, USD performance versus major currencies such as the EUR and GBP continues to influence USD/CAD movements.
