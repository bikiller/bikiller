The Australian Bureau of Statistics will release February's trade data on Thursday at 00:30 GMT. The trade surplus is projected to narrow to $2.5 billion, down from $2.63 billion in January. This shift could influence the AUD/USD exchange rate, with traders closely watching for any surprises that could affect the Australian dollar's performance against the USD.

A smaller surplus might signal weakening export growth, potentially putting pressure on the AUD. Should the actual data come in below expectations, the AUD/USD pair could face a downward adjustment, testing support levels. On the other hand, any better-than-expected result could boost the Australian dollar, leading to potential resistance at recent highs. Market participants will be keenly focused on this release for further direction in AUD/USD trading.
