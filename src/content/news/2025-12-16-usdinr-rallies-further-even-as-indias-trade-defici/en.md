The USD/INR currency pair continued its upward trajectory on Tuesday, marking the fourth consecutive day of losses for the Indian Rupee against the US Dollar. Despite a reported shrinkage in India's trade deficit, the exchange rate for USD/INR has seen increased momentum, pushing the pair higher as traders react to broader market sentiments and economic indicators.

As the USD strengthens, market participants are closely monitoring key technical levels. The current exchange rate reflects a robust demand for the Greenback, overshadowing any positive developments in India's trade balance. The ongoing volatility in forex trading highlights the challenges faced by the INR, raising questions about its resilience amidst global economic fluctuations.
