Isabel Schnabel, a member of the European Central Bank's executive board, warned on Wednesday about the need to remain cautious regarding inflation risks in Europe. Speaking at the Frankfurt School of Finance and Management, Schnabel highlighted the importance of closely monitoring the ongoing energy price shock, which continues to put upward pressure on inflation. The ECB must stay vigilant to avoid further market instability, which could impact the EUR/USD exchange rate.

As inflation concerns persist, traders are keeping a close eye on the ECB's policy stance and its impact on the euro. The EUR/USD pair has fluctuated in recent sessions, responding to inflation data and expectations of potential interest rate hikes. With inflationary risks still high, especially from energy costs, the euro's performance could see significant volatility against the USD in the coming weeks, depending on the ECB’s next steps.
