US President Donald Trump sparked market movements on Good Friday, asserting on Truth Social that the US could easily reopen the Strait of Hormuz, seize oil, and generate substantial profits. This bold statement has added volatility to the forex market, with the USD gaining strength against major currencies like the EUR. As traders digested the comments, the EUR/USD pair saw a brief dip, fluctuating around 1.0800 before stabilizing.

The speculation surrounding Trump’s remarks has created short-term uncertainty in energy markets, impacting the USD’s performance. Crude oil prices reacted, edging higher as concerns about potential geopolitical tensions rose. This development has pushed the USD further up against the JPY, with USD/JPY reaching levels near 134.50. With markets on edge, traders are now focusing on any further signals from the US government regarding Middle East policies, knowing such statements could continue to affect the exchange rate and global risk sentiment.
