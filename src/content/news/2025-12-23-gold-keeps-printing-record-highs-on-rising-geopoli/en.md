Gold (XAU/USD) surged to near record highs during Asian trading on Tuesday, reflecting a strong increase in demand for safe-haven assets amid rising geopolitical tensions and economic uncertainty. The precious metal has appreciated by 10% over the past month and is up nearly 70% in 2025, showcasing its appeal as investors seek refuge from market volatility.

This robust performance in gold prices is influencing currency markets, particularly the USD, as traders adjust their positions in response to shifting economic sentiments. With ongoing Fed rate cut expectations, the exchange rate dynamics may continue to favor gold, potentially leading to further upward movements as geopolitical factors and economic indicators evolve.
