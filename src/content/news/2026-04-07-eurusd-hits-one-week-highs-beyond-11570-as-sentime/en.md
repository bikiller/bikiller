The EUR/USD currency pair surged past the 1.1570 mark on Tuesday morning, hitting a one-week high as sentiment improved across European markets. The Euro regained strength, bouncing back from earlier losses to extend its recovery against the US Dollar. The exchange rate reached new weekly peaks, signaling renewed confidence in the single currency.

This upward movement comes amid a more optimistic market outlook, with traders reacting to positive economic signals from the Eurozone. The Euro’s advance against the USD reflects a shift in risk sentiment, as the Dollar’s recent strength begins to soften. Investors are now focused on the potential for further gains in the EUR/USD pair, with technical levels above 1.1570 offering key resistance in the short term.
