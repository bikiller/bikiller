USD/CHF remains under pressure for the fourth consecutive day, trading near 0.7910 during early Asian trading on Thursday. The Swiss Franc (CHF) has strengthened against the US Dollar (USD) as market participants seek safety amid ongoing uncertainty surrounding the US-Iran ceasefire situation. The persistent geopolitical tension continues to weigh on the greenback, boosting demand for safer assets like the CHF.

The exchange rate for USD/CHF is now hovering close to the 0.7900 level, with the currency pair showing little signs of reversing its downward trajectory. Traders are closely monitoring developments in the US-Iran standoff, as any escalation or resolution could significantly impact market sentiment. As long as these risks persist, the Swiss Franc is likely to maintain its position as a preferred safe-haven currency in the near term.
