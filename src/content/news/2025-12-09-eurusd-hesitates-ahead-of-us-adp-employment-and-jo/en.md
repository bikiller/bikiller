EUR/USD is currently facing downward pressure as it trades at 1.1635, following a failure to regain the key resistance level of 1.1650. This move has resulted in a negative shift on the daily chart for the currency pair, indicating a potential shift in sentiment among traders. 

Market participants are closely monitoring upcoming US ADP employment and JOLTS jobs data, which could significantly impact the exchange rate. Any surprising results from these reports may lead to increased volatility in the USD, further influencing the EUR/USD trading dynamics. Investors remain cautious as they anticipate these critical economic indicators.
