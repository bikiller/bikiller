The EUR/USD currency pair is holding steady above the 1.1500 mark, trading near 1.1520 during the early Asian session on Friday. Traders are closely watching the U.S. PCE inflation data set to be released later today, which could impact the exchange rate. The euro is showing resilience, supported by expectations that the European Central Bank may keep tightening its policy stance.

However, the pair's potential for further gains appears limited as geopolitical tensions in the Middle East continue to escalate. Such uncertainty may dampen investor sentiment, putting pressure on the euro against the stronger U.S. dollar. Despite the positive technical levels, market participants remain cautious ahead of the economic data and global risk events.
