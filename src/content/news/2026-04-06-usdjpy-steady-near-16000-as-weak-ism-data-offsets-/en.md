The USD/JPY currency pair remained stable on Monday, trading just below the 160.00 mark. The exchange rate edged up by a marginal 0.1%, closing near 159.60. This limited movement came amidst a quiet market session, with traders awaiting the upcoming release of key US economic data.

Weakness in the ISM manufacturing index data weighed on the USD, offsetting any momentum from geopolitical concerns. Despite these external factors, the yen held steady, and market participants appeared cautious, focusing on the potential impact of future US reports on the broader market sentiment. The exchange rate’s stability indicates a wait-and-see approach before more decisive trading occurs.
