Silver (XAG/USD) is trading around $74.10 on Monday, down 2.23% on the day. The metal briefly hit an intraday low near $72.61, as a stronger US Dollar (USD) pressured the market. Geopolitical tensions, driven by the collapse of US-Iran talks, have intensified demand for the greenback, further weighing on silver prices. 

The rise in oil prices is adding to inflation concerns, fueling uncertainty in the global economy. This, coupled with a robust USD, is pushing traders away from precious metals like silver. The outlook remains volatile, with the XAG/USD pair likely to stay under pressure as investors react to the broader economic and geopolitical climate.
