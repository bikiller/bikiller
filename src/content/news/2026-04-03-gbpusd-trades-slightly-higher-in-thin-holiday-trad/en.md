GBP/USD traded slightly higher on Friday, with the British Pound gaining modest ground against the US Dollar. The currency pair hovered near the 1.2470 mark, as the USD softened ahead of key U.S. economic data. Thin holiday trading conditions contributed to a subdued market, with volatility remaining low. 

The Greenback faced pressure amid expectations surrounding the U.S. Non-Farm Payrolls (NFP) report, due later in the day. Traders are eyeing the employment data for insights into the health of the U.S. economy and its potential impact on Federal Reserve policy. Despite the slight GBP gain, broader market direction remains uncertain, with the exchange rate continuing to trade within a narrow range.
