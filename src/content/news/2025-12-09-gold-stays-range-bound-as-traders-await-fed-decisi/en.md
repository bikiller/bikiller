Gold (XAU/USD) remains range-bound as it trades steadily in a narrow pattern, reflecting the cautious sentiment among investors ahead of the Federal Reserve's interest rate decision on Wednesday. As traders await clarity on monetary policy, the exchange rate for gold has shown little movement, maintaining stability despite fluctuations in other markets.

The anticipation surrounding the Fed's announcement is keeping traders on the sidelines, influencing the overall trading sentiment in the precious metals market. With the focus on USD movements and potential impacts on the EUR/USD currency pair, investors are closely monitoring any signs that may suggest shifts in interest rates or economic outlook that could affect gold's appeal as a safe haven.
