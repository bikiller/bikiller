EUR/USD has staged a notable rebound, climbing from 1.1446 to 1.1563, signaling renewed trading interest in the currency pair. Analysts at UOB, Quek Ser Leang and Lee Sue Ann, note that the exchange rate’s current momentum suggests potential gains toward 1.1595. However, upside remains capped, with 1.1620 identified as a strong resistance level that could temper further advances in the near term.  

The recent bounce in the euro against the USD reflects a combination of technical support and market positioning, as traders adjust to short-term fluctuations. The trading pattern indicates an upside bias, but volatility is likely to remain contained within the established range. Market participants are watching key levels closely, with the 1.1560–1.1595 zone seen as critical for sustaining bullish momentum.  

UOB highlights that while EUR/USD has room for incremental gains, the broader trend remains range-bound. Traders may consider taking profits near resistance at 1.1620 or monitoring pullbacks for entry opportunities. The exchange rate’s behavior underscores the delicate balance between EUR strength and USD resilience, shaping trading strategies and short-term market outlooks for the currency pair.
