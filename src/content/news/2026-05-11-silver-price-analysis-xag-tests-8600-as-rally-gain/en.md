Silver (XAG/USD) surged over 7% on Monday, breaking through critical technical resistance at $83.05, the daily high from April 17. This rally also surpassed the psychological level of $85.00, paving the way for a significant test at $86.00 per troy ounce, marking its highest point in two months.

The rally in silver prices can be attributed to a combination of strong demand and favorable market conditions, including fluctuations in the USD and broader economic indicators. As the currency pair gains momentum, traders are closely monitoring key resistance levels, with the potential for further upward movement if buying trends persist in the coming sessions.
