Gold prices faced pressure at the start of the week, with Spot Gold (XAU/USD) opening slightly lower. The precious metal struggled to hold onto the $4,600 level, as it encountered resistance amid growing market concerns. In the early hours of the Asian session, the XAU/USD pair battled to maintain its footing, signaling potential volatility ahead.

The decline comes as fears mount over global economic uncertainty, impacting investor sentiment. Traders are closely monitoring the $4,600 support level, with a break below potentially signaling further downside risk. The USD’s strength has also weighed on gold, as the currency pair struggles to recover from recent losses. If the $4,600 mark fails to hold, further declines could materialize, particularly if broader market trends continue to favor the USD.
