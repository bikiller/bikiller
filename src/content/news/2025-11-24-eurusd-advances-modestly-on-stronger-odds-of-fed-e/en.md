EUR/USD made modest gains on Monday, advancing over 0.10% to trade at 1.1525, following dovish remarks from Federal Reserve officials. The exchange rate experienced a peak of 1.1550 during the session, reflecting increased expectations for a potential rate cut by the Fed. 

As the US Dollar consolidates amid these developments, traders are closely monitoring the implications of the Fed's stance for future monetary policy. The currency pair's movement indicates a cautious optimism among investors regarding the euro's resilience against the dollar, as market sentiment shifts in anticipation of potential easing measures.
