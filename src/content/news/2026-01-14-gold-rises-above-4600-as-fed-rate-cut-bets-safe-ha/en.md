Gold (XAU/USD) has climbed above $4,600, approaching its record high of $4,634.64 set in the previous session. As of Wednesday, the precious metal is trading around $4,620.00 per troy ounce. This surge is primarily driven by increasing bets on a potential rate cut by the Federal Reserve, alongside a growing demand for safe-haven assets amid economic uncertainty.

The rising price of gold reflects broader market sentiments as investors seek refuge in tangible assets in light of fluctuating currency pairs and concerns over the strength of the USD. With the ongoing speculation around monetary policy adjustments, market participants are closely monitoring gold's performance as it navigates near historical highs, positioning itself as a critical element in trading strategies.
