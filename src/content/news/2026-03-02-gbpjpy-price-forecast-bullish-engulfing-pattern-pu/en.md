GBP/JPY traded higher during the North American session, rising by 0.24% after recovering from a low of 209.35. The move came amid risk aversion, driven by escalating tensions between the US and Iran in the Middle East. At the time of writing, the pair is testing the key 211.00 level, which could open the door for further bullish momentum.

The current exchange rate of 210.98 suggests that the GBP/JPY is nearing a critical technical threshold. A bullish engulfing pattern has formed on the charts, potentially signaling continued upward movement. Market participants will closely monitor any developments around 211.00, as a clear break above this level could pave the way for a push towards higher targets.
