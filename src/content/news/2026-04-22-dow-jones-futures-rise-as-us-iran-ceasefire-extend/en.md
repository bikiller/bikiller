Dow Jones futures climbed 0.59% to surpass 49,600, reflecting positive sentiment in the markets as the US-Iran ceasefire continues. This momentum extended to the S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 futures, which rose 0.63% and 0.81%, respectively, approaching levels of 7,150 and 26,850. Traders are closely monitoring these developments as they could influence currency pairs, particularly the USD against the EUR.

The ongoing ceasefire has alleviated some geopolitical tensions, providing a boost to investor confidence ahead of the regular trading hours in the U.S. This renewed optimism may impact the exchange rates, specifically how the USD trades against other major currencies. Market participants are likely to remain vigilant for further updates that could sway trading strategies and shape the direction of the forex market.
