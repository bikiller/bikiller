USD/JPY remained largely flat on Monday, closing near 159.40 after a quiet session. The currency pair traded within a narrow 75-pip range, fluctuating between 159.10 and 159.85. The market showed little directional bias as traders awaited key central bank decisions. The session’s small-bodied candles highlighted indecision, with participants cautious ahead of the Bank of Japan's policy announcement on Tuesday.

The trading environment was marked by uncertainty, as the USD/JPY exchange rate faces potential volatility in the coming days. Market focus is squarely on the BoJ's upcoming decision, which could shape the pair’s next move. The broader sentiment suggests that traders are hesitant to take strong positions before the twin policy meetings of the BoJ and the Federal Reserve later this week, keeping the market range-bound for now.
