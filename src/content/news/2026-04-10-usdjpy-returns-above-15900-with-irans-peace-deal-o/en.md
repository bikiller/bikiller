The USD/JPY currency pair is trading above 159.00 on Friday, maintaining a steady position near the key 160.00 level. The relative stability of the US Dollar reflects shifting investor sentiment amid concerns over the fragile ceasefire in Iran. As tensions remain high, traders have scaled back on their US dollar shorts, influencing the exchange rate dynamics.

With the US Dollar strengthening, the market is closely monitoring developments surrounding Iran’s peace deal. Any deterioration in the ceasefire could further bolster the USD as investors seek safe-haven assets. This ongoing uncertainty in geopolitical events has kept the USD/JPY trading near critical resistance, potentially paving the way for more volatility in the coming sessions.
