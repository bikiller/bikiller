West Texas Intermediate (WTI) Crude Oil prices remain stable as trading activity slows with the onset of the holiday season. The current market environment indicates cautious optimism among traders, with WTI poised for potential gains against a backdrop of fluctuating demand and geopolitical factors. However, any significant dip below the $60 threshold could trigger renewed selling pressure.
