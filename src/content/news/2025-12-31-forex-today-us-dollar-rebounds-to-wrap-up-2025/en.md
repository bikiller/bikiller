The US Dollar experienced a strong rebound as trading closed for 2025, with the USD/EUR exchange rate rising significantly. This upward trend was largely driven by robust economic data from the US, which bolstered confidence in the greenback.

Traders reacted positively to the announcements of improved employment figures and inflation rates, prompting a wave of buying in USD-denominated assets. The increased demand for the dollar suggests a shift in trading strategies, as investors anticipate further tightening of monetary policy by the Federal Reserve. As the year concludes, the market will closely monitor these developments for their implications on future currency pair movements.
