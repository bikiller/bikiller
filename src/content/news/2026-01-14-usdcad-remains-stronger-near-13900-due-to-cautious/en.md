The USD/CAD currency pair continues to demonstrate strength, trading around the 1.3900 level during the Asian session on Wednesday. Traders are closely monitoring economic indicators and Fed commentary as these factors may shape the future direction of USD. The current trading activity suggests a robust demand for the U.S. dollar against the Canadian dollar, indicating a preference for the greenback amid ongoing market uncertainties. Investors will be keen to see if this momentum can be sustained in the coming sessions.
