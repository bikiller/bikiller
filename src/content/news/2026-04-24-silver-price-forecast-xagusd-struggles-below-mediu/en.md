Silver (XAG/USD) is trading flat on Friday, maintaining a cautious stance as it struggles to break through resistance levels. The exchange rate remains pressured by oil-driven inflation, a situation exacerbated by ongoing tensions between the U.S. and Iran. These geopolitical factors have heightened market expectations for a sustained period of higher interest rates, adding to the bearish sentiment surrounding silver.

As traders navigate the current market landscape, silver's performance remains tethered below its medium-term simple moving averages (SMAs). The lack of clear direction highlights the challenges the precious metal faces in overcoming the psychological barriers set by inflationary concerns and interest rate forecasts. Investors will be closely monitoring these developments as they could significantly influence trading strategies in the days ahead.
