Gold prices have extended their upward movement for the third consecutive session, surpassing the $4,700 per ounce mark. This rally comes amid growing expectations that the conflict in the Middle East may soon come to an end, potentially boosting investor confidence. The surge in gold prices reflects increasing demand for safe-haven assets, with traders closely monitoring geopolitical developments.

While gold's rally has been notable, analysts caution that the precious metal remains vulnerable to fluctuations in global sentiment. The strength of the USD, particularly in relation to major currency pairs like EUR/USD, could limit further gains. Additionally, market participants are keeping an eye on any potential shifts in monetary policy from the Federal Reserve, which could influence gold's outlook in the coming weeks.
