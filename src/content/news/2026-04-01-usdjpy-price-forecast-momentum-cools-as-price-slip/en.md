The USD/JPY currency pair is experiencing a period of consolidation on Wednesday, trading in a tight range as the Japanese Yen (JPY) struggles to gain ground despite a softer US Dollar (USD). At the time of writing, the exchange rate stands at 158.50, having retreated from the 160.00 level reached earlier this week. The price movement highlights a cooling momentum after a strong rally earlier in the week.

The failure of the JPY to capitalize on the USD's pullback has resulted in limited trading action. While the USD remains relatively weak, it is not enough to push the exchange rate lower as the pair stabilizes just above its short-term average. Traders are closely monitoring any further developments that could drive a breakout from the current range.
