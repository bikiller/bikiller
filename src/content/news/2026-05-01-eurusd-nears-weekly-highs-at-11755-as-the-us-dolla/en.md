The EUR/USD pair is edging closer to its weekly high, currently trading at 1.1742, just shy of 1.1755, the week’s peak. The Euro has gained traction against a weakening US Dollar on Friday, benefiting from the USD’s ongoing struggles in the market. With the pair holding near the top of the weekly range, the Euro is showing resilience.

The US Dollar faltered due to a combination of factors, including a lack of significant domestic data and a more cautious tone from investors. This weakness has allowed the Euro to gain momentum, as traders move to capitalize on the relative strength of the European currency. The EUR/USD’s ability to approach 1.1755 suggests that traders are positioning themselves for potential further upside in the near term.
