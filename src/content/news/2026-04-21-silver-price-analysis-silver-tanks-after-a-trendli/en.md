Silver (XAG/USD) experienced a significant decline of nearly 3.50% on Tuesday, following a critical break below its support trendline. This move came after the market printed a doji pattern on Monday, signaling uncertainty among traders. The pullback has raised concerns about potential further losses, with analysts now focusing on the possibility of prices approaching the $70 mark.

The recent volatility in silver prices is partly attributed to the ongoing tension surrounding US-Iran negotiations, which has left investors wary. As the market grapples with these geopolitical dynamics, the exchange rate movements in precious metals like silver are likely to remain sensitive to any developments. Traders are advised to closely monitor these factors as they navigate the shifting landscape in the forex market.
