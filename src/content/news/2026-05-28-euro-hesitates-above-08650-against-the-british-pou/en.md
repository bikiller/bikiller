The Euro (EUR) is showing limited movement against the British Pound (GBP) on Thursday, trading just above the 0.8650 level. The EUR/GBP currency pair has struggled to sustain gains above 0.8660 following a 0.4% rally over the past two sessions. Despite attempts to push lower, support remains firm near 0.8655, keeping the exchange rate range-bound amid heightened market caution.  

Geopolitical tensions in the Middle East are influencing trading sentiment, prompting investors to weigh risk alongside technical levels. The flat movement in EUR/GBP highlights the market’s uncertainty, with traders reluctant to commit ahead of potential developments. USD strength and broader currency flows are also affecting pair dynamics, contributing to the pair’s restrained momentum.  

Analysts suggest that while the EUR bulls have shown resilience, breaking above 0.8660 convincingly will be key for a renewed upside. On the downside, maintaining support above 0.8655 is crucial to avoid a deeper retracement. Forex participants are closely watching these thresholds, as any decisive move could impact short-term trading strategies and broader EUR and GBP positioning across currency markets.
