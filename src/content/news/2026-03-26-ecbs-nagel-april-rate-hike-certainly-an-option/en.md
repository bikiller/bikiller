ECB policymaker and Bundesbank chief Joachim Nagel sparked market speculation on Thursday, signaling that an interest rate hike in the upcoming April policy meeting is "certainly an option." His comments came during European trading hours, causing the EUR/USD pair to see a brief uptick. As the market digests these hawkish remarks, the euro strengthens, maintaining key support levels above 1.0700.

The potential for tightening monetary policy from the ECB has traders closely watching the EUR/USD exchange rate. With inflation pressures lingering in the Eurozone, the anticipation of a rate hike could further drive the euro higher, potentially pushing the currency pair toward the 1.0800 resistance mark. Investors are now factoring in an increased probability of a rate increase, which may influence broader market trends in the coming weeks.
