EUR/USD is currently testing two-and-a-half-month highs at 1.1765 as traders position themselves ahead of the important US employment and consumption data due for release. This movement has occurred despite the recent disappointing preliminary Manufacturing and Services Purchasing Managers Indexes (PMIs), which typically impact market sentiment negatively.

The currency pair has demonstrated resilience, continuing its broader bullish trend as the market looks to assess the implications of upcoming economic indicators. With the USD under pressure, traders are keenly watching how the US data will influence the exchange rate and potentially shape future trading strategies.
