The US Dollar (USD) strengthened as traders positioned themselves ahead of the holidays, bolstered by a Trump-imposed blockade on Venezuelan oil. This geopolitical factor has added momentum to the greenback's rise against major currencies. As of now, the USD has shown resilience, with the EUR/USD exchange rate reflecting a notable shift in market sentiment.

Despite the current firmness in the USD, slowing labor market trends are raising expectations that the Federal Reserve may adopt a more aggressive easing strategy by 2026. This potential shift could influence future currency pair dynamics, particularly as market participants weigh the implications for exchange rates in the coming months. As the USD maintains its upward trajectory, traders will closely monitor economic indicators for signs of the Fed's next moves.
