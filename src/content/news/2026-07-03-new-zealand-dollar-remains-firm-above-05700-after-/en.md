NZD/USD currency pair extends its gains during Asian trading hours on Friday, holding firm above the 0.5700 mark and hovering near 0.5705. The exchange rate shows renewed strength in the New Zealand Dollar as trading sentiment improves. With the USD trading activity subdued ahead of the US Independence Day holiday, price action remains relatively stable but biased slightly higher.

Chinese services PMI data provided support to the New Zealand Dollar, reinforcing expectations of steady regional demand and helping the NZD/USD currency pair maintain its position above the 0.5700 exchange rate level. Broader forex trading conditions remain thin, with the US dollar sidelined due to the holiday, limiting directional momentum across major currency pairs.

Looking ahead, traders expect the NZD/USD exchange rate to remain anchored near the 0.5700 support level, with limited volatility as USD liquidity stays reduced. Market participants will closely monitor upcoming economic data releases for fresh cues on both the US dollar and New Zealand Dollar outlook, particularly in relation to global risk sentiment.
