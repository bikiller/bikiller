USD/CAD is expected to see modest downside heading into the year-end, as the Federal Reserve is likely to resume rate cuts in the third quarter. This scenario points to potential weakness for the US Dollar, which could impact the exchange rate. ING predicts that the pair may struggle to maintain upward momentum due to softer USD demand in the latter half of 2026.

On the other hand, Canada faces its own challenges. The Bank of Canada remains cautious amid concerns over the USMCA talks and job market uncertainties. These domestic factors could limit the Canadian Dollar’s ability to capitalize on USD weakness. The exchange rate for USD/CAD is likely to remain in a range-bound trading pattern unless there is a significant shift in either the Fed's policies or Canada's economic outlook.
