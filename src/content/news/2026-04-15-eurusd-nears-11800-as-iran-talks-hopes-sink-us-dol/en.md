The EUR/USD currency pair extends its rally for a seventh consecutive session, currently trading at 1.1790, up 0.30%. The US Dollar is under pressure, sliding to a six-week low as market optimism over potential US-Iran talks dampens the Greenback’s appeal. Despite a significant uptick in US inflation, domestic data seems to have taken a back seat in influencing the market sentiment.

Traders are closely watching the developments surrounding the US-Iran negotiations, which are adding to uncertainty in the broader forex market. As the EUR/USD exchange rate edges closer to the key 1.1800 level, the focus shifts toward geopolitical factors, while the impact of US economic data on the dollar remains subdued. This move highlights the continued vulnerability of the USD in the face of external developments.
