The EUR/USD currency pair continues to trade with a firm tone, holding above the 1.1800 level and hovering near 1.1805 in early Asian trading on Thursday. The exchange rate reflects sustained demand for the Euro (EUR) as broader market sentiment shifts toward risk appetite, encouraging traders to move away from the US Dollar (USD).

Support for the pair is largely driven by easing geopolitical tensions, particularly optimism surrounding a potential ceasefire between the United States and Iran. This development has improved overall market confidence, reducing safe-haven demand for the USD and allowing the EUR to edge higher. In this environment, forex trading activity shows a preference for higher-yielding currencies.

If the current risk-on mood persists, the EUR/USD could maintain its upward bias, with the 1.1800 level acting as a key psychological support zone. Traders will closely monitor geopolitical headlines and broader macro signals, as shifts in sentiment could quickly influence the pair’s direction and short-term trading dynamics.
