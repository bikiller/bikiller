The USD/CAD currency pair has gained momentum, edging closer to the 1.3700 mark during early European trading hours. This uptick is driven by ongoing demand for the US Dollar, which has seen broad strength across global markets. The exchange rate, hovering around 1.3695, reflects the dollar’s resilience amid global uncertainty, with investors seeking safe-haven assets.

Since reaching a high of nearly 1.3930 in January, USD/CAD has been on a downward trajectory, establishing a pattern of lower highs and lower lows. Despite this recent pullback, the pair remains sensitive to shifts in broader market sentiment. If the current trend persists, further testing of key levels could be expected, with 1.3700 acting as a critical short-term resistance point for traders.
