EUR/GBP is holding steady around 0.8670 during the Asian session on Monday, recovering from earlier losses. The pair opened with a gap down as the Euro (EUR) struggled under pressure from rising demand for safe-haven assets amid escalating tensions in the Middle East. Traders remain cautious, weighing geopolitical risks and their impact on global markets.

The uncertainty surrounding the region has fueled a flight to safety, boosting currencies like the USD while weighing on the Euro. As a result, the EUR/GBP exchange rate has found support near the 0.8650 mark. Traders are closely monitoring the situation, as further volatility could lead to fluctuations in the currency pair, especially as safe-haven demand intensifies.
