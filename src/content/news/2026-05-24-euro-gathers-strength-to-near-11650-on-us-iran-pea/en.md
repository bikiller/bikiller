The EUR/USD currency pair advanced toward the 1.1640 region during Monday’s early Asian trading session, with the Euro extending gains against the USD as improving prospects for a US-Iran peace agreement lifted overall market sentiment. The softer demand for the US Dollar reflected a broader shift toward risk-sensitive assets, allowing the EUR exchange rate to strengthen as traders reduced safe-haven positioning.

Market participants viewed the diplomatic progress between Washington and Tehran as a positive development for global stability, encouraging renewed appetite for higher-yielding currencies and risk-linked trading strategies. The Euro continued to gather momentum near the 1.1650 level after buyers maintained control through the previous session, while the USD remained under pressure amid easing geopolitical concerns and moderating defensive flows into the greenback.

From a technical perspective, the EUR/USD pair held firm above the 1.1600 psychological level, reinforcing bullish near-term sentiment in the forex market. Traders are now closely monitoring whether the currency pair can sustain a break toward the 1.1650 resistance zone, which could open the door for additional upside movement. Meanwhile, any renewed geopolitical tension or stronger US economic signals may support the USD and limit further EUR gains.
