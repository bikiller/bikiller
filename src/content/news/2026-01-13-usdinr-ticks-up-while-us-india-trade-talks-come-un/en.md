The Indian Rupee (INR) is experiencing a decline against the US Dollar (USD), with the USD/INR exchange rate hovering around 90.50. This movement comes as US-India trade talks are set to take center stage, potentially influencing market sentiment. Traders are closely monitoring these discussions for indications of future trade policies, which could impact currency performance.

As the trading session unfolds, market participants are assessing the implications of the trade negotiations on the INR. A successful outcome could bolster the Rupee, while any setbacks might lead to further depreciation. With the USD maintaining its strength, the dynamics of the USD/INR pair are likely to remain under scrutiny in the coming days, making it essential for traders to stay informed on developments from the talks.
