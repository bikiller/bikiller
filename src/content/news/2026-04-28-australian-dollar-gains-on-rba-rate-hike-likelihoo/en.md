The Australian Dollar (AUD) continues its bullish run, extending gains for the third consecutive day against the US Dollar (USD). AUD/USD is currently trading around 0.7190 during the Asian session on Monday, bolstered by rising expectations of an upcoming rate hike by the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA). The pair remains well-supported, as market sentiment anticipates tighter monetary policy from the central bank.

The shift in investor sentiment towards the AUD is fueled by speculation that the RBA will increase interest rates in its upcoming meetings. With global inflationary pressures and a stronger economic outlook in Australia, the likelihood of a rate hike has driven traders to favor the Australian Dollar. This bullish momentum has kept AUD/USD trading above key support levels, highlighting the currency's strength amid ongoing global market volatility.
