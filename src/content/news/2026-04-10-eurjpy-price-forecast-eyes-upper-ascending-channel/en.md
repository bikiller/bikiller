EUR/JPY has maintained its upward trajectory for a second consecutive day, trading near 186.10 during the Asian session on Friday. The pair continues to display a strong bullish bias, supported by its movement within an ascending channel on the daily chart. The exchange rate has been climbing steadily, with no immediate signs of reversal, highlighting persistent buying interest in EUR against JPY.

Traders are now eyeing the upper boundary of the ascending channel, located just below the 186.50 mark. A break above this level could signal further gains for EUR/JPY, potentially extending its bullish momentum. Market sentiment remains positive as the Euro benefits from favorable conditions, while the Japanese Yen struggles amid a weaker economic outlook.
