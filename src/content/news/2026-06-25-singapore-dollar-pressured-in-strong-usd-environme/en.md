The latest trading pattern highlights persistent demand for USD across major currency markets, limiting support for regional currencies despite relatively stable conditions elsewhere.

Technical indicators also suggest that bullish momentum remains intact for the USD/SGD currency pair. According to the OCBC strategists, daily momentum continues to point higher, reinforcing expectations that the exchange rate could stay supported in the near term. At the same time, the Relative Strength Index (RSI) has moved into overbought territory, indicating that while the prevailing trading trend favors the USD, the pace of recent gains may be becoming increasingly stretched.

Looking ahead, traders are likely to monitor whether sustained USD strength and subdued risk appetite continue to drive the currency pair higher.
