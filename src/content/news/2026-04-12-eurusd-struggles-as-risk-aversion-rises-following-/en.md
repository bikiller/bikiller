The EUR/USD currency pair is facing downward pressure, with the exchange rate weakening amid growing risk aversion in global markets. Following the collapse of US-Iran peace talks, investor sentiment turned cautious, pushing the euro lower. At the time of writing, EUR/USD is trading near 1.1670, having opened Monday’s session with a noticeable gap down.

This risk-off environment has led to a shift in market sentiment, weighing on the euro as traders seek safer assets. The failure of diplomatic talks between the US and Iran has heightened concerns over geopolitical stability, further intensifying selling pressure on EUR/USD. As the pair struggles to regain footing, attention will be on potential developments that could shift the balance between the USD and EUR in the coming sessions.
