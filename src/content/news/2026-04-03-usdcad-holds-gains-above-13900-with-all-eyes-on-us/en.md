The USD/CAD currency pair continues to hold steady above the 1.3900 level as the US Dollar remains in control against its Canadian counterpart. At the time of writing, the exchange rate stands near 1.3925, approaching the year-to-date high of 1.3966. Traders are eyeing this level closely, as it marks a key resistance point for the pair.

The market’s focus now shifts to upcoming US jobs data, which could fuel further movement in the USD/CAD exchange rate. Positive figures could reinforce the dollar’s strength, potentially pushing the pair higher. Conversely, any unexpected downturns in the labor market may lead to a reversal, with the Canadian Dollar possibly gaining ground. The 1.3966 high is a critical level to watch in the near term for signs of momentum.
