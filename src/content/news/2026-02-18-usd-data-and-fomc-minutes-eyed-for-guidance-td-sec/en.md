The US dollar has seen notable movement recently, as the yield curve flattened after a strong rally in interest rates. Investors took profits, prompting a pullback, while Federal Reserve officials maintained a cautious stance on inflation and future policy actions. The FOMC's wait-and-see approach has left traders uncertain, with a focus on upcoming data for clearer guidance.

The EUR/USD exchange rate remains in a tight range, reflecting this uncertainty. Analysts are closely monitoring both economic data and the release of the FOMC minutes, which may offer insight into the Fed's next steps. The dollar's near-term direction hinges on how markets interpret these developments, with traders adjusting positions based on fresh signals.
