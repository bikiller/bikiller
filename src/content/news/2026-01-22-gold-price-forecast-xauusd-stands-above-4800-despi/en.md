The price movement reflects a resilient appetite for gold amid shifting global risk sentiment. While the USD remains a key player in market dynamics, investor caution continues to drive steady support for gold. As trading activity persists, XAU/USD's stability above critical levels reinforces its status as a favored hedge against volatility.
