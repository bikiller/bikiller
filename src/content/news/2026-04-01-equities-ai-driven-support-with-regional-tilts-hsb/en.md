HSBC's global CIO team maintains an Overweight outlook on global equities for the next six months, driven by AI-driven opportunities and strong earnings resilience. They see potential in the U.S., Japan, and Asia ex-Japan, while positioning themselves Underweight on Europe ex-UK and Neutral on Emerging Markets. This tilt towards AI and regional strength is likely to impact currency pairs like USD/EUR and USD/JPY in the coming months.

As global equities see support from AI and robust earnings, the outlook for the USD remains positive, especially against the EUR, which could experience pressure given HSBC's cautious stance on European markets. Similarly, the USD/JPY could benefit from stronger performance in Japan, while emerging market currencies might face a more neutral trading environment. The shift in regional preferences reflects broader market sentiment and could lead to more volatile exchange rate movements.
