The USD/JPY exchange rate dropped to around 159.60 during the European session on Tuesday, marking a slight decline for the currency pair. This movement comes as markets react to improved hopes for peace in the Middle East, which has eased global risk sentiment. The yen strengthened as a result of the reduced demand for safe-haven assets like the US dollar.

The trading action reflected broader shifts in market sentiment, as investors adjusted their positions amid positive geopolitical developments. Despite the dip in the USD/JPY, the currency pair remains relatively high compared to its recent range. Traders will be closely watching for further updates on geopolitical tensions and any potential impacts on broader risk appetite in the coming days.
