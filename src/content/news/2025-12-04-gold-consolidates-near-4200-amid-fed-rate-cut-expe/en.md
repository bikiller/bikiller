Gold (XAU/USD) is consolidating around $4,200, trading within the $4,160 to $4,260 range as traders adopt a cautious stance ahead of the Federal Reserve's upcoming monetary policy meeting. The market remains apprehensive amid expectations of potential rate cuts, influencing investor sentiment and positioning in the precious metal.

As the Fed prepares to unveil its decisions, market participants are closely monitoring the implications for the USD and overall exchange rates. The current stability in gold prices reflects a broader pause in trading activity, with many looking to gauge how future monetary policy shifts might impact the forex market and the dynamics between the USD and EUR.
