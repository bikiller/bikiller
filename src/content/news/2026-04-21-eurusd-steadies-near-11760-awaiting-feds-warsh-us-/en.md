The Euro (EUR) is showing modest losses against the US Dollar (USD), with the EUR/USD exchange rate stabilizing around 1.1765 ahead of the US trading session on Tuesday. The currency pair faced resistance at the 1.1790 level on Monday, indicating market hesitation as traders await upcoming developments.

Market participants are closely monitoring comments from Federal Reserve official Kevin Warsh, which could influence USD strength. Additionally, ongoing US-Iran peace talks are adding another layer of complexity, potentially impacting investor sentiment. As the forex market anticipates these key events, the euro remains under pressure, highlighting the delicate balance between geopolitical factors and monetary policy influences.
