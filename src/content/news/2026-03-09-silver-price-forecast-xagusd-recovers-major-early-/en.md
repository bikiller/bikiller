Silver prices (XAG/USD) have recovered a significant portion of early losses, moving back toward their opening level of $84.00 during Monday's European trading session. The currency pair found support after dipping lower earlier in the day, as traders remained cautious amid fluctuating market sentiment. Despite the recovery, uncertainty remains regarding the direction of silver prices, as investors await more economic data for clearer guidance.

The price movement of silver has been closely tied to broader market factors, with the exchange rate of the USD influencing much of the volatility. The XAG/USD pair faces continued pressure from fluctuations in risk sentiment, as traders assess the outlook for global economic conditions and monetary policy. With no clear breakout in sight, silver's outlook remains uncertain, with key technical levels near $84.00 acting as a potential pivot point for further moves.
