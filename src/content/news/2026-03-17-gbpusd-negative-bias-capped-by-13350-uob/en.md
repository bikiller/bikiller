GBP/USD has experienced a sharp rebound from 1.3226 to 1.3340, but according to UOB's Senior Technical Strategist Quek Ser Leang, this move appears to be driven by short-covering rather than a shift in trend. The pair's recovery has been capped by the 1.3350 resistance level, limiting further upside momentum in the near term.

Looking ahead, GBP/USD is expected to trade within a range of 1.3250 to 1.3350. This range-bound movement suggests that, despite the rebound, a significant breakout remains unlikely without a more substantial change in market dynamics. Traders will likely remain cautious, monitoring for any developments that could push the exchange rate outside of this range.
