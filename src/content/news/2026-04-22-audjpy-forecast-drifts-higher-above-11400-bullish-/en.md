The AUD/JPY currency pair has gained traction, drifting higher to approximately 114.05 during the early European session on Wednesday. This upward movement reflects a bullish trend, fueled by a hawkish stance from the Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA). Traders are responding positively to the RBA's signals, which suggest a potential tightening of monetary policy.

As the Australian Dollar continues to strengthen against the Japanese Yen, the price remains comfortably above the 100-day EMA, indicating sustained bullish momentum. Market participants are closely monitoring this exchange rate, as further advances could lead to increased trading activity and potential resistance levels. The interplay between the RBA's direction and market sentiment may continue to influence the AUD/JPY dynamics in the near term.
