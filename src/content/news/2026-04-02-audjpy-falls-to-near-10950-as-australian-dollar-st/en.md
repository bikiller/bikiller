The AUD/JPY currency pair is experiencing a pullback, retreating from its recent gains and trading at around 109.60 during the Asian session on Thursday. This decline follows two consecutive days of upward movement, with the pair approaching 109.50 earlier in the day. The Australian Dollar has come under pressure, driven by growing concerns over geopolitical risks, which are weighing on investor sentiment and limiting bullish momentum.

Geopolitical uncertainties have exacerbated the Australian Dollar's struggles, contributing to a cautious mood in the forex market. As the AUD/JPY exchange rate fluctuates near key support levels, traders are closely monitoring the broader market environment for any signs of potential volatility. This technical level at 109.50 could be a critical point, with any further downward movement potentially opening the door for additional losses in the short term.
