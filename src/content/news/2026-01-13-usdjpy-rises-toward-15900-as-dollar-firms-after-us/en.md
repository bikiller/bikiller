The USD/JPY currency pair climbed further toward the 159.00 mark on Tuesday, reflecting the US Dollar's strengthening following the release of the latest inflation data. The US inflation report sparked renewed confidence in the dollar, pushing the exchange rate near levels not seen since July 2024.

As traders reacted to the inflation figures, the Japanese Yen (JPY) continued to weaken against the Greenback. This trend highlights market sentiment favoring the dollar in light of potential economic implications, emphasizing the ongoing volatility in forex trading as investors assess future monetary policy moves from the Federal Reserve.
