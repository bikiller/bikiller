Gold (XAU/USD) remains mostly unchanged on Thursday, recovering from earlier losses as traders assess geopolitical tensions and the Federal Reserve’s outlook. The precious metal's price hovers around $5,170, a slight rebound from intraday lows near $5,125. The US Dollar (USD) has paused its earlier advance, contributing to the stability in gold prices as Treasury yields also ease after rising earlier this week.

Market participants are keeping a close eye on developments surrounding the Fed's monetary policy and global economic uncertainties. The slight pullback in Treasury yields and USD momentum has created a balanced environment for gold, which continues to trade sideways. This consolidation could signal a wait-and-see approach as traders gauge further economic signals and potential shifts in the currency pair dynamics.
