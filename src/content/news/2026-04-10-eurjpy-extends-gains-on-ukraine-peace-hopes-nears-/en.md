The Euro (EUR) has gained ground against its major counterparts on Friday, largely driven by reports suggesting that Russia and Ukraine are nearing a peace agreement. This optimism has supported EUR/JPY, pushing it from mid-March lows of 182.00 to a recent high of 186.50. The currency pair now approaches its year-to-date high of 186.88, highlighting strong bullish momentum.

This upward move in EUR/JPY reflects broader market sentiment, where improved geopolitical news is fueling risk appetite. Traders are closely watching for further developments between Russia and Ukraine, with a breakthrough potentially boosting the Euro even more. Technical levels show 186.88 as the next key resistance, marking a critical point for the currency pair’s continued rally.
