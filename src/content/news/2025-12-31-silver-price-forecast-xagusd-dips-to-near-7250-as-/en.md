Silver prices (XAG/USD) have seen a notable decline, dropping to approximately $72.50 during the Asian trading session on Wednesday. As the market absorbs this news, fluctuations in the exchange rate could continue, impacting trading strategies for both retail and institutional investors. With silver now hovering around this key technical level, market participants will be closely monitoring developments that could drive further price action in the currency pair.
