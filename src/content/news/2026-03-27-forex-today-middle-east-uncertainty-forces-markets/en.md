The foreign exchange market remains cautious today as ongoing geopolitical instability in the Middle East continues to affect trading sentiment. Tensions have led to volatility across major currency pairs, with the EUR/USD hovering near 1.0700, showing a modest decline of 0.2% on the day. The USD has strengthened against other currencies, partly driven by safe-haven demand amidst concerns over the region's uncertainties.

In addition, the GBP/USD is testing key support levels at 1.2300, while the USD/JPY remains resilient above 131.50, reflecting a continued preference for the dollar. Traders are closely monitoring the situation for any developments that could drive further fluctuations in the exchange rate. The cautious approach is likely to persist as markets await more clarity on geopolitical risks.
