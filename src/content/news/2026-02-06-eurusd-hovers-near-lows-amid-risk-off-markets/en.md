The EUR/USD currency pair saw modest gains on Friday, hovering around the 1.1800 mark after dipping to a two-week low of 1.1765 earlier in the day. Despite the uptick, the Euro remains under pressure, reflecting broader market sentiment as risk-off conditions weigh on global trading. 

Market participants are keeping a close eye on the USD, which continues to benefit from safe-haven demand amid uncertainty. The Euro's struggle to maintain strength against the Dollar highlights the ongoing concerns surrounding the economic outlook and potential shifts in investor risk appetite. As the trading session progresses, the EUR/USD exchange rate remains sensitive to broader market sentiment and potential shifts in U.S. economic data.
