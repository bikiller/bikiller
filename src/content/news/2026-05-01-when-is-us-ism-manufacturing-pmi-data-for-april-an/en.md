The US ISM Manufacturing PMI for April is set for release today at 14:00 GMT, drawing keen attention from forex traders as it often signals the health of the US economy and influences the USD. Analysts expect the data to impact market sentiment, potentially affecting risk appetite and driving movements in key currency pairs like EUR/USD. A stronger-than-expected reading could boost the USD, putting pressure on the euro.  

EUR/USD trading has been hovering near recent support around 1.0900, with resistance levels eyed at 1.1000. If the PMI surprises on the upside, the exchange rate could test lower levels as traders rotate into USD assets. Conversely, a softer PMI could ease USD demand, providing relief for the euro and prompting short-term rebounds. Market participants will closely watch the correlation between manufacturing momentum and broader economic indicators.  

Technical traders may position around key Fibonacci retracements and moving averages, anticipating volatility spikes following the release. Intraday trading strategies are likely to focus on quick reactions to PMI surprises, while longer-term investors weigh the implications for US growth and monetary policy. With EUR/USD already navigating tight ranges, the data could be the catalyst for directional moves across forex markets.
