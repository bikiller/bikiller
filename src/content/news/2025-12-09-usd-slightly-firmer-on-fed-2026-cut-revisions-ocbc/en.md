As traders analyze the implications of the Fed's revised outlook, the exchange rate dynamics are expected to shift. With the USD gaining traction, currency pairs such as EUR/USD may see increased volatility. Investors are keenly watching for upcoming economic indicators that could either reinforce the Dollar's position or prompt new adjustments in exchange rates.
