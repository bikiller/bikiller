EUR/JPY continues to show bullish momentum, trading near 182.95 in early European hours on Friday. The currency pair remains in positive territory, buoyed by a persistent uptrend above the 100-day Exponential Moving Average (EMA). This technical indicator suggests further gains could take EUR/JPY closer to the key 183.00 level in the near term.

The Japanese Yen faces mounting pressure due to uncertainty surrounding the Bank of Japan’s interest rate policy. Market participants are closely watching BoJ signals, with the Yen vulnerable to further weakness against the Euro as traders speculate on potential shifts in Japan's monetary stance. This backdrop is likely to support further Euro strength in the near term.
