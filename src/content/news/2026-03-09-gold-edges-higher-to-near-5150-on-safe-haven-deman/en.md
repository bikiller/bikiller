Gold prices (XAU/USD) have edged higher, trading near $5,140 during the early Asian session on Tuesday. The precious metal continues to gain support from persistent geopolitical risks in the Middle East, which are driving demand for safe-haven assets. Despite recent selling pressures, the current trading level remains resilient as investors seek stability amid market uncertainty.

The ongoing tensions in the region are contributing to the strength of gold, with traders eyeing further volatility in global markets. The price of gold is holding steady near key support levels, with $5,150 acting as a near-term resistance point. As safe-haven demand rises, the USD's movement and broader economic data will likely play a pivotal role in determining the next direction for XAU/USD.
