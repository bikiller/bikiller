Gold prices surged to a record high of $4,555 per ounce during the early Asian session on Monday, driven by heightened geopolitical risks and growing expectations for a U.S. interest rate cut. The XAU/USD currency pair is gaining momentum as investors flock to safe-haven assets amid rising global uncertainties, pushing the exchange rate to unprecedented levels.

The demand for gold reflects a broader trend in the market as traders assess the implications of potential monetary policy shifts by the Federal Reserve. With the USD under pressure and competing currencies like the EUR also affected, the bullish sentiment in the gold market may continue as uncertainties about inflation and economic stability persist.
