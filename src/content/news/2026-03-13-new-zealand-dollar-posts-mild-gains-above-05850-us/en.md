The New Zealand Dollar (NZD) is holding modest gains against the US Dollar (USD), with the NZD/USD pair trading around 0.5855 during the early Asian session on Friday. Despite the uptick, concerns over ongoing geopolitical tensions in the Middle East are limiting the Kiwi’s ability to extend its rally. Traders are wary of the potential risks these conflicts pose to broader market stability.

As market participants await the release of key US PCE inflation data later in the day, the US Dollar's strength remains a key factor in shaping the trading outlook. Should the data point to persistent inflationary pressures, it could reinforce expectations of further tightening from the Federal Reserve, which may weigh on the NZD/USD pair and curb its current upward momentum.
