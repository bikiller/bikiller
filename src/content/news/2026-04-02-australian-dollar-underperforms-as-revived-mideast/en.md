The Australian Dollar (AUD) is underperforming in the forex market, trading 0.75% lower against the US Dollar (USD) during Thursday's European session. The AUD/USD exchange rate has dropped to around 0.6875 as heightened geopolitical risks in the Middle East have weighed on risk-sensitive assets. Traders have been shifting toward safer assets, pushing the Aussie lower.

The revived concerns about instability in the Middle East have caused a sell-off in higher-yielding currencies like the Australian Dollar. The AUD's decline is also fueled by broader market uncertainty, with investors seeking the relative safety of the USD. As global risk sentiment remains fragile, the Australian Dollar faces significant pressure, leaving it vulnerable to further declines in the near term.
