Market sentiment remains cautious, influenced by former President Trump's recent tariff threats, which are helping to limit any further losses for the pair. As traders monitor geopolitical developments and economic indicators, the exchange rate reflects the intricate dynamics of U.S. and Canadian economic performance amid ongoing uncertainties.
