Gold (XAU/USD) continued its upward momentum on Wednesday, extending gains from the previous day as market sentiment improved amid growing optimism that the US-Israel conflict with Iran could soon be resolved. At the time of writing, XAU/USD is trading around $4,748, marking its highest level in nearly two weeks. This surge reflects a shift in risk sentiment, with traders anticipating a potential de-escalation in geopolitical tensions.

The strengthening of gold prices comes as investors flock to safe-haven assets, driven by uncertainty in the global market. As the US dollar (USD) faces pressure, gold's appeal increases, particularly for those seeking stability. The XAU/USD exchange rate remains near key resistance levels, suggesting potential for further gains if the optimism continues to influence market sentiment. Traders will closely monitor any developments in the Middle East, as these could heavily influence the direction of gold in the short term.
