The USD/CAD currency pair gained 0.33% on Tuesday, trading around the 1.3670 mark. The pair had a steady climb from Monday's intraday low near 1.3600, reflecting cautious optimism in the market as traders prepare for key central bank decisions. The movement in the exchange rate comes ahead of the Federal Reserve and Bank of Canada meetings, adding a layer of anticipation to the current trading environment.

With the USD holding its ground against the Canadian dollar, market participants are closely watching for any signs of divergence in the central banks' policies. The Fed's stance on interest rates and economic growth, alongside the Bank of Canada's actions, could significantly influence the exchange rate. Investors are keen to assess the implications of these meetings, with the potential for volatility in the USD/CAD pair based on the outcome of the policy announcements.
