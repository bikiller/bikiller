USD/JPY surged by 0.48% on Tuesday, with the exchange rate hovering at 157.91. This follows last Thursday’s intervention by Japanese authorities in the forex market, which led to a sharp 2.50% drop, or roughly 400 pips, as they bought Yen to curb its depreciation. The intervention temporarily stabilized the market, but the impact is now fading.

As the market regains stability, buyers are now eyeing the 160.00 level. The Greenback remained firm during the session, while the Yen's recent weakness continues to influence the currency pair's movement. With Japan’s intervention fading, the USD/JPY is poised for further upward momentum, potentially testing new highs if buyers maintain control.
