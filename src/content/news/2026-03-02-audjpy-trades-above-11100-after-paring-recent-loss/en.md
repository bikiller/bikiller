AUD/JPY has recovered from recent losses, trading just above the 111.00 mark during the Asian session on Monday. The Australian Dollar has managed to regain some ground, pushing the exchange rate higher despite a backdrop of rising safe-haven demand. This rebound comes as global markets remain jittery due to escalating tensions in the Middle East, influencing investor sentiment.

The currency pair's move back above 111.00 reflects a notable shift in momentum, as the Australian Dollar gains strength. Despite broader risk aversion, which typically benefits safe-haven currencies like the USD and JPY, the AUD shows resilience. Market participants will closely monitor how these geopolitical developments unfold and whether the AUD/JPY can maintain its upward trajectory or face renewed pressure in the coming sessions.
