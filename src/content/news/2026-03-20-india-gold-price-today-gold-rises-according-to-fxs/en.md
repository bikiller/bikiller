Gold prices in India experienced an uptick on Friday, reflecting a broader trend in the global market. As per FXStreet data, the yellow metal saw a modest rise, driven by fluctuations in the USD/INR exchange rate and broader market sentiment. The price movement mirrored the increased demand for safe-haven assets, with investors seeking stability amid ongoing geopolitical tensions and market uncertainty.

The rise in gold prices comes as the USD continues to hold strong, keeping gold prices buoyant in local currency terms. This upward trend in gold is also supported by recent fluctuations in the EUR/USD, with the Euro weakening against the Dollar. Traders are keeping an eye on key technical levels in gold trading, watching closely for potential resistance and support zones, which could signal the next phase of price action.
