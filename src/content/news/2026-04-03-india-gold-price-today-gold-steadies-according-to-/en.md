Gold prices in India held steady on Friday, with little movement in the market, according to FXStreet data. The USD's performance against major currency pairs like the EUR also contributed to the muted market response, offering little direction for gold traders.

Despite the flat movement in gold prices, market analysts suggest that the US dollar's recent fluctuations, along with global economic uncertainties, are maintaining gold's safe-haven appeal.
