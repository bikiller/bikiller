Silver (XAG/USD) has fallen by 2.12% on Wednesday, currently trading around $85.30. The precious metal faces downward pressure as the US Dollar (USD) rebounds, bolstered by rising US Treasury yields. This shift in market dynamics reduces demand for non-yielding assets like silver, weighing on its recent bullish momentum.

Geopolitical risks continue to add volatility, but the strengthening USD remains the primary factor influencing silver's price action. With the exchange rate affected by rising US yields, silver's potential for further gains appears limited in the near term. Traders are closely monitoring how these macroeconomic factors will impact the XAG/USD pair in the coming sessions.
