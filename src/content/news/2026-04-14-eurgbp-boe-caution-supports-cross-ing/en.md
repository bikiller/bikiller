The EUR/GBP currency pair saw steady trading as the market focused on comments from Bank of England officials. Governor Andrew Bailey, along with hawkish members Catherine Mann and Megan Greene, were set to speak, with Greene recently adopting a more balanced tone. Despite the cautious stance from the BoE, the exchange rate held firm, reflecting the market's mixed expectations on future UK monetary policy.

The focus now shifts to how the BoE's rhetoric may influence the EUR/GBP pair in the short term. Bailey’s cautious outlook, paired with a more neutral tone from Greene, suggests that traders may need to reassess their views on UK rate hikes. As the exchange rate hovers around key technical levels, the market is weighing the balance between inflation concerns and economic growth as the central bank's next move remains uncertain.
