Repeated failures near the multi-month resistance around 0.8750 have capped upside attempts, reinforcing the technical ceiling that has limited bullish momentum in recent sessions. Each approach toward 0.8750 has drawn selling interest, keeping the EUR/GBP exchange rate anchored below this barrier and highlighting the importance of this level for near-term direction in the currency pair.
