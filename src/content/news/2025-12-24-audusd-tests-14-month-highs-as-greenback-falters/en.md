The AUD/USD currency pair soared to test 14-month highs on Tuesday, rising by two-thirds of a percent, while building on the previous day's 0.68% gain. The US Dollar (USD) experienced widespread weakness, prompting traders to favor the Australian Dollar (AUD) as the exchange rate continues to climb.

This sustained upward movement in AUD/USD reflects growing market confidence in the Australian economy, alongside concerns surrounding the USD. As the Greenback falters against major currencies, including the EUR, traders are closely monitoring developments that could further impact the exchange rates and fuel additional volatility in the forex market.
