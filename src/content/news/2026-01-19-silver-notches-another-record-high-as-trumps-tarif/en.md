This surge reflects a broader trend as traders seek stability in volatile conditions.

As the exchange rate for silver continues to climb, the implications for the forex market are significant. The elevated demand for XAG/USD may further impact other currency pairs, particularly as traders weigh the potential fallout from escalating tariffs on the broader economy.
