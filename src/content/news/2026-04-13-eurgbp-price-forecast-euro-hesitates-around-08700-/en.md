The EUR/GBP exchange rate has been trading in a narrow range, hovering between 0.8695 and 0.8725. As market sentiment remains cautious, the euro is struggling to break away from this tight price band, with traders awaiting clearer direction. The pair's price action reflects ongoing uncertainty in broader market conditions, as the euro's strength against the pound is limited.

Factors such as geopolitical developments and mixed economic data have contributed to the stagnation in EUR/GBP. With traders adopting a wait-and-see approach, the currency pair shows little movement outside the established range. As the markets eye potential shifts in risk appetite, a breakout above 0.8725 could signal further upside, while a dip below 0.8695 may expose the euro to greater downside risk against the British pound.
