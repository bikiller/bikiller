The USD/CAD currency pair saw some selling pressure after a brief uptick to the 1.3680 area during the Asian session on Monday. However, the downward momentum was limited, as the pair lacked follow-through selling, holding steady near the 1.3660 mark. The exchange rate remains vulnerable to further downside, as it faces resistance near the 200-hour Exponential Moving Average (EMA), which is proving crucial for bullish momentum.

If the pair fails to hold above the 1.3660 support level, it could face a deeper pullback. Traders will closely monitor the 200-hour EMA, as a break above this level could shift the momentum in favor of the bulls. The price action suggests cautious trading, with potential for increased volatility depending on the 200-hour EMA's performance in the coming sessions.
