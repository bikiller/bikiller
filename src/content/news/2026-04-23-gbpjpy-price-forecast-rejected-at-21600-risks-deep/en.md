The GBP/JPY currency pair faced downward pressure, retreating from a peak around 215.70 to test the critical support level at 215.00. This shift comes as risk appetite wanes among traders, particularly due to escalating tensions in the Middle East, creating uncertainty about the conflict's direction and its potential impact on global markets.

Currently, GBP/JPY is trading at 215.06, indicating a cautious sentiment among investors. The rejection at the 216.00 mark raises concerns about the stability of this exchange rate, as traders assess the overall market environment. Should the pair break below the 215.00 level, further declines may be anticipated, reflecting broader market volatility influenced by geopolitical developments.
