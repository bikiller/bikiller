The US Dollar Index (DXY) is nearing the 99.00 mark during early European trading on Thursday, continuing its upward trajectory. The index, which measures the USD against a basket of six major currencies, has gained momentum amid escalating geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. Investors are flocking to the safe-haven USD as uncertainty mounts.

The latest rally in the DXY is also supported by robust US services data, which has provided a boost to the outlook for the world's largest economy. This combination of geopolitical risks and strong economic indicators is pushing USD/JPY and EUR/USD exchange rates lower. Traders are closely monitoring the DXY's technical resistance at 99.00, with further gains potentially signaling more upside for the greenback.
