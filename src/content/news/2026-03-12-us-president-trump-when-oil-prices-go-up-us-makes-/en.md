In a post on Truth Social, US President Donald Trump emphasized that rising oil prices, which have surged since tensions escalated with Iran, are benefiting the US economy. Higher oil prices tend to increase revenue for the US, particularly due to the strength of the USD. The US dollar, backed by its energy exports, gains traction as global demand for oil rises.

Trump also reiterated his primary concern: preventing Iran from acquiring nuclear weapons. This stance continues to influence market sentiment, impacting the EUR/USD and USD/JPY exchange rates. While oil prices may boost the USD in the short term, geopolitical risks surrounding Iran’s nuclear ambitions add volatility to the forex market, keeping traders alert to potential fluctuations in the USD's value against major currencies.
