Silver prices continued their decline on Tuesday, with XAG/USD trading at $72.24, down 0.69%. The metal struggled to break through the $75.00 resistance level, as momentum shifted in favor of sellers. The failure to hold above this key level signals further downside potential, with the next target seen at $70. 

Adding to the bearish sentiment, a crossover between the 20-day and 100-day simple moving averages (SMAs) was confirmed. The 20-day SMA moving below the 100-day SMA suggests that bearish momentum is gaining strength. If this trend persists, silver may face additional pressure, with traders eyeing the $70 support level as a potential entry point for further short positions.
