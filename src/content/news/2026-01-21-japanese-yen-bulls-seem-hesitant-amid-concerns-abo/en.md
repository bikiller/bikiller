The Japanese Yen (JPY) is showing strength against the US Dollar (USD) during the Asian trading session on Wednesday, maintaining a slight lead in the currency pair. However, uncertainty looms as bulls exhibit hesitation, driven by concerns regarding Japan's fiscal health ahead of the upcoming Bank of Japan (BoJ) meeting. 

As traders assess mixed economic signals, the exchange rate of JPY/USD remains sensitive to developments around Japan's monetary policy. Market participants are closely monitoring technical levels, with a focus on how fiscal stability may influence BoJ's decisions and, consequently, the yen's trajectory in the near term.
