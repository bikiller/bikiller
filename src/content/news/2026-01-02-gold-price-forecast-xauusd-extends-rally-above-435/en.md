Gold prices (XAU/USD) surged to nearly $4,375 during early European trading on Friday, continuing their upward trajectory. With the US dollar under pressure, the bullish sentiment surrounding gold may persist, potentially leading to significant trading opportunities in the coming sessions. Market participants will be closely monitoring Fed announcements and broader economic indicators that could influence the currency pair's movement.
