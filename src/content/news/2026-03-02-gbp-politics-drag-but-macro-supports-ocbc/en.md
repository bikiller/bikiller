Political instability in the UK continues to put pressure on the British Pound, with Labour’s recent by-election defeat and a looming leadership challenge casting uncertainty. As a result, GBP/USD is facing downward momentum, holding around key support levels. The market sentiment remains cautious, and traders are closely monitoring any developments that could further weigh on the Pound.

Despite the political risks, UK macroeconomic fundamentals offer some support. Economic data, while mixed, suggests resilience in sectors like manufacturing and services. This provides a buffer for GBP/EUR, which remains confined within a range as traders balance risks against the broader economic backdrop. The Pound's outlook remains tied to both domestic political stability and global market trends.
