The European Central Bank (ECB) opted to maintain its key interest rates at their current levels during its April policy meeting, in line with market expectations. The decision comes as the ECB continues to assess the economic outlook and inflation dynamics in the Eurozone. As a result, the EUR/USD exchange rate saw limited movement, staying near key support levels around 1.0850.

The market response to the ECB's announcement was largely muted, with investors maintaining a cautious stance ahead of upcoming economic data. EUR/USD held steady as traders awaited further cues on the central bank's future policy stance. Despite the ECB's cautious approach, the euro remains vulnerable to global economic uncertainties, with the currency pair likely to remain within a range unless significant developments occur. The central bank's next steps will be closely scrutinized as inflation trends and economic growth play a key role in shaping future policy decisions.
