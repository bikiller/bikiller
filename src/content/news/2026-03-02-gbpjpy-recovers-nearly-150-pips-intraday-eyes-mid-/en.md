The GBP/JPY currency pair has experienced a sharp recovery of nearly 150 pips intraday, bouncing back from the Asian session low near 209.00. This surge has taken the exchange rate to a fresh daily high, filling the bearish gap that had formed earlier this week. The movement highlights the current weakness in the Japanese yen, which continues to struggle against its counterparts, including the British pound.

With the pair now eyeing the mid-210.00s, market sentiment suggests a potential shift in the yen's outlook. Weaker demand for the JPY, coupled with a stable performance in the GBP, has led to the upward momentum. Traders will be watching closely for any signs of further recovery or a reversal, with key levels like 210.50 and 211.00 coming into focus as potential resistance. The ongoing dynamics between the GBP and JPY will play a crucial role in determining the pair’s direction in the coming sessions.
