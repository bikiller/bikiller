The Japanese Yen (JPY) has experienced a notable intraday pullback, retreating from a high against the US Dollar (USD) reached earlier on Monday. This movement comes despite an overall supportive fundamental backdrop, leading the currency pair to fill the weekly bullish gap during the first half of the European session.

As traders reacted to changing market dynamics, the JPY's exchange rate against the USD has shown fluctuations, reflecting the broader weakness of the dollar. The trading session revealed critical technical levels as the JPY adjusts to these developments, highlighting the ongoing volatility in forex markets as investors weigh economic indicators and geopolitical factors.
