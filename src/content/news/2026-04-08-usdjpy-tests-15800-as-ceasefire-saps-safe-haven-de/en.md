The USD/JPY currency pair experienced a decline of approximately 0.66% on Wednesday, retreating from an intraday high near 160.00. As trading settled, the exchange rate hovered around 158.50, highlighting a notable shift in market sentiment.

The pullback in USD/JPY can be attributed to a ceasefire that has diminished safe-haven demand for the Japanese yen. With geopolitical tensions easing, investors are pivoting away from traditionally safe currencies, impacting their respective exchange rates. This shift may lead to increased volatility in the forex market as traders reassess the balance between risk and safety moving forward.
