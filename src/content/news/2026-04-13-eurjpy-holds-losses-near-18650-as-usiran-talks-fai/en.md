EUR/JPY remains under pressure, holding losses near the 186.50 level after early trading in Asia on Monday. The currency pair traded around 186.60 as the Euro (EUR) struggled due to the breakdown of US-Iran peace talks. The uncertainty surrounding the negotiations weighed heavily on the risk-sensitive Euro, causing a pullback in the exchange rate.

The failure of diplomatic discussions between the United States and Iran led to a loss of confidence in global markets, triggering a decline in EUR/JPY. As a result, the currency pair faces continued downside risks, with 186.50 acting as a key support level. Traders are likely to watch for further developments in geopolitical tensions and their impact on the Euro, which remains sensitive to risk-off sentiment.
