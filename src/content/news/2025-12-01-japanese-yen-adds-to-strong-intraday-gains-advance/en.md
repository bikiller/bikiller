The Japanese Yen (JPY) continued its strong intraday momentum, rising to a fresh two-week high against the US Dollar (USD) during the early European trading session on Monday. The exchange rate surged as the Yen capitalized on a broadly weaker USD, reflecting a shift in market sentiment that favored risk-off currencies amid global uncertainties.

As traders digested the latest economic data and geopolitical developments, the JPY's advance highlighted its safe-haven status. The USD's decline was further fueled by weaker-than-expected U.S. economic indicators, which contributed to a diminished outlook for the currency. This movement in the currency pair underscores the dynamic nature of the forex market, with the Yen gaining traction against the backdrop of fluctuating investor confidence.
