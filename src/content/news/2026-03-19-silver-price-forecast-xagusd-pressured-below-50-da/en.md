Silver (XAG/USD) is under pressure, trading below the 50-day SMA as it remains vulnerable to downside risks. On Thursday, the metal recovered slightly from daily lows, supported by a pullback in the US Dollar (USD) and Treasury yields. Despite this short-term bounce, silver’s bearish tone persists, with market participants reassessing central bank decisions and their potential impact on global rate expectations.

The currency pair's struggle is a reflection of broader market sentiment, as investors navigate shifting interest rate outlooks and mixed central bank signals. Although the USD weakness provides temporary relief, silver remains on the defensive, with downside risks still intact. Market participants will closely watch upcoming economic data and central bank moves, which could either reinforce or challenge the current price trend for XAG/USD in the near term.
